# `packages/modules/commerce/` - MBT Commerce Module

This document outlines the purpose, API, data model, and operational details of the MBT Commerce Module.

---

## 1. Purpose

The MBT Commerce Module is the foundational infrastructure for all transactional and recurring revenue activities across the MBT platform. It centralizes the logic for subscription billing (leveraging Stripe for recurring charges), one-off product purchases (e.g., merchandise, event tickets, services), and managing per-tenant pricing plans. Additionally, it integrates the Stripe Customer Portal for self-service account management. This module is designed to be consumed by any brand within the MBT ecosystem that engages in commerce, such as Big Muddy Records (recurring label services), Tuthill Design (recurring realtor packages), Big Muddy Radio (recurring sponsor packages), and Big Muddy Inn (direct bookings). It also serves to unify and replace ad-hoc Stripe integrations, including the existing DSD subscription tiers, ensuring a consistent and robust commerce experience. This module does not encompass general ledger accounting, comprehensive inventory management beyond basic product definitions, or complex fulfillment logistics; its scope is strictly billing, checkout, and plan management.

## 3. Data Model

The Commerce Module introduces and manages several Prisma models. All models related to commerce are multi-tenanted via a `tenantId` field, which links to an existing `DirectoryBusiness` record.

*   **`Plan` (New):** Represents a pricing plan offered by a `DirectoryBusiness` (tenant).
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id`)
    *   `stripePlanId`: `string` (Stripe Price ID)
    *   `name`: `string`
    *   `description`: `string?`
    *   `price`: `Int` (in cents)
    *   `currency`: `String` (e.g., 'usd')
    *   `interval`: `String` ('month', 'year')
    *   `intervalCount`: `Int`
    *   `trialPeriodDays`: `Int?`
    *   `active`: `Boolean` (defaults to true)
    *   `metadata`: `Json?`
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

*   **`Subscription` (New):** Represents an active or past subscription of a `Customer` to a `Plan`.
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id`)
    *   `customerId`: `string` (Foreign key to `Customer.id`)
    *   `planId`: `string` (Foreign key to `Plan.id`)
    *   `stripeSubscriptionId`: `string` (Stripe Subscription ID)
    *   `status`: `String` ('active', 'past_due', 'canceled', 'unpaid', etc.)
    *   `currentPeriodStart`: `DateTime`
    *   `currentPeriodEnd`: `DateTime`
    *   `cancelAtPeriodEnd`: `Boolean`
    *   `canceledAt`: `DateTime?`
    *   `platformFeePercent`: `Float` (See License Profile section)
    *   `metadata`: `Json?`
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

*   **`Product` (New):** Represents a one-off item available for purchase by a `DirectoryBusiness` (tenant).
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id`)
    *   `stripeProductId`: `string` (Stripe Product ID)
    *   `stripePriceId`: `string` (Stripe Price ID for default price)
    *   `name`: `string`
    *   `description`: `string?`
    *   `price`: `Int` (in cents)
    *   `currency`: `String` (e.g., 'usd')
    *   `type`: `String` ('service', 'good', 'ticket')
    *   `active`: `Boolean` (defaults to true)
    *   `metadata`: `Json?`
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

*   **`Order` (New):** Represents a completed purchase of one or more `Product`s.
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id`)
    *   `customerId`: `string` (Foreign key to `Customer.id`)
    *   `stripeCheckoutSessionId`: `string`
    *   `totalAmount`: `Int` (in cents)
    *   `currency`: `String`
    *   `status`: `String` ('pending', 'complete', 'failed', 'refunded')
    *   `lineItems`: `Json` (e.g., `[{ productId, quantity, unitPrice }]`)
    *   `platformFeeAmount`: `Int` (Calculated based on `ModuleEngagement`)
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

*   **`Customer` (New):** Represents a customer in the context of the Commerce module, linking to Stripe. This model serves as an intermediary between Stripe's customer object and our internal `User` or `DirectoryBusiness` if applicable.
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id` – for customers of a specific tenant)
    *   `stripeCustomerId`: `string` (Stripe Customer ID)
    *   `email`: `string?`
    *   `name`: `string?`
    *   `directoryBusinessId`: `string?` (Optional: If the customer *is* an MBT `DirectoryBusiness`)
    *   `userId`: `string?` (Optional: If the customer *is* an MBT `User`)
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

*   **`ModuleEngagement` (New):** Tracks the specific commercial agreements for tenants using MBT modules.
    *   `id`: `string` (UUID)
    *   `tenantId`: `string` (Foreign key to `DirectoryBusiness.id`)
    *   `moduleId`: `string` (e.g., 'commerce')
    *   `platformFeePercent`: `Float` (The agreed-upon percentage for this module)
    *   `startDate`: `DateTime`
    *   `endDate`: `DateTime?`
    *   `metadata`: `Json?`
    *   `createdAt`: `DateTime`
    *   `updatedAt`: `DateTime`

## 4. Stripe Integration

The Commerce Module is deeply integrated with Stripe, operating primarily using the **Stripe Connect platform pattern**. This means our MBT platform account orchestrates transactions on behalf of connected `DirectoryBusiness` accounts. Each `DirectoryBusiness` (tenant) that uses the Commerce Module will have its own connected Stripe Express or Standard account, allowing them to directly receive funds while MBT can apply platform fees.

**Key Environment Variables:**

*   `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY`: The secret key for the MBT platform Stripe account. Used for creating Connect accounts, managing platform-level webhooks, and direct platform charges.
*   `STRIPE_CONNECT_WEBHOOK_SECRET`: The webhook secret for events coming from connected Stripe accounts (e.g., `invoice.payment_succeeded` from a Tuthill Design account).
*   `STRIPE_BILLING_WEBHOOK_SECRET`: The webhook secret for events coming from the MBT platform Stripe account (e.g., `checkout.session.completed` for platform-managed one-off sales).

**Webhook Event Handling:**

The `processStripeWebhookEvent` function handles critical events from both the platform and connected accounts to ensure data synchronization and trigger business logic.

*   **`checkout.session.completed`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Confirms successful completion of a Stripe Checkout session.
    *   **Action:** For one-off product purchases, triggers `fulfillOrder` to mark the `Order` as complete and potentially initiate fulfillment workflows. For subscription checkouts, updates `Subscription` status.
    *   **Origin:** Can be from platform account (e.g., for MBT-branded merch) or connected account (e.g., Tuthill Design selling a ticket).

*   **`invoice.payment_succeeded`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Indicates a successful payment for a subscription invoice.
    *   **Action:** Updates the `Subscription` status, `currentPeriodStart`, `currentPeriodEnd`. Crucially, this event also triggers the calculation and recording of the `platformFeeAmount` for MBT based on the `Subscription.platformFeePercent` and the invoice total.
    *   **Origin:** Primarily from connected accounts for tenant subscriptions.

*   **`customer.subscription.updated`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Captures changes to a subscription (e.g., plan upgrade/downgrade, trial end, status change).
    *   **Action:** Synchronizes the `Subscription` model with the latest Stripe data, including `planId`, `status`, `cancelAtPeriodEnd`, etc.
    *   **Origin:** Connected accounts.

*   **`customer.subscription.deleted`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Notifies of a subscription cancellation or deletion.
    *   **Action:** Marks the `Subscription` as 'canceled' in our database and sets `canceledAt`.
    *   **Origin:** Connected accounts.

*   **`customer.created`, `customer.updated`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Syncs customer information.
    *   **Action:** Creates or updates `Customer` records in our database, linking to `stripeCustomerId`.
    *   **Origin:** Both platform and connected accounts.

*   **`account.updated`**:
    *   **Purpose:** Notifies of changes to a connected Stripe account (e.g., capabilities, requirements, details).
    *   **Action:** Updates our internal representation of the connected `DirectoryBusiness`'s Stripe account status, potentially triggering alerts for pending requirements.
