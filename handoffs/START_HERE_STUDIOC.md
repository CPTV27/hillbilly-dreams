## How Review and Approval Works

Every review task on your list follows the same pattern:

1. Open the task
2. Click the link (a Lightroom gallery, a video, a draft article, a photo set)
3. Look at it
4. Leave an Asana comment saying either **"approved"** or what needs to change
5. Mark the task complete

No file uploads. No downloads. You say yes or you say what to fix.
