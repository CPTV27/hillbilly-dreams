## How This List Works

Every to-do you and Tracy need to do is now a task on your Asana list, assigned to **Big Muddy** (this shared seat). Open a task, do the thing, mark it done. Short, simple.

## How Review and Approval Works

Chase sends a lot of things for review — photo galleries, magazine drafts, video cuts, social posts. Every review task follows the same pattern:

1. Open the task
2. Click the link (it goes to a Lightroom gallery, a Google Doc, a live web page, etc.)
3. Look at it
4. Come back to the Asana task, leave a comment that says either **"approved"** or exactly what you want changed
5. Mark the task complete

**You never download files. You never upload files.** You just open the link, say yes or say what to fix, and mark it done.
