# Big Muddy Records — Strategy & Operations

## The Model

Big Muddy Records (originally "Monumental Taco") is a hybrid management company and label services partner. The full business plan lives in Google Drive: [Monumental Taco - How It Works](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1hdvkFmGgZfrHhEg6HXyWq7Q3n22LUTEo3ODkNqm2UAk/edit)

### Core Principles

1. **Artist keeps ownership** — No exceptions. No reversion clauses. You own your masters from day one.
2. **Artist funds their own production** — No debt. No recoupment.
3. **Label provides services at cost** — Marketing, publicity, management built into transparent retainer.
4. **Revenue share only when money is made** — Alignment of interests.
5. **Annual renewal** — Not locked in for multiple albums or years.

### Economic Structure

**Artist pays:**
- **$4,000/month retainer** ($48,000/year)
- **20-25% of gross revenue**

**What the retainer covers:**
- $2,000/month to services:
  - Professional publicist (year-round PR campaign)
  - $24,000/year marketing/advertising budget
  - Content creation (social videos, promotional assets)
  - Marketing tools and platforms
- $2,000/month to operations:
  - Strategic management
  - Creative oversight and quality control
  - Industry coordination and partnerships
  - Business operations

**Retainer recoupment:**
- Pay $48K over the year (monthly)
- At year-end, calculate 20-25% of gross revenue
- If that exceeds $48K, label receives the difference
- If it doesn't, retainer covers it (no additional payment)

### Ecosystem Advantage

Big Muddy Records isn't just a label — it's backed by a media ecosystem:

| Brand | Channel | What It Does for Artists |
|-------|---------|------------------------|
| Big Muddy Radio | bigmuddyradio.com | Playlists, live sessions, podcast features |
| Big Muddy Magazine | bigmuddymagazine.com | Features, interviews, photo essays |
| BuyCurious Art | buycuriousart.com | Merch, limited edition releases |
| Big Muddy Touring | bigmuddytouring.com | Venue (Blues Room), lodging, touring route |
| Outsider Economics | outsidereconomics.com | Philosophy, economic model storytelling |

Every release gets amplified through ALL of these channels. Earned media, not just paid ads.

- **Prevost touring bus** — Mobile production and touring
- **Photography (Chase Pierson)** — Professional visual content for every release
- **Delta Dawn (AI)** — Content generation, social media, visual assets

### Capacity

2-4 artists per year. Quality over quantity. Founder-level attention on every project.
