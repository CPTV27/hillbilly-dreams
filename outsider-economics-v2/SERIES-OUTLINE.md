# Outsider Economics — Post Series

Standalone posts. Each one works alone, hits on Substack, and stacks into a book if we want.

## The Sequence

1. **The $450,000 Secret** — DONE. The math nobody ran. 20 people, 20 hours, coordination premium. The hook for everything.

2. **The Extraction Trap** — Where your money actually goes. 82 cents of every dollar leaves your zip code in 30 days. The leaky bucket. Why your town works hard and stays broke.

3. **The Coordination Premium** — Why five people who talk to each other are worth 23x five people who don't. Metcalfe's Law for labor. The math behind the multiplier.

4. **Time Is the Only Currency That Can't Leave Town** — Your neighbor's Saturday can't get wired to a hedge fund. Why labor-based exchange is structurally un-extractable.

5. **The Task Board** — Not an app. Not a startup. A whiteboard with three columns. The simplest infrastructure that turns a neighborhood into an economy.

6. **Building Without Banks** — Internal exchange rates. Local currency mechanics. Post-capital community models that actually work.

7. **Federation, Not Scale** — Every org that scales past 100 people starts acting like the thing it was built to replace. So don't scale. Federate.

8. **The First 90 Days** — Step-by-step. Week by week. How to actually stand one of these up.

9. **What Kills Coordination Systems** — The pitfalls. Free riders, burnout, scope creep, the charismatic founder problem.

10. **The Off Switch** — CBDCs, programmable money, platform dependency. Why building a parallel system isn't paranoia — it's infrastructure.

## Format per post
- 1,500–2,500 words
- Standalone — no "last week we discussed"
- Opens with a scene or a number, never throat-clearing
- Ends with a concrete action (24h / 7d / 90d)
- Each post has its own math receipts
