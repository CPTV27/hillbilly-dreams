## Technical Notes

- **Drone:** DJI — wide establishing shots, overhead reveals, tracking shots on roads
- **Sony on gimbal:** Medium shots, slider-style moves, shallow DOF beauty shots
- **DJI Osmo:** Run-and-gun, candid moments, street footage
- **Frame rate:** 24fps for cinematic, 60fps for slow-motion beauty shots
- **Grade:** Warm, slightly desaturated, film-like. Not oversaturated. Not cold.
- **Audio:** Capture ambient sound with everything — river, music, crowd, rain. B-roll audio is gold for editing.
