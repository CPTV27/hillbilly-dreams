## Background & Career

JP Houston is a Canadian-born American entertainer with a career spanning decades across music, television, film, radio, and live performance. His piano-driven performances draw comparisons to Harry Nilsson and Billy Joel, while his humor and storytelling style has been compared to Bo Burnham and Tim Minchin.

### Early Career & Television Composing

- Composed the theme song for PBS/YTV children's series **The Big Comfy Couch** while still in high school -- one of his earliest professional credits
- Nominated for a **Gemini Award** (Canadian Emmy equivalent) in 2001
- Hundreds of composition credits across television, theater, and film
- Written for **PBS, BBC, HBO, CBC**, and many other networks
- Work appeared on **The Rosie O'Donnell Show**, **Funny or Die Presents**, and other national/international productions
- A song co-written by Houston was featured in the **Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade**
- Original music composer for TV series **Learn with Me** (2023-2025, 24 episodes)
- Songwriter and theme music composer for TV series **Sven & Visdommen** (1992-2013, 41 episodes)

### IMDB

### Live Performance & Touring

- Toured and recorded across North America and Europe
- Opening slots for **Leon Russell, Glen Hansard, Kyuss**, and others
- Led the **house band at Pappy & Harriet's** in Pioneertown, California for a two-year stint (wrapped 2013)
- At Pappy's, traded songs with guests including **Victoria Williams, Band of Horses, The Jayhawks, M. Ward, John Doe**
- Shared stage and studio with **Victoria Williams, Tim Easton, Gram Rabbit**
- Member of **The Relationship** -- Brian Bell's (Weezer) side project

### American Parlor Songbook (NPR)

- Launched in 2013 as a weekly syndicated NPR variety show
- Home station: **KVCR (FM) NPR**, Southern California
- Carried on **20+ stations** from Florida to Alaska
- Format: Original songs, stories, and comedy sketches performed from the piano
- Co-hosted with wife **Julie Van Dusen**
- Joined by guest musicians, comedians, actors, and raconteurs
- Available as podcast on TuneIn, PRX, and other platforms
- [KVCR: American Parlor Songbook](https://www.kvcrnews.org/show/american-parlor-songbook)
