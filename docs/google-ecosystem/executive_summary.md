## 2. CAPITAL PARTNER PROTECTION (Summary)

### Structural Protections
| Mechanism | Strength Rating |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Real Estate in Separate LLC | ★★★★★ |
| First Mortgage Position | ★★★★★ |
| Title Insurance | ★★★★★ |
| UCC Filings on Equipment | ★★★★☆ |

### Financial Safeguards
| Covenant | Threshold | Testing |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Preferred Return | 8% annually (current), 10% (cumulative) | Monthly |
| Debt Service Coverage | ≥ 1.25x | Quarterly |
| Loan-to-Value | ≤ 65% | Annually |
| Minimum Liquidity | 3 months OpEx | Monthly |
| CapEx Reserve | 5% of revenue | Monthly |

### Control Rights (Capital Partner Approval Required)
- Sale of real estate asset (unanimous)
- Additional debt encumbrance (unanimous)
- Major capital expenditures >$25K (majority)
- Change in business model (unanimous)
- Operator removal (majority)
- Annual budget (majority)

### Yield Expectations
| Scenario | Year 1-3 | Year 4-7 | Year 8+ |
|----------|----------|----------|---------|
| Conservative | 8-10% | 10-12% | 12-15% |
| Base Case | 10-12% | 12-15% | 15-18% |
| Upside Case | 12-15% | 15-20% | 20-25% |

## 3. OPERATOR (STUDIO C) COMPENSATION FRAMEWORK

### Base Compensation
| Component | Calculation | Annual |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Management Fee | 4% of Gross Revenue | $48,000 |
| Creative Services | Market rate | $60,000 |
| Reimbursables | Actual + 10% | $24,000 |
| **Total Base** | | **$132,000** |

### Performance-Based Compensation
| Tier | Trigger | Studio C Share |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Tier 1 | CAD exceeds preferred return | 15% of excess |
| Tier 2 | CAD exceeds 1.5x preferred | 20% of excess above 1.5x |
| Tier 3 | CAD exceeds 2x preferred | 25% of excess above 2x |

### Equity Participation
- **Class B Membership Interest**: 25-35% of operating entity
- **Vesting**: 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff
- **Performance Acceleration**: Full vesting if IRR > 20%

### Alignment Mechanisms
- Revenue growth targets (>15% YoY)
- EBITDA margin targets (>35%)
- Guest satisfaction scores (>4.7/5.0)
- Brand value metrics
- Clawback provisions for misrepresentation

```

                    ↓

                    ↓
    Hospitality (25% margin) benefits from brand
```
