# MBT Refund and Cancellation Policy

Welcome to MBT. We value your business and aim to provide clear, fair policies regarding refunds and cancellations for our various products and services. This document outlines the terms for subscriptions, one-off purchases, inn bookings, and private events. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and our procedures.

*   **Fairness:** We strive for fairness and transparency in all our dealings. Our goal is to ensure a clear understanding of our terms for every customer.
*   **Communication is Key:** If you have any concerns or questions regarding a purchase, booking, or subscription, please contact us first. We are committed to resolving issues promptly and effectively.
*   **Policy Updates:** This policy may be updated periodically. The version in effect at the time of your purchase, booking, or subscription renewal will apply.

## 1. Subscriptions

**(Applies to DSD, Records label services, Tuthill realtor packages, Radio sponsor packages, and similar recurring services.)**

*   **No Contract, Cancel Anytime:** You have the flexibility to cancel your subscription at any time without penalty or long-term commitment. There are no hidden fees for cancellation.
*   **Cancellation Default: Period-End:** When you choose to cancel your subscription, it will remain active, and you will continue to have full access to the service until the end of your current paid billing period (e.g., the end of the current month or year). No further charges will be made after the current period concludes. This ensures you receive the full value for the period you've paid for.
*   **No Refunds for Current Period (Industry Standard):** As a general rule, and consistent with industry standards for subscription services, we do not offer refunds for the current billing period once it has begun. You will retain access to the service for the remainder of the period you have already paid for.
*   **Notable Exception: Documented Service Failure:** In the rare event that we fail to provide the agreed-upon core service due to an issue directly attributable to us, and this failure is clearly documented and verified by MBT, you may be eligible for a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of your current billing period. This exception does not apply to issues arising from your own equipment, internet service, third-party software, or a misunderstanding of the service features on your part. We define "documented service failure" as a verifiable and sustained inability on our part to deliver the primary functionality of the subscribed service as advertised.

## 2. One-Off Orders

**(Applies to merchandise, prints, books, digital downloads, and tickets for events.)**

### Tickets

*   **Refundable Window:** Tickets for MBT events are fully refundable if your cancellation request is received up to 24 hours before the scheduled start time of the event.
*   **Within 24 Hours:** No refunds will be issued for cancellations made within 24 hours of the event's scheduled start time. This policy is in place due to the operational costs and commitments associated with event planning.
*   **Transferability:** If you are unable to attend an event for which you have purchased a ticket within the non-refundable window, you are welcome to transfer your ticket to another person. Please notify us of the transfer, especially if the ticket is personalized or requires specific entry credentials, to ensure smooth access for the new attendee.

### Physical Merchandise (Merch, Prints, Books)

*   **14-Day Return Window:** You may return unopened physical merchandise within 14 days of the delivery date for a full refund. To be eligible for a refund, items must be in their original, resalable condition, with all tags, packaging, and seals intact.
*   **Shipping Costs:** Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item received was damaged, defective, or incorrect due to an error on our part.
*   **Damaged or Incorrect Items:** If you receive an item that is damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, please contact us immediately (within 48 hours of receipt) with photographic evidence. We will arrange for a replacement or a full refund and will cover any associated return shipping costs if applicable.

### Digital Downloads

*   **No Refunds (Industry Standard):** Due to the nature of digital products, which are instantly accessible and cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense, all sales of digital downloads (e.g., music files, e-books, digital art prints, software licenses) are final and non-refundable. This policy is consistent with industry standards for digital content. We encourage you to review product descriptions carefully before making a purchase.

## 3. Inn Bookings (Direct)

**(Applies to bookings made directly through MBT, not through Online Travel Agencies (OTAs).)**

*   **Free Cancellation (Up to 7 Days):** You may cancel your inn booking free of charge and receive a full refund if your cancellation request is received 7 days or more before your scheduled arrival date.
*   **Partial Refund (3-7 Days):** For cancellations made between 3 and 7 days before your scheduled arrival date, a 50% refund of your total booking cost will be issued.
*   **No Refund (Within 3 Days):** Cancellations made within 3 days of your scheduled arrival date are generally non-refundable.
*   **Exception: Documented Emergencies:** We understand that unforeseen and unavoidable circumstances can arise. In cases of documented emergencies (e.g., severe illness requiring hospitalization, death in the immediate family, government-issued travel restrictions, or natural disasters preventing travel), please contact us as soon as possible. We will review such requests on a case-by-case basis and may offer a partial or full refund, or a credit for a future stay, at our sole discretion. Official documentation (e.g., doctor's note, death certificate, government advisory) will be required for verification.
*   **OTA Bookings:** Bookings made through Online Travel Agencies (e.g., Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb) are subject to the specific cancellation and refund policies of that respective OTA. Please refer directly to their terms and conditions, as we cannot override or modify policies for bookings made through third-party platforms.
