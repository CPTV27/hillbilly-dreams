## Concept A — “Digital hygiene”

**Hero headline:** Your Google listing is lying to tourists in Natchez — and you’re paying for it in empty tables.

**Subtext:** Wrong hours, old photos, silent reviews, dead social feeds — that’s the math customers see before they ever walk in. Deep South Directory fixes the basics, keeps them current, and posts on a schedule you approve. You run the business; we run the digital hygiene.

**Tiers (one line each)**

- **Free** — Limited AI, your business profile, Gmail hooked in — enough to look alive.
- **$25 Essentials** — Full AI that knows your menu and your reviews — not a generic chatbot. Earn credits when you feed the network real content.
- **$50 Pro** — The professional network for the corridor — LinkedIn energy, Deep South addresses. Vicksburg, Clarksdale, Natchez — not a coast you’ve never met.
- **$99 Marketing** — Listings corrected, reviews watched, social drafted weekly — you approve what ships. Set the cadence; we don’t ghost you after signup.
- **$250 Engine** — Magazine, radio, photography — the same media stack we run on ourselves, pointed at your business.

**CTA button text:** Fix my listing — start free

**Why Deep South Directory**

- **The math is visible** — Search your business on your phone right now. That’s what a stranger in Natchez sees tonight.
- **Credits, not coupons** — Contribute photos, events, or copy the corridor can use — your bill drops when you show up.
- **No $1,000-a-month agency retainer** — $99 for the hygiene pack beats paying someone in another time zone to forget you exist.
- **You approve the posts** — Autopilot doesn’t mean silent autopilot. Nothing goes live without your OK.

## Concept B — “Replaces ChatGPT”

**Hero headline:** Cancel the generic AI — this one already knows your business.

**Subtext:** It’s trained on your hours, your reviews, your competitors, and the people actually searching for dinner in Natchez tonight — not the whole internet. Start at $25 for the full assistant; step up when you’re ready for the network and the hygiene layer.

**Tiers (one line each)**

- **Free** — Tight AI access, live profile, Gmail connected — proof we’re not a PDF download.
- **$25 Essentials** — The ChatGPT replacement — full AI, your context, your voice. Credits back when you publish useful stuff to the directory.
- **$50 Pro** — LinkedIn plus ChatGPT — for the Deep South. Professional graph for operators who’d rather shake hands in Greenville than cold-DM a stranger in Austin.
- **$99 Marketing** — Google Business Profile handled, reviews answered, social on rails — draft, approve, repeat.
- **$250 Engine** — Full media company access — print, airwaves, lens — the Big Muddy machine on contract.

**CTA button text:** Start with Essentials — $25

**Why Deep South Directory**

- **Context beats clever** — Generic models don’t know your Friday special or your last one-star meltdown — ours does.
- **The corridor is the dataset** — Real towns, real businesses — Natchez to Memphis — not scraped noise.
- **Upgrade when the pain changes** — AI first, network second, hygiene third — you don’t buy the Ferrari to drive to the mailbox.
- **Credits reward participation** — The more you give the network, the less extraction hits your card.

## Concept C — “Set it and forget it — with receipts”

**Hero headline:** Marketing that runs without you babysitting a dozen tabs — from $99 a month, not $1,000.

**Subtext:** We wire your listings, monitor reviews, draft weekly posts, and hold the line on accuracy — you sign off, we ship. Free and $25 tiers get you in the door with AI and credits; $50 adds the corridor network; $250 turns on the full media engine.

**Tiers (one line each)**

- **Free** — On-ramp AI, business profile live, Gmail integrated — see the dashboard before you spend a dollar.
- **$25 Essentials** — Full AI that replaces the generic bots — plus earn-down credits when your content helps the map.
- **$50 Pro** — Professional network for the Mississippi corridor — introductions and reputation that travel farther than a Facebook group.
- **$99 Marketing** — Digital hygiene as a subscription — Google right, reviews answered, social consistent — approve and move on.
- **$250 Engine** — Magazine placement, radio reach, photography — the operating stack behind Big Muddy — pointed at you.

**CTA button text:** Set it up — see tiers

**Why Deep South Directory**

- **Retainer math loses** — One dinner rush in Natchez covers a month of hygiene — compare that to a coastal agency invoice.
- **Forgotten listings cost real money** — Wrong hours aren’t a typo — they’re a door that never opens.
- **You stay sovereign** — We’re not renting you to a national directory that sells ads against your name.
- **Tiers match how you actually grow** — AI, then network, then always-on marketing, then media — no bundle you don’t need yet.
