# Term Sheet: Scan2Plan + Twinner Growth & Ops Pilot

**12-Month Agreement | Hillbilly Dreams Inc → Scan2Plan LLC / Twinner LLC**

---

## Objective

Recover lost margin through automation. Scale new bookings without adding headcount. License the technology by module so you pay for what you use.

---

## Module 1: Core Ops Node — $2,000/mo
*Replaces: a part-time office manager or bookkeeper*

- **Financial Sync** — Real-time QuickBooks mapping. See actual profit per job the moment the data hits, not at month-end.
- **Project Rails** — End-to-end tracking from lead to deliverable. No "where is this job?" emails.
- **Capacity Forecasting** — Pipeline data tells you when to hire and when to push, based on numbers, not gut feel.
- **Unified View** — Both Scan2Plan and Twinner on one screen. One login, one dashboard.
- **Infrastructure** — Cloud compute, security, backups, monitoring. All managed.

## Module 2: Growth Engine — $1,000/mo
*Replaces: a marketing agency or lead-gen service*

- **Precision Outreach** — Authenticated email sequences targeting high-value commercial accounts (architects, GCs, facility managers).
- **Case Study Autopilot** — Completed projects automatically become SEO-rich case studies on the website.
- **Attribution Tracking** — Every lead the system generates is tagged. We only claim performance fees on leads this module produces. Your direct sales are yours.
- **Google Business Profile Sync** — Local SEO optimization runs automatically.

## Module 3: Efficiency Dividend — $0 upfront / $100 per project
*Replaces: site visits, manual measuring, travel time*

This module is licensed separately. No monthly fee. You pay per use, and the customer pays for it — not you.

- **AI Video Scoping** — Instead of a $500 site visit, the client shoots a walkthrough with their phone. The AI extracts square footage, materials, hazards, room count, and project scope. A structured quote lands in the system in minutes.
- **Invoice Line Item** — Appears on the customer's invoice as "AI Scoping & Data Processing Fee: $100"
- **The Split** — Owen keeps $50 (pure profit, no expenses). HDI receives $50 (licensing fee).
- **No Cap** — More projects, more revenue. Both sides scale together.

**The math:**

| Method | Cost to Customer | Owen's Margin | HDI's Cut |
|---|---|---|---|
| Manual scoping (site visit) | ~$500 (labor + travel) | ~$100 after expenses | $0 |
| AI video scoping | **$100** (tech fee) | **$50** (pure profit) | **$50** (license) |

Customer saves ~$400. Owen nets $50 for zero extra work. At 858 estimates/year, that's $42,900 in new margin for Owen and $42,900 in licensing revenue for HDI.

### How This Changes Twinner

For Twinner projects (digital twins, higher complexity, higher stakes):

- **Virtual Site Audit** — Offer a $150 "pre-scan assessment" using the AI video tool. Costs you $50 in licensing. Gets you in the door with zero risk.
- **Zero-Risk Quoting** — Most companies lose money on digital twin projects because scope creep during manual site visits is brutal. The AI doesn't miss details. You quote from data, not a field tech's gut feeling.
- **Upsell Path** — Every Scan2Plan project is a Twinner lead. "We scanned your building. Want a persistent digital twin for $X/year?"
