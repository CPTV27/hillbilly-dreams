# Tracy's Dashboard — Quick Start Guide

## What You're Looking At

When you open the computer, Chrome will show your personal dashboard. There are three pages bookmarked for you:

---

### 1. Your Dashboard (`/tracy`)
**This is your home base.** It shows:
- The business overview for all 8 companies
- Revenue targets and occupancy goals for the Inn
- The wedding opportunity breakdown
- What Chase needs from you (5 specific asks)
- The ownership structure (equal thirds: Chase, Tracy, Amy)

**To get here:** Click the "Tracy" bookmark or go to measurablybetterthings.com/tracy

---

### 2. Review Desk (`/admin/review`)
**This is where you approve content.** The AI agents generate drafts — social posts, magazine blurbs, radio scripts — and they show up here for your approval.

**How it works:**
- You'll see a stack of cards, each with a piece of content
- **Green button (✓ Approve)** — content is good, send it out
- **Red button (✗ Reject)** — throw it away
- **Refine button** — type a note like "make it warmer" or "too long" and the AI rewrites it
- **Click any text to edit it directly** — just click, type, and click away to save

You don't need to write anything from scratch. Just review what the AI made and tune it.

---

### 3. Command Center (`/admin/command`)
**This is the overview of everything.** It shows:
- How many businesses are in the directory
- Links to all 11 websites
- The top sales targets ranked by urgency
- A search box to find anything in the knowledge base

---

## The Daily Routine (15 minutes)

1. **Open the Review Desk** — see what the agents drafted overnight
2. **Scroll through the cards** — approve the good ones, reject the bad ones
3. **Edit anything that needs a human touch** — click the text and fix it
4. **Check your dashboard** — see if anything needs attention
5. **Done** — the approved content publishes automatically

---

## If Something Looks Wrong

- **Wrong photo on a page?** Tell Chase — he can swap it from the Media Vault
- **Content sounds off?** Click "Refine" and type what's wrong
- **Can't log in?** The password is your name (tracy, tracy alderson-allen, or tracy alderson allen)
- **Need Chase?** He's monitoring everything from his laptop
