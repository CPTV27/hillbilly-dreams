## 4. Production & Installation Notes

- **Installation Order:**
    1.  Apply full-body 2080-M12 base wrap to all panels.
    2.  Apply 2080-G240 gold hairline accents over the base wrap.
    3.  Apply all cut-vinyl elements (wordmarks, logo grid, QR codes) last.
- **Heat Application:** Use a heat gun for conforming vinyl to complex curves. Do not exceed 3M's recommended surface temperature of 212°F (100°C). Post-heat all stretched areas and edges to 190-212°F to prevent lift.
- **Edge Sealing:** Apply a thin, even bead of 3M Edge Sealer 3950 to all exposed vinyl edges, especially in wheel wells, door jambs, and along the roof line.
- **QR Code Production:**
    - **Error Correction:** Use Level H (High, ~30%) for maximum scan reliability.
    - **Module Size:** For a 4"x4" code, ensure module size is sufficient for scanning from 3-5 feet. **Note:** Reliable scanning from 15+ feet requires a code size of approximately 18"x18". The 4" spec is for close-range interaction.
    - **URL:** Use the full, canonical URLs provided. Do not use URL shorteners. Test all codes with multiple devices (iOS, Android) before final application.
- **Matte Vinyl Care:** Provide client with care instructions. No wax or polish. Use only cleaners specifically designed for matte finishes (e.g., Chemical Guys Meticulous Matte, 3M Matte Wrap Detailer). Hand-wash only.
- **Expected Lifespan:** 3M 2080 series has a vertical exposure lifespan of up to 7 years. Horizontal surfaces (hood, roof) may see a reduced lifespan.
