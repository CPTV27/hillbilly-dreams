## 3 · What we sell

### Offering 1 — Multicam event production (per event)
- **What it is:** Multi-camera shoot of concerts, festivals, corporate events, weddings
- **What's included:** Pre-production · multi-camera shoot · audio capture (or take from venue feed) · same-day backup
- **Price:** $___ /day for standard · $___ /day for premium
- **Packaging:** Per-event; day rate; multi-day discounts
- **Sold via:** Direct outreach to venues / festivals / touring acts · referrals from Big Muddy Touring · cross-sell from Tuthill realtor clients hosting events

### Offering 2 — Broadcast-quality live streaming
- **What it is:** Real-time stream to YouTube · Twitch · client's platform · Big Muddy Radio video integration
- **What's included:** Multi-camera switch · branded graphics · audio mix · stream encoding · post-stream archive
- **Price:** $___ /day production fee + $___ for streaming infrastructure
- **Packaging:** Per-event; recurring stream contracts available
- **Sold via:** Same channels · also venue partnerships (regular venue gets recurring rate)

### Offering 3 — Post production
- **What it is:** Editing of concerts, events, documentaries (often Big Muddy or Tuthill source material)
- **What's included:** Edit · color · audio mix · titles · delivery in client formats
- **Price:** $___ per finished minute · $___ per project
- **Packaging:** Per project
- **Sold via:** Direct · post-shoot upsell from clients we shoot live · external edit-only clients

### Offering 4 — Premium cinema package (Wave 1 + Phase 1 video kit dependent)
- **What it is:** Higher-tier multicam with wireless TX, gimbal, full Ronin 4D rig
- **What's included:** Standard offering + wireless multi-cam coverage · cinema-grade lensing
- **Price:** $___ /day (premium tier — currently ~$4,500 reference)
- **Packaging:** Premium tier above standard multicam
- **Sold via:** Same channels; pitched for high-stakes events

### Offering 5 — Recurring venue partnerships
- **What it is:** Standing arrangement with a venue to cover all/most shows
- **What's included:** Discounted rate per show · priority scheduling · co-branded archive
- **Price:** $___ /mo retainer covers N shows
- **Packaging:** Monthly recurring
- **Sold via:** Direct to venues with regular programming

### Offering 6 — Studio C self-marketing (eats own dog food)
- **What it is:** Studio C uses MBT's social + content + portfolio modules to market itself
- **What's included:** Internal use of platform
- **Price:** Internal cost — covered through Studio C's MBT relationship
- **Packaging:** Internal
- **Sold via:** N/A (this is operational tooling)
