# Brand Offering — Big Muddy Records

*Pre-populated draft for Monday April 20 partner session. Major model clarification 2026-04-18 evening per Chase: house catalog + non-exclusive promo + recurring label services for approved artists.*

---

## 1 · The brand in one sentence

A label with three concentric purposes: (1) house the **Amy Allen** catalog and the **Mechanical Bull** catalog, (2) offer **non-exclusive promotion partnerships** to outside artists who aren't on a label and would benefit from Big Muddy's media structure, and (3) for artists we approve onto the label proper, add a **low monthly fee** for full marketing and label-type services. Recurring revenue, not just unit sales.

## 3 · What we sell

### Offering 0 — In-house catalog (the foundation)
- **Amy Allen catalog** — recordings, releases, ongoing
- **Mechanical Bull catalog** — recordings, releases, ongoing
- **What's included:** Full label release support: distribution, marketing, merchandise, sync exposure
- **Price:** Internal — costs absorbed; revenue flows back to artists per their splits with the label
- **Sold via:** All channels below

### Offering 1 — Physical records (catalog + partner artists)
- **What it is:** Vinyl + CD pressings of releases
- **What's included:** Record + sleeve/jacket art (often Chase photography) + insert
- **Price:** $___ vinyl · $___ CD
- **Packaging:** Single release; potential subscription / record-of-the-quarter
- **Sold via:** Records site · in-Inn gift shop · merch table at shows · partner record stores

### Offering 2 — Downloads + streaming (catalog + partner artists)
- **What it is:** Direct-purchase digital + distribution to Spotify · Apple Music · Tidal · YouTube Music
- **What's included:** Lossless + MP3 versions; ongoing streaming royalties (split with artists per contract)
- **Price:** Downloads $___ per album / $___ per single. Streaming free to listeners; revenue per stream split.
- **Sold via:** Records site · Bandcamp · streaming platforms

### Offering 3 — Merch tied to releases
- **What it is:** Shirts, posters, stickers tied to specific releases
- **Price:** $___ per item
- **Packaging:** Per release; bundle with vinyl pre-orders
- **Sold via:** Records site · merch table at shows

### Offering 4 — Non-exclusive promotion partnership (NEW — outside artists)
- **What it is:** A no-equity, no-exclusivity arrangement to help an outside artist reach audiences via Big Muddy's media structure (Touring, Magazine, Radio, Records-adjacent promotion)
- **What's included:** Artist gets included in playlists, episode coverage, magazine features, show booking consideration. Big Muddy gets first-look on releases for promotion.
- **Price:** TBD — could be barter (artist comps Big Muddy on shows or recording sessions), small flat per cycle, or revenue share on what we directly help generate
- **Packaging:** Cycle-based (e.g., quarterly) or release-based
- **Sold via:** Direct artist outreach · agent network
- **Why it works:** Most artists who aren't on a label have no infrastructure. We have the structure already running for our in-house catalog. Adding outside artists to the existing pipeline costs us little; the promotional reach is real value.

### Offering 5 — Recurring label services (NEW — for artists we approve onto the label proper)
- **What it is:** Low monthly fee for full label-style marketing and services
- **What's included:** Distribution management · promotion (radio/podcast/playlist/social/magazine) · merchandise pipeline · sync licensing outreach · monthly performance dashboard · Studio C support at preferred rates
- **Price:** $___ /month (low — designed to be accessible)
- **Packaging:** Month-to-month, 30-day notice
- **Sold via:** Approved-artist invitation only — Big Muddy Records picks who's on this tier
- **Critical model note:** This is the **recurring revenue play** for Big Muddy Records. Unit sales are lumpy; service subscriptions are predictable.

### Offering 6 — Sync licensing (future — both catalog and partner artists)
- **What it is:** Catalog tracks licensed for film, TV, ad, podcast use
- **What's included:** License rights · usage scope · term
- **Price:** Per-license (varies)
- **Packaging:** Per request
- **Sold via:** Direct outreach + sync agents
