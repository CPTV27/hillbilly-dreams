## What We Are

Big Muddy Entertainment is a music company based in Natchez, Mississippi. We run a record label, a radio network, a touring circuit, and a live performance band — all on one platform, all under one roof.

The roof happens to be a six-room blues hotel on the Mississippi River.

---

## What We Operate

### Big Muddy Records
Artist-friendly label services. We don't own your masters. You do. 100%. Always.

What we provide:
- Distribution (digital + physical)
- Production support
- Catalog management
- Sync licensing and placement
- Royalty tracking and direct Stripe payouts

**The deal:** Flat monthly retainer + revenue share. Artist keeps 100% of masters. We make money when you make money.

### Big Muddy Radio
Curated playlists, live sessions, and the American Parlor Songbook — streaming from Natchez to everywhere.

- Playlist curation across blues, gospel, Americana, Delta soul
- Live session recording at the Blues Room
- Podcast and interview content
- Streaming on all major platforms

### Big Muddy Touring — The Snowbird Circuit
A working touring route along the Mississippi Corridor. Memphis to New Orleans and 14 more cities across five states.

- 18 cities on the route
- Real venues, real audiences
- Prevost bus logistics
- Lodging at corridor properties (starting with the Inn)
- Every tour stop is a content event — radio, magazine, social

### Rise Up Gospel & Blues Band
A touring band and talent development program. Not a one-off act. A franchisable brand.

**The model:**
- Amy Pierson and Arri Aslin anchor the flagship unit
- Regional talent fills the rotating cast — pulled from tour stop talent searches
- Multiple units can tour simultaneously (like Yachtley Crew or Blue Man Group)
- Every show is a performance AND a talent search
- US first. Europe next.

**What Rise Up produces:**
- Live entertainment at the Inn's Blues Room
- Content for Radio (recordings) and Magazine (stories)
- New artist discovery for Records
- Community investment in every town we play

---

## How It All Connects

One artist booking triggers seven revenue events:

1. They play the Inn's stage
2. The show gets recorded for Radio
3. Magazine writes the city guide for the tour stop
4. The artist goes on Rise Up's roster
5. Their catalog lives on Records
6. The touring route adds the venue
7. Kiosk mode sells the ticket at the front desk

Every dollar circulates. No division is an island.

## What Makes Us Different

| Us | Traditional Label |
|----|-------------------|
| 100% masters retained | Label owns masters |

| Built-in venue (the Inn) | No venue relationship |
| Built-in media (Radio + Magazine) | Hire a publicist |
