## 1. Tier Architecture

### Directory — "Front Porch" — $99/mo
*What they have now. The hook.*

| Deliverable | Quantity |
|---|---|
| Social posts (1 platform) | 12/mo |
| GBP optimization | Setup + monthly |
| Magazine directory listing | Static listing |
| Monthly performance report | PDF email |
| AI search optimization | Basic schema |

**What it replaces:** Social scheduling ($30) + review tool ($250) + email tool ($30) = ~$310/mo in separate tools. They save $200+/mo on day one.

**What it doesn't do:** No content calendar. No cross-promotion. No video. No dedicated strategy. No magazine features. No radio. No events. They're in the directory but they're not in the *network*.

---

### Pro — "River Room" — $499/mo
*The upgrade. Full network access.*

| Deliverable | Quantity |
|---|---|
| Social posts (up to 6 platforms) | 60/mo |
| Professional images | 8/mo |
| Short-form video | 2/mo |
| Dedicated content calendar | Weekly cadence |
| Full review management | Response + generation |
| Weekly email newsletter | Branded template |
| Magazine feature articles | 1/quarter |
| Radio mentions (Anthologist Radio) | 2/mo |
| Event cross-promotion | All corridor events |
| Dedicated magazine page | Permanent |
| Monthly strategy call | 30 min |
| Analytics dashboard | Real-time, self-serve |
| AI search optimization | Full structured data |

**The pitch (see Section 3 below):** You're getting found at $99. At $499, you get *promoted*. Your business shows up in the magazine, on the radio, in every event listing. You're not just in the directory — you're part of the story we're telling about this corridor.

**What it replaces:** Social manager ($500) + email tool ($50) + review platform ($300) + content creation ($500) + analytics ($50) = ~$1,400/mo. They save $900/mo.

---

*Full media partnership. Co-branded everything.*

| Deliverable | Quantity |
|---|---|

| Professional photography | Monthly session (20 images) |

**What "full media package" means in plain English:**
We run your entire digital presence. You show up, do your thing, and we handle the story — the photos, the posts, the newsletter, the magazine placement, the radio spot, the ads. When someone Googles "best [your category] in Natchez," you're the answer. When someone asks ChatGPT, same thing. When someone picks up the magazine or tunes into the radio, your name's in rotation. You're not a listing. You're a brand partner.

**What it replaces:** Marketing hire ($3,000) + photographer ($500) + videographer ($800) + ad management ($500) + PR ($1,000) + analytics ($200) = ~$6,000/mo. They save $4,500/mo.

## 2. Pro Candidate Criteria — Natchez Focus

**Not restaurants.** Restaurants churn. They're price-sensitive, seasonal, and the owner is in the kitchen. Pro candidates are businesses where the owner has time to *use* the dashboard and where digital presence directly drives revenue.

### Tier 1 Candidates (Highest fit for $499 Pro)

| Category | Why Pro | Example Businesses |
|---|---|---|
| **Boutique hotels / B&Bs** | Occupancy = bookable online. Reviews drive revenue directly. Need content year-round. Already spending $250-600/mo on tools. | Monmouth Historic Inn, Dunleith, The Guest House, Mark Twain Guesthouse |
| **Event venues / wedding venues** | High-value bookings ($5K-50K). One extra booking/year pays for the tier. Need portfolio content + social proof. | Stanton Hall, The Carriage House, Dunleith event space |
| **Real estate offices** | Listings need exposure. Agents need personal brands. Market reports = newsletter content. | Natchez Realty, Keller Williams Natchez, Adams County listings |
| **Law firms** | Reputation-driven. Reviews matter enormously. Need to be the Google answer for "Natchez attorney." | Regional firms with 3+ attorneys |
| **Medical/dental practices** | Patient acquisition is digital. Reviews = everything. High LTV per patient. | Dentists, optometrists, specialty clinics |

### Tier 2 Candidates (Good fit, longer sales cycle)

| Category | Why | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tour operators** | Sell experiences online. Need seasonal content. | Natchez Pilgrimage Tours, ghost tours, river tours |
| **Antique districts / galleries** | Need to reach collectors beyond walk-in traffic. | Franklin Street antique row |
| **Professional services** | Accounting firms, insurance agencies, financial advisors. Reputation + search visibility. | Trust-based, review-driven |

### Enterprise ($1,500) Candidates

| Type | Why Enterprise | Candidate Profile |
|---|---|---|
| **Hotels with 20+ rooms** | Revenue justifies full media. Need managed ads + professional content pipeline. | Monmouth, Dunleith, any incoming boutique hotel operator |
| **Destination venues** | Weddings, corporate retreats. Need co-branded content + PR. | Stanton Hall, any venue doing 50+ events/year |
| **Multi-location businesses** | Need unified brand across locations. | Regional chains with Natchez presence |
| **Tourism boards / CVBs** | Natchez Convention & Visitors Bureau. Full partnership. | Visit Natchez |

## 3. The Upgrade Pitch

### Email / Conversation Script: Directory → Pro

**Subject:** Your directory listing is working. Here's what's next.

---

Hey [Name],

Quick update on your Measurably Better listing — you've gotten [X views / X clicks / X direction requests] this month. The directory is doing its job.

But here's the thing: you're competing for attention with every other listing. Right now, your business shows up. At Pro, your business gets *featured*.

**What changes at $499/mo:**

- **60 posts/month across 6 platforms** instead of 12 on one. Your Instagram, Facebook, Google, TikTok — all running, all on-brand, all scheduled.
- **Magazine features.** Not a directory listing — an actual article about your business in the Corridor Magazine, quarterly.
- **Radio mentions.** Your name on Anthologist Radio, twice a month.
- **Video content.** 2 short-form videos per month. The kind of stuff that actually gets shared.
- **Real-time dashboard.** Not a monthly PDF — a live dashboard you can check anytime. See what's working, what's not.
- **Monthly strategy call.** 30 minutes with someone who knows your market and your numbers.

You're currently spending [estimated $X/mo] on [tools they likely use]. Pro replaces all of it and adds the network — the magazine, the radio, the events, the corridor-wide audience that no single tool gives you.

One hotel owner told us a single booking from a magazine feature paid for six months of Pro.

Want to see the dashboard? I can set up a 15-minute walkthrough this week.

[Name]

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I'm happy at $99" | "You should be — it's a great deal. Pro is for when you want to stop managing your own content and start being part of the media network. Most businesses upgrade after they see what the directory alone can't do." |
| "$499 is a lot" | "You're probably spending $300-500 on tools that don't talk to each other. Pro replaces all of them and adds the magazine, radio, and events. Net cost is often lower than what you're paying now." |
| "I don't have time for strategy calls" | "That's the point — we do the strategy. The call is 30 minutes so you can tell us what's coming up and we build the content around it. Most clients say it's the most productive 30 minutes of their month." |
| "What if it doesn't work?" | "Month to month. No contract. If you don't see results in 90 days, drop back to Directory. We'll still be here." |
