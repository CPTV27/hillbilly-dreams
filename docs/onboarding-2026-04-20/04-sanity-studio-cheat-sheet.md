### THE 4 MOST COMMON TASKS

1.  **Edit an existing article**
    1.  Sidebar → `Article`
    2.  Click the article title you want to edit.
    3.  Edit fields (Title, Hero Image, Body, etc.).
    4.  Click `Publish` (blue button, top right).
    5.  Refresh `bigmuddymagazine.com` after ~60 seconds to see the change.

2.  **Change a photo**
    1.  In any image field, click `Select` or `Replace`.
    2.  Choose `Big Muddy Photo Library` from the options.
    3.  Click the photo you want to use.
    4.  Click `Publish` to make the change live.

3.  **Edit a page hero (Pricing, Shows, etc.)**
    1.  Sidebar → `Page Content`.
    2.  Find the page by its slug (e.g., `pricing`, `shows`, `private-events`).
    3.  Edit the `Hero Headline`, `Hero Subhead`, or `Footer` fields.
    4.  Click `Publish`.

4.  **Create a new article from scratch**
    1.  Sidebar → `Article` → Click `Create new` (pencil icon, top right).
    2.  Fill required fields: `Title`, `Slug` (auto-generates), `Category`, `City`.
    3.  Pick `Hero Image` from the Photo Library.
    4.  Type or paste body content into the editor.
    5.  Click `Publish`.

### QUICK REFERENCE

> #### KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
> - **`Cmd` + `S`**: Save draft
> - **`Cmd` + `Shift` + `P`**: Publish document
> - **`Cmd` + `/`**: Toggle keyboard shortcuts help
> - **`Esc`**: Close current view/dialog

> #### DRAFT vs. PUBLISHED
> - **Yellow Dot** (in sidebar): Unsaved changes.
> - **Green Checkmark**: Published and live.
> - **Workflow**: Save your draft first (`Cmd+S`), then `Publish`.

### WHEN SOMETHING IS WEIRD

-   **"I don't see my change live"**
    -   Wait 60 seconds, then do a hard refresh (`Cmd`+`Shift`+`R` on Chrome).

-   **"I can't find the article I was working on"**
    -   Check the `Drafts` filter at the top of the article list.

-   **"My image won't upload"**
    -   File is likely too big. Use the `Big Muddy Photo Library` instead of uploading a new file.

-   **"I deleted something by accident"**
    -   Tell Chase immediately. Sanity has version history and can restore it.
