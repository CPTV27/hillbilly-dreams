# Social Post Content-Type Template Specification

This document defines the template specification for generating social media posts within the MBT Content Creation Wizard. It outlines the rules for brand voice selection, platform-specific constraints, content elements, and the underlying AI system prompt.

---

## 1. Brand Routing

The wizard determines the appropriate brand voice profile for a social post based on the following hierarchy:

1.  **Explicit Override**: If an explicit `voice_profile` is provided during the content generation request, it takes precedence.
2.  **Source Content / Draft Brand**: If no explicit override, the wizard infers the brand from the `source_content_brand` or `draft_brand` associated with the content being promoted.
3.  **Default**: If neither of the above is available, the wizard will default to the "Big Muddy CPP" voice profile.

**Brand-to-Voice Mapping:**

*   **Inn**: "Big Muddy Inn Voice" (Warm, inviting, luxurious, focused on hospitality and experience)
*   **Magazine**: "Big Muddy Magazine Voice" (Informative, insightful, culturally aware, journalistic but engaging)
*   **Touring**: "Big Muddy Touring Voice" (Energetic, exciting, community-focused, emphasizes live music experience)
*   **Records**: "Big Muddy Records Voice" (Authentic, passionate, artist-centric, focused on music discovery and appreciation)
*   **Radio**: "Big Muddy Radio Voice" (Friendly, engaging, community-oriented, highlights local connection and music curation)
*   **CPP**: "Big Muddy CPP Voice" (Broad, organizational, community-benefit focused, professional but accessible)
*   **Tuthill**: "Tuthill Foundation Voice" (Philanthropic, impactful, formal but inspiring, focused on legacy and community investment)
*   **Studio C**: "Studio C Voice" (Creative, innovative, collaborative, highlights production quality and artistic process)

## 2. Per-Platform Constraints

The wizard applies specific formatting and length constraints based on the target social media platform.

### Instagram

*   **Character Limit (Caption)**: ~2,200 characters (practical limit for readability: ~500-1000 characters).
*   **Hashtag Conventions**: 5-15 relevant hashtags. Place in a separate block at the end of the caption or in the first comment. Mix broad, niche, and branded hashtags.
*   **Link Handling**: **No direct links in captions.** The prompt should instruct the AI to use phrases like "Find more at our profile link" or similar, *without* explicitly using "link in bio" (see Forbidden Phrases).
*   **Emoji Policy**: Liberal use. Emojis should enhance tone, break up text, and add visual interest.

### TikTok

*   **Character Limit (Caption)**: ~150 characters. Prioritize brevity and impact.
*   **Hashtag Conventions**: 3-5 highly relevant, trending, or branded hashtags. Integrate naturally into the caption where possible.
*   **Link Handling**: **No direct links in captions.** Assume video content will include a visual CTA or a call to action to "check profile."
*   **Emoji Policy**: Liberal use. Emojis are essential for conveying tone and energy in short-form video captions.

### Facebook

*   **Character Limit (Post)**: ~63,206 characters (practical limit for engagement: ~200-500 characters).
*   **Hashtag Conventions**: 1-3 relevant hashtags. Integrate naturally within the post or at the end.
*   **Link Handling**: Direct links are encouraged and should be included when relevant. The wizard will automatically strip the link preview if an image/video is attached.
*   **Emoji Policy**: Moderate use. Emojis should add warmth and personality but not dominate the text.

### X (formerly Twitter)

*   **Character Limit (Tweet)**: 280 characters. Brevity is paramount.
*   **Hashtag Conventions**: 1-2 highly relevant, concise hashtags. Place at the end or integrate if it doesn't hinder readability.
*   **Link Handling**: Direct links are encouraged and should be included. Links automatically shorten.
*   **Emoji Policy**: Sparse to moderate use. Emojis should be used strategically for emphasis or to convey emotion concisely.

## 3. Voice Routing Rules

Cross-brand contamination is strictly prohibited. The wizard employs a hard-fail mechanism if an attempt is made to generate content for a brand using a voice profile from a different, unrelated brand.

**Specific Rule:**
*   If the `source_brand` is 'Records', the `voice_profile` *must* be "Big Muddy Records Voice" or a specifically approved sub-variant. An attempt to use "Big Muddy Magazine Voice" (or any other non-Records voice) will result in a hard-fail and an error message.
*   Conversely, if the `source_brand` is 'Magazine', the `voice_profile` *must* be "Big Muddy Magazine Voice." An attempt to use "Big Muddy Records Voice" (or any other non-Magazine voice) will result in a hard-fail.

This rule applies to all brand pairings where distinct identities are critical. The wizard will check if the selected `voice_profile` is compatible with the `source_brand` before passing the request to the AI model.

## 4. Required Elements Per Post Type

Each social post generated by the AI must contain the following structural elements, adapted for the target platform and brand voice:

1.  **Opening Hook**: An engaging first sentence or phrase designed to immediately grab attention and encourage further reading/viewing.
2.  **Central Content**: The core message or information being conveyed, tailored to the specific platform's length and tone.
3.  **Soft Call to Action (CTA)**: A subtle, brand-appropriate prompt encouraging further engagement, as defined in Section 8.
4.  **Hashtag Block (if applicable)**: A set of relevant hashtags, formatted according to platform conventions. For platforms like TikTok where hashtags are often integrated, this might be less of a distinct "block."

---

## 5. Forbidden Phrases

The wizard will regex-block the following exact strings from appearing in any generated social post. The AI should be instructed to avoid these, and the wizard will act as a final filter.

*   `check out`
*   `don't miss`
*   `tag a friend`
*   `link in bio` (This phrase is overused and can be replaced with more creative, brand-specific CTAs)
*   `this is your sign`
