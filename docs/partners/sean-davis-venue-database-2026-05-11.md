**Date:** 2026-05-11

## TL;DR

- **66 venues** across **14 towns** in **4 states** (TN, AR, MS, LA), Mississippi corridor.
- **46 of 66 (70%)** are in our **50–300 capacity sweet spot** (tiers XS + S). That's where the booking math works.
- **Density leaders:** Memphis (15), Clarksdale (11), New Orleans (9). These three towns are 53% of the database.
- **Within 2 hours of Natchez:** Jackson, Vicksburg, Natchez itself, Baton Rouge — 15 venues. This is the day-trip ring.
- **The 300-cap ceiling matters.** The skill says we're not a festival act. 20 of 66 venues (M + L tiers) are above our target. Treat them as exceptions, not routine routing.

## How to read this

Capacity tiers (from the catalog schema):

| Tier | Range | BMT fit |
|---|---|---|
| **XS** | (≤100) | In our 50–300 sweet spot (low end) |
| **S** | (100–300) | In our 50–300 sweet spot — primary target |
| **M** | (300–700) | Above our 300-cap ceiling — exception only |
| **L** | (700+) | Over capacity — festival/major touring only |

Flags: ✓ = inside our 50–300 sweet spot. ⚠ = above 300 cap, booking exception.

## Caveats — known gaps in this data

Worth flagging up front so Sean doesn't over-trust the table:

1. **No "relationship status" field.** The database has a `confidence` column, but that's data-quality (how sure we are about the record), not "have we booked here." We need to add a `relationship` column with values like `booked` / `contacted` / `prospect` / `pass`. **Ask:** Chase, can you mark up the ones you've actually played or have a relationship at?
2. **Capacity numbers are mostly standing only.** Seated capacity is `UNKNOWN` across nearly every venue. For listening-room shows this matters — flag the rooms where the seated number changes the booking math.
3. **Production data is sparse.** House PA brand, stage dimensions, monitor configs — mostly `UNKNOWN`. We can't cost a backline-and-production-needs show off this database yet.
4. **The Big Muddy Inn is not in this list.** It's the home base, so it was excluded from "venues to scout." If Sean is going to use this as a reference doc, we should add the Inn as row 1 for completeness.
5. **Helena-West Helena (4 venues) isn't on the corridor mental map in the scouting skill.** It should be — at 4h from Natchez, it's a real circuit stop, not a side quest.
6. **No "Friends of the Arcade"-style sponsor data per venue.** That kind of cost-offset relationship is what made the Arcade math work. If other venues have similar friends-of orgs or local sponsors, we don't have them yet.
