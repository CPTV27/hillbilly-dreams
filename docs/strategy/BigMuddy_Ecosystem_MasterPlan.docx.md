# **3\. The Intercompany Contract — A2 Natchez → Newco**

*This contract is the structural centerpiece of the entire arrangement. Get this right and everything else follows.*

A2 Natchez LLC hires Newco to provide marketing, booking optimization, content production, touring development, and operational strategy services. The fee is variable: Newco guarantees the Inn breaks even every month, and in exchange keeps everything above that line.

## **3.1 How the Fee Works**

| *Surplus month: Inn revenue exceeds operating expenses. Newco invoices A2 Natchez for the full surplus. Inn ends at zero. Newco books the revenue and distributes to the three partners quarterly.* *Deficit month: Inn expenses exceed revenue. Newco advances funds to A2 Natchez to cover the gap, recorded as an intercompany loan at 0% interest. No Newco distribution that month.* *Loan repayment: In future surplus months, the intercompany loan is repaid in full before any surplus fee flows to Newco.* *Net result over time: The Inn always lands at break-even. Every dollar of margin improvement from strategy and execution flows to Newco. The three partners share it equally.* |
| :---- |

## **3.2 Break-Even Definition**

The break-even calculation must be precisely defined in the contract to prevent disputes. Recommended definition:

* INCLUDED in break-even expenses: mortgage or rent, utilities, staffing (including Tracy and Amy compensation — see Section 3.4), insurance, OTA and booking fees, maintenance and repairs, supplies, existing debt service

* EXCLUDED from break-even: capital expenditures above $5,000 per item (treated as separate LLC-level decisions requiring member vote); new debt taken on without member consent

* Measurement: prior month actuals, reconciled by the 15th of the following month

## **3.3 Services Contract — Key Terms**

| Term | Detail |
| :---- | :---- |
| Parties | A2 Natchez LLC (Client) and \[Newco\] (Agency) |
| Services delivered | Marketing strategy, OTA and booking management, content production (40 clips/month target), social media pipeline, Blues Room programming support, touring development, revenue optimization, operational consulting |
| Fee structure | Variable monthly fee \= A2 Natchez gross revenue minus A2 Natchez operating expenses (break-even). Fee may be zero or negative in deficit months. |
| Deficit coverage | If fee is negative, Newco advances the deficit amount to A2 Natchez as an intercompany loan at 0% interest, repaid from future surplus fees before any Newco distribution |
| Invoicing | Monthly, by the 15th of the following month based on prior month actuals |
| Documentation | Newco must document services delivered monthly: content clips produced, booking changes made, strategy sessions held, revenue results vs. prior month. Required for arm's-length compliance. |
