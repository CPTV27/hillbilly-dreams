# Feature Tiers — Product Roadmap
## Last Updated: March 26, 2026
## Source: Perplexity Deep Research + Internal Strategy

---

## CORE INSIGHT
"Your biggest competitor isn't QuickBooks or Mailchimp. Your biggest competitor is the owner's fatigue."

## FREE: The Trojan Horse (Integration Lock-In)
Solve the #1 pain point (manual data entry) so deeply they never unplug.

1. Universal POS-to-Accounting Sync (Square/Toast → QBO/Xero)
2. The "One Number" Daily SMS (yesterday's revenue, one text)
3. Basic Receipt Capture (snap a photo, log the expense)
4. Central Notification Hub (all alerts in one feed)
5. Directory listing on Deep South Directory

## $20/mo: The Doer (Marketing & Operations)
"Take a photo. It does the rest."

5. The Photo Content Engine (snap → caption → posts to IG/FB/Google)
6. 5-Number Daily SMS Briefing (metrics + one suggested action)
7. AI Review Auto-Responder (draft + post replies to Google/Yelp)
8. Basic AI CRM (auto-saves customer data from POS/bookings)
9. Email Newsletter Generator (photos + reviews → monthly email)
10. Competitor Watchlist (pricing changes, review surges)
11. Social Listening Alerts (SMS if mentioned on Reddit/Facebook)

## $99/mo: The Manager (Compliance & Decisions)
Transitions from handling chores to preventing disasters.

12. Automated Compliance Calendar via SMS (tax, licenses, inspections)
13. Decision Queue (SMS approve/decline on POs, refunds, shifts)
14. Voice-to-Action Bookkeeping ("Paid the plumber $200" → logged)
15. Smart Inbox Auto-Drafting (customer emails → 1-tap response)
16. AI Business Coach (voice idea → 1-page business plan/ROI)
17. Multi-Channel Menu/Inventory Sync (update once → everywhere)
18. Reputation Dispute Engine (flag and appeal fake reviews)
19. Smart Shift Scheduling (staffing gaps from calendar/POS data)

## $1,500/mo: The Operator (Full Outsourced OS)
For $500K+/yr businesses. Replaces back-office headcount entirely.

20. AI Cash Flow & Margin Forecasting (predictive SMS alerts)
21. Demand & Weather Prediction (foot traffic + staffing suggestions)
22. Automated AR/AP Chaser (polite escalating invoice follow-ups)
23. Dynamic Pricing Suggestions (margin shrinkage alerts)
24. Multi-Location Roll-up (unified SMS across 2-3 locations)
25. "Ask Me Anything" Data Query (text a question → real answer)

## THE ANTI-CHURN ARCHITECTURE

| Churn Reason | Industry Average | Our Solution |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Infrequent usage (#2, rising) | Users forget to log in | SMS-first — product is on their lock screen every morning |
| Manual data entry (#1 pain) | 37% still retyping data | Free POS sync eliminates this on day 1 |
| Too complex | Dashboard fatigue | No dashboard. Text + camera + voice |
| Doesn't show ROI | Users can't find the value | Daily text TELLS them the value |

## PRICING NOTE
Tiers: Free/$20/$99/$1,500/Custom. The $20 tier is the conversion point. The $1,500 tier replaces the old S2PX $5K/mo — still massive value at $18K/year vs Tyler's $25-75K. The free tier is the trojan horse.
