## Reviews + Reputation

### Q: Do you syndicate reviews to Google / Yelp or other platforms?

No, the Deep South Directory hosts its own independent review system. We do not syndicate reviews directly to or from platforms like Google, Yelp, or TripAdvisor.

*   **Why this approach?**
    *   **Focus on Local Community:** Our reviews reflect genuine feedback from customers engaging specifically within the Deep South Directory ecosystem, fostering a trusted local community resource.
    *   **Control & Quality:** We maintain full control over our review policies, moderation processes, and data, ensuring a high standard of quality and relevance for our users and businesses.
    *   **Avoid Third-Party Rules:** This allows us to avoid the complex and often restrictive rules, algorithms, and potential fees associated with third-party review platforms.

While we don't syndicate reviews, your Deep South Directory listing itself is indexed by search engines like Google. This means that when customers search for your business, your Deep South Directory profile—including your star rating and review count—can appear in search results, contributing to your overall online reputation.
