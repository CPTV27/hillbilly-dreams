## PRICE SENSITIVITY DATA

- Free → Paid conversion: low single digits to ~10%
- $10-20/mo: "no-brainer" range for single-purpose tools
- $50-100/mo: scrutiny starts, need to clearly replace multiple tools
- $100-300/mo: explicitly "too expensive" for point solutions
- Per-seat pricing: universally hated
