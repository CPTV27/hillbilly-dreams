# Google Workspace Reseller Analysis

This document outlines the viability of incorporating Google Workspace licensing directly into the $99 Deep South Directory (DSD) *Marketing* tier.

## Reseller Program Economics
Under the Google Cloud Partner Advantage Program, authorized resellers typically receive an average **20% margin/discount** on standard list pricing, depending on volume commitments and specific partner tracks.

*   **Standard Direct Pricing:** Google Workspace Business Starter is currently $7.20 / user / month.
*   **Estimated Reseller Cost:** ~$5.76 / user / month.
*   **Margin Yield:** ~$1.44 / user / month if sold at standalone parity, or absorbed as a customer acquisition cost (CAC) into a higher-tier bundle.

## DSD Tier Integration
**Can this be included in the $99 DSD tier?** 
Yes. The unit economics are highly favorable. Absorbing a $5.76 monthly cost within a $99 MRR tier leaves $93.24 in gross margin (before other software costs like local AI/hosting). 

### Frictional Value
By provisioning the seat, DSD becomes the customer's core utility provider. Features included for the end-user:
*   Custom domain business email (`owner@mainstreetbusiness.com`)
*   Google Calendar scheduling
*   Google Drive (30GB pooled storage)
*   Google Meet

### Strategic Recommendation
Rather than selling "discounted Workspace," DSD should pitch **"Digital Hygiene & Professional Comms"** as a single $99 line item. We provision the seat on our enterprise master account, issue their `@domain.com` alias, and immediately convert DSD from a "nice-to-have marketing expense" into "mission-critical infrastructure" they cannot churn without losing their email routing.
