# Google Workspace Reseller Economics

This document calculates the viability of MBT entering the Google Cloud Partner Advantage Program as a registered Workspace Reseller, effectively shifting DSD from an advertising platform into core IT infrastructure.

## Can MBT become a Google Workspace Reseller?
**Yes.** IT MSPs and digital agencies routinely enroll in the Google Cloud Partner Advantage program. The prerequisite is establishing a track record of tech-adjacent billing and committing to a baseline volume of user seats.

## Unit Economics
*   **Retail Rate:** Google Workspace Business Starter is sold retail for **$7.20 / user / month**.
*   **Reseller Rate:** Authorized partners typically command an automatic 20% margin. This drops our raw cost down to **~$5.76 / user / month**.
*   **Standalone Margin:** If we sold it pure retail at $7.20, we net $1.44 per seat. This is inefficient as a core business model.

## Bundling in the DSD $99 Tier
Can we bundle custom email (`owner@localbusiness.com`) into the $99 DSD Marketing Tier?

**Yes. And we must.** 
*   **The Math:** $99.00 MRR - $5.76 Workspace Host Cost = $93.24 Gross Margin.
*   **The Strategy:** Instead of making $1.44 markup on an email seat, we use the $5.76 expense to absolutely cement our $99/mo subscription. 

## Final Recommendation
**Highly Recommended.** 

A local business will quickly churn on a $99 "marketing fee" if they have a slow sales month. But a business **cannot** churn if cancelling the $99 tier means their primary business email stops receiving messages. By issuing their `@domain.com` workspace seat directly through DSD's master account, DSD ceases to be an ad expense; it becomes their mission-critical internet utility bill.
