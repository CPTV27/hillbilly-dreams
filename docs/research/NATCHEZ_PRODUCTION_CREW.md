# Natchez Production Crew — Local Talent Pipeline

## Goal

Build a roster of local production talent in the Natchez / Southwest Mississippi region for freelance work across HDI brands. Mississippi's film tax incentives bring productions to the state — between gigs, this crew works freelance.

---

## Roles Needed

### Photography
- **Real estate** — feeds Tuthill Design and DSD client onboarding
- **Event** — shows, art shows, openings, community events
- **Food / restaurant** — DSD merchant profiles, magazine features
- **Portrait / headshot** — artist profiles, team photos, directory listings

### Video / Film
- **Camera operators** — multi-cam shows, events, session recordings
- **Editors** — social clips, promo reels, session recordings, property tours
- **Drone operators** — property aerials, town footage, magazine B-roll
- **Gaffers / lighting** — show production, studio sessions

### Audio
- **Live sound engineers** — shows at the Inn, outdoor events
- **Recording engineers** — session work, podcast production
- **Podcast production** — Big Muddy Radio content

## Where to Find Them

### Film Industry Pipeline
- **Mississippi Film Office** — maintains a crew database, searchable by role and region
- **Louisiana Film Commission** — New Orleans crew who travel for work
- **Local Facebook groups** — MS/LA film crew networking groups
- **Crew agencies:** Staff Me Up, ProductionHub, Mandy.com

### Local Networks
- **Alcorn State University** — mass communications / media production program
- **Copiah-Lincoln Community College** — media arts
- **Natchez Little Theatre** — local production community, tech crew
- **Mississippi Public Broadcasting** — freelancers who work between state productions
- **Vicksburg / Jackson freelancers** — within driving distance

### Music Scene
- **Local bands and sound engineers** — the touring shows will surface these naturally
- **Church audio teams** — experienced live sound operators
- **Studio owners** — home studio operators who do session work

---

## Engagement Model

### Tier 1: Per-Gig Freelance
- Show up, do the job, get paid
- Standard day rates: $300-500/day photographer, $400-800/day video, $200-400/day audio
- Best for: event coverage, one-off shoots

### Tier 2: Retainer / Recurring
- Monthly retainer for guaranteed availability
- 2-4 gigs per month at reduced per-gig rate
- Best for: DSD onboarding photography, regular show coverage

### Tier 3: Partnership
- Revenue share on specific brands (like Studio C model)
- Equipment provided or subsidized
- Best for: key creative roles, video editor, lead photographer
