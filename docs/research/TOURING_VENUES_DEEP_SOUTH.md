## Operating checklist (every venue)

1. **Capacity & egress** — posted max vs. comfortable sell.
2. **PA / lights** — house system spec, tie lines, power.
3. **Load-in / parking** — Sprinter + trailer rules (Big Muddy Touring fleet).
4. **Settlement** — guarantee vs. door, merch split, hospitality.
5. **Promo stack** — Radio + Magazine + Deep South Directory listing for partner businesses on the route.
