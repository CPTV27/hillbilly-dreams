# NOLA Session Musicians & Logistics
**Client:** Amy's Live at Five (May 8)  
**Genre:** Country / Americana  

## Talent Sourcing Options (New Orleans)
While NOLA is historically rooted in jazz and brass, there is a dedicated Americana/Honky-Tonk subcircuit operating out of venues like d.b.a. and BJ's Lounge in the Bywater. 
*   **Booking Platforms:** SoundBetter and GigSalad maintain rosters of dedicated NOLA studio session players available for regional hire.
*   **Pickup Groups:** Establishing a relationship with local Americana fixtures (e.g., Gal Holiday and the Honky Tonk Revue or players from The Deslondes ecosystem) acts as an efficient shortcut to assembling a cohesive backing band rather than hiring siloed session players.

## Rehearsal Facilities
*   **Fountainbleau Rehearsal Studios:** A primary staple for touring and local bands requiring lockout rooms.
*   **The Music Shed:** A premium option with live-room rentals suited for larger ensembles.
*   *Requirement:* Secure a lockout for 2 full days prior to the Natchez load-in.

## Budget Considerations
A conservative operational baseline to hire a standard 4-piece backing group (drums, bass, electric guitar, pedal steel/keys):

*   **Band Fees:** $150 – $300 per musician / per day ($600 – $1,200 total per day).
*   **Travel (NOLA → Natchez):** ~3 hours driving distance. Mileage/gas stipends should be calculated for 2-3 standard vehicles.
*   **Lodging:** 1-2 nights in Natchez. A block of hotel rooms averages $150/night per room.
*   **Rehearsal Spaces:** NOLA spaces average $30 – $50/hour for fully equipped backline rooms.

## Schedule Blueprint
*   **NOLA Rehearsal 1:** Introductions, charting out the setlist.
*   **NOLA Rehearsal 2:** Full set run-throughs, dynamic marking.
*   **Travel Day / Natchez Rehearsal 3:** Load-in to venue, tech rehearsal, monitors/FOH check.
