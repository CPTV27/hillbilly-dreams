## 3. Adams County (Tourism + Project Layer)

### Structural Reality (Confirmed)
**Adams County Tourism does NOT exist as a separate public-facing organization.** Tourism for Natchez/Adams County is handled through NCPC/Visit Natchez. The Mississippi Tourism Association's Adams County impact sheet directs tourism readers to visitnatchez.org. The Adams County official website does NOT list a tourism department, tourism board, or county DMO equivalent.

**Tax history explains the ambiguity:** Before April 16, 1998, Natchez convention and tourism taxes applied to businesses in Adams County; after that date, the tax language shifted toward businesses doing business in Natchez. NCPC still uses Natchez/Adams County promotional language, but its commission is appointed by the City of Natchez, NOT by the Adams County Board of Supervisors.
