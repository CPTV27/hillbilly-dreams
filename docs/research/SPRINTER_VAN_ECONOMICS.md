# The Big Muddy Touring Sprinter: Buy / Lease / Rent Matrix

To standardize transportation for primary label acts and execute the Big Muddy Route consistently, we must determine the capital structure for securing a Mercedes Sprinter (or equivalent transit van).

## 1. Capital Allocation: Buy vs. Lease vs. Rent

### The Rental Route (Agnostic)
*   **Cost:** ~$3,000 to $7,500/month (varies wildly based on "splitter" configuration).
*   **Pros:** Zero maintenance liability. Predictable per-tour expense.
*   **Cons:** Highest annualized bleed ($36K+ if touring year-round). Strict mileage caps trigger brutal overage fees. Prohibits custom exterior branding.

### The Lease Route (Strategic)
*   **Cost:** ~$1,200 – $1,800/month (Commercial Lease).
*   **Pros:** Moderate monthly burn. Keeps capital fluid. Newer vehicle limits breakdown risks off-grid.
*   **Cons:** Commercial leases still enforce mileage caps. Requires tier-1 corporate credit.

### The Purchase Route (Equity)
*   **Cost:** ~$50,000 to $80,000 (Used/Customized) or $90,000+ (New).
*   **Pros:** Unlimited miles. Fully depreciable asset against Big Muddy Touring LLC taxes (the entity that holds the van — separate from MBT for road and vehicle liability). Absolute brand control (full exterior vinyl wraps).
*   **Cons:** We assume 100% of the mechanical liability and require $10K+ upfront cash.

## 2. Operating Overhead (Per Tour Estimates)
*   **Commercial Insurance:** ~$1,500 - $2,500 annually.
*   **Fuel:** Diesel (approx. 18-20 MPG). A standard Natchez → Memphis loop runs ~$150 in fuel.
*   **Routine Maintenance:** Oil changes every 10k miles. Tire rotation. Budget $150/month in reserve.

## 3. The "Rolling Billboard" / Revenue Generation
*   **Brand Placement:** An owned or leased van allows for a full 3M vinyl exterior wrap. The van becomes a mobile billboard crossing the Deep South, heavily brandishing *Big Muddy Records, Deep South Directory, and Big Muddy Touring*.
*   **Sub-Leasing:** When independent label acts are *not* on the road, the van sits idle. By carrying proper commercial fleet insurance, Big Muddy Touring LLC can rent the Sprinter out to external regional bands via platforms like Sharegrid or Outdoorsy (for minimal configurations), potentially offsetting the monthly payment entirely.

## 4. Alternative Comparison: Flights + Local Rentals
*   **The Fly-In Model:** Flying a 4-piece band + gear costs roughly $1,200 in basic airfare, plus $1,500 for a week-long SUV/Minivan rental at the destination, plus backline gear rentals ($500). Total: ~$3,200 per run.
*   **Conclusion:** Flying is completely economically inviable for the 200-500 capacity club circuit. You must own the road.

## The Official Recommendation
**Commercial Purchase (Used) via Big Muddy Touring LLC.**
Execute a commercial loan for a gently used, pre-configured Sprinter (2018-2021 model year). Wrap it entirely in Big Muddy branding. Control the asset, eat the depreciation, and sub-lease it to trusted network bands during offline periods to neutralize the monthly note.
