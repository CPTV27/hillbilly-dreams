# Natchez Downtown HQ Property Research

This document outlines the real estate viability for establishing a consolidated Big Muddy / MBT headquarters in downtown Natchez, MS.

**Governance:** Tracy and Amy are **equity partners** in Inn and related operating entities — any live/work or retail lease structure should be reviewed with them as partners, not as a landlord–employee arrangement.

## Ideal Property Parameters
The operational thesis requires a mixed-use/multi-level footprint encompassing:
1. **Gallery/Front-of-House:** Street-level retail or gallery space for public-facing engagement and art exhibitions.
2. **Office/Studio:** Secondary rooms for dedicated recording (e.g. podcasts), post-production, and administrative desks.
3. **Residential/Apartment:** Upper-floor living quarters to minimize cost-of-living overhead and house visiting talent.

## Paul Green Real Estate Availability
Paul Green Real Estate maintains a prominent share of the commercial inventory in the historic downtown overlay.
*   **433 Main Street (Example Template):** A two-story historical structure explicitly mapped for our exact use case—commercial/retail footprint on the bottom floor, with renovated residential units on the second story.
*   **104 S. Wall Street & Core Historic District:** Office space clustering near the courthouse. Properties in the immediate walkable radius of Main, Pearl, and Commerce streets are prime targets.

## Sourcing Strategies
*   **Downtown Doorway Portal:** The Downtown Natchez Alliance facilitates the "Downtown Doorway" program, which matches businesses with available multi-use storefronts and occasionally aggregates investment/renovation incentives.
*   **Direct Broker Engagement:** Establish contact directly with Paul Green Real Estate (301 Highway 61 S / 601-442-2768) to surface unlisted or upcoming mixed-use downtown properties.

## Estimated Lease Budget Range
Compared to coastal hubs or even the Northeast/Bearsville market, Natchez commercial leases remain highly efficient:
*   **Average Footprint (1,500 – 2,500 sq ft):** Typically bounded between $1,200 to $2,500 per month, heavily dependent on the condition of the upper-floor residential renovations and immediate street frontage.
*   **Note:** Structuring a live/work lease effectively eliminates a standalone residential rent expense, deeply subsidizing the commercial footprint.
