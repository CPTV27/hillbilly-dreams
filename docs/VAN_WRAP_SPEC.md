## The Concept

The van is the touring vehicle for Big Muddy Touring — a music-hospitality company based in Natchez, Mississippi. It carries musicians, equipment, and a portable PA system to shows across the Mississippi corridor. The wrap should feel premium, understated, and road-ready. Think tour bus energy at Sprinter scale.

**Mood:** Matte black with gold/brass lettering. Not flashy — confident. A touring vehicle, not an advertisement. The kind of van that makes people ask "who's that?"

---

## Wrap Design

### Base
- **Full matte black wrap** — the entire vehicle, bumper to bumper
- Satin or matte finish, not gloss

### Primary Branding — Both Sides
- **"BIG MUDDY TOURING"** in gold/brass metallic vinyl
- Font: Clean serif, medium weight (think Playfair Display or similar editorial serif)
- Size: Large enough to read from across a parking lot (~8-10" letter height)
- Placement: Centered on the flat panel area of each side, between the wheel wells
- Color: Metallic gold/brass (#c8943e approximate — warm gold, not yellow, not chrome)

### Rear
- **"BIG MUDDY"** centered on the rear doors
- Smaller than side lettering (~6" letter height)
- Same gold/brass metallic
- Below it, smaller: **"bigmuddytouring.com"** in the same gold, ~2" height
- Optional: small gold accent line or rule between the two

### Front / Hood
- **No branding on the front.** Keep it clean.

### Accent Details (Optional)
- Thin gold pinstripe along the belt line (the horizontal body line) — both sides
- If budget allows: small gold "BMT" monogram on the driver and passenger doors, near the handle (~3" circle or square)

### What NOT To Do
- No phone numbers
- No "call us for booking" text
- No QR codes
- No full-color graphics or photos
- No glossy finish
- No chrome — matte/satin metallic only

## Notes for the Shop

- This van will be photographed constantly — it's both a vehicle and a marketing asset
- The gold lettering is the only color on the entire van. The contrast is the whole point.
- If the gold metallic vinyl comes in different sheens, go warmer and more matte — not shiny chrome gold
- The van will be driven on highways and back roads across Mississippi, Louisiana, Tennessee, and up to New York. The wrap needs to be road-durable.
