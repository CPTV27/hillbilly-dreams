# Utopia — Brand Identity Guidelines

**Spec version:** 2026-04-21
**Brand owner:** Utopia Studios (Bearsville, New York — independent client of Chase Pierson Photography, Studio C, and Tuthill Design)
**Voice context:** No standalone voice doc yet — pull from `.claude/agents/BEARSVILLE_STUDIO_AGENT.md` and the Bearsville pivot memory
**Designer:** Chase Pierson (in-house visual brief for the book project — executed in Figma + Canva Team; Utopia's own team authors the ultimate Utopia identity if and when they choose to refresh it)
**Template reference:** Rosemary Hallmark Creative / The Big Muddy Inn + Blues Room, March 2023

> **Positioning premise:** Utopia is the **historic recording studio at Bearsville** — the room where Todd Rundgren built his own cathedral, where The Band recorded, where a specific kind of American music history lives. This guideline is the **Chase-authored design brief for the Utopia photography book project** — it establishes how Utopia should be presented inside Chase's upcoming monograph and in any Chase/Studio C/Tuthill collateral that references it.
>
> **Important boundary:** Utopia is **not** an HDI/MBT property. It is a client. This spec captures the visual direction Chase and the Bearsville Studio Agent use when producing Utopia-related content. The final in-house brand identity for Utopia belongs to Utopia itself; this document informs our side of the collaboration.

---

## 1. Cover concept

- Background: **Tape Oxide** (see §5), full bleed — a warm, slightly desaturated deep brown like the back of a 2" reel of tape.
- Primary mark: `UTOPIA` set in a high-contrast display serif (Canela or Butler), Brass Highlight, centered upper-third, letter-spaced loose.
- Below: `STUDIOS · BEARSVILLE · EST. 1969` in a geometric small-caps sans (Söhne or Graphik), Cream Analog, letter-spacing wide.
- A single horizontal hairline rule between `UTOPIA` and the subline, Brass Highlight, 1pt.
- Optional motif: the outline of the Bearsville main building (a classic modernist wooden studio exterior) in fine Brass Highlight line weight, lower right, subtle.
- Bottom-left: `VISUAL GUIDELINES FOR THE BEARSVILLE STUDIOS BOOK PROJECT`
- Bottom-right: `BY CHASE PIERSON · HILLBILLY DREAMS INC.`

## 3. Primary logo — the Utopia Wordmark

Inspired by the 1970s Bearsville/Utopia visual legacy (Todd Rundgren-era sleeve design, warm analog tones, precise mid-century display serifs) without copying it:

- `UTOPIA` set in Canela Medium (or Butler Regular) all caps, Brass Highlight, letter-spaced loose (tracking 120/1000).
- A 1pt horizontal rule under the wordmark in Brass Highlight, spanning the full width.
- Below the rule: `STUDIOS` in Söhne small caps, letter-spaced wide (tracking 300/1000), Cream Analog.
- The whole lockup is balanced, symmetrical, printed-in-a-book-in-1973.

## 4. Secondary logo — the Arch Mark

- A simple **half-round arch silhouette** (representing the studio's main live-room ceiling, or a theater proscenium), with `UTOPIA` inset in Canela small, centered horizontally.
- Used for: monograph chapter dividers, print-portfolio section headers, sticker + letterpress merch, the cover of Chase's book's "Utopia" chapter.
- Color treatments: Brass Highlight on Tape Oxide (primary), Cream Analog on Forest Green (alternate), embossed blind on cream print stock (luxury editions).

## 5. Brand colors

| Role | Name | Pantone | Hex | CMYK | RGB |
|------|------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Primary dark | **Tape Oxide** | 497 C | `#4A2C1E` | 40 / 70 / 80 / 50 | 74 / 44 / 30 |
| Secondary dark | **Forest Green** | 5535 C | `#1F3A2B` | 80 / 50 / 75 / 50 | 31 / 58 / 43 |
| Primary accent | **Brass Highlight** | 872 C metallic (or 871 C as non-metallic) | `#A08242` | 30 / 45 / 85 / 30 | 160 / 130 / 66 |
| Primary light | **Cream Analog** | 11-0907 TPG | `#F3EFE6` | 5 / 6 / 14 / 0 | 243 / 239 / 230 |
| Rare accent | **Analog Orange** | 7578 C | `#D96A3B` | 10 / 70 / 85 / 5 | 217 / 106 / 59 |

Rationale: The palette is the **interior of a 1970s tape machine room**. Tape Oxide is the color of the oxide coating on a reel of 2" tape. Forest Green is the color of the landscape you see out the studio windows onto the Catskills. Brass Highlight is the metal of the patch bay, the capstan, the VU meter bezel. Cream Analog is the inner sleeve of a gatefold record. Analog Orange is the rare warm pop — used only for the peak LED indicators in editorial layouts, or the first letter of a pull quote.

Proportions in a typical book spread: 45% Cream Analog (body ground), 30% Tape Oxide (chapter openers + hero), 15% Brass Highlight (display type + rules), 8% Forest Green (chapter pages referencing the landscape), 2% Analog Orange (accent only).

## 6. Incorrect usage

- Do **not** modernize the identity toward current "creator" or "startup" aesthetic (no gradients, no neon, no Material 3).
- Do **not** use photos of the studio that don't meet Chase's technical standards (gallery-ready or don't use them).
- Do **not** use Todd Rundgren imagery, album art, or quotes without explicit permission from the relevant rights holders — this is a **physical place** guideline, not an artist guideline.
- Do **not** combine Utopia colors with Studio C colors on a piece that presents Utopia as part of the Studio C brand — Utopia is Utopia; Studio C is production services hired by Utopia.
- Do **not** use script fonts. The 1970s warmth is held by the serif and the tone, not by handwriting.
- Do **not** treat Utopia as an HDI property in any published collateral — it's a client relationship, not an owned brand.

## 7. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Usage |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Display (wordmark) | **Canela** (or Butler) | Medium / Regular | Wordmark + chapter titles only |
| Headline | **Canela** | Light / Regular | Feature titles, book section heads |
| Subhead | **Söhne** | Regular / Semibold | Decks, section labels, page folios |
| Body | **Söhne** | Buch / Regular | Book body text, feature copy, captions |
| Caption / metadata | **Söhne Mono** | Regular | Photo credits, file names, gear lists, recording session notes |
| Italic / emphasis | **Canela Italic** | Regular | Pull quotes + inline emphasis |

## 8. Submark + studio marks

1. **Arch mark** (see §4).
2. **Session mark:** a small 16×16mm tile with `SESSION [MMM YYYY]` in Söhne Mono — stamped on the first page of any session log, analogous to a track sheet header.
3. **Gear mark:** an inline icon set (Söhne Mono hairline outlines) for the studio's signature equipment — Trident console, Studer tape machine, Neumann mic — used on gear-detail captions in the book.
4. **Artist appearance mark:** when an artist's session is covered in an article, the lockup `UTOPIA × [ARTIST NAME]` in Canela Medium small caps, Brass Highlight, with the artist's name italicized.

## 9. Patterns

- **Pattern A — Tape Texture:** a very fine granular texture on Tape Oxide (0–4% Forest Green noise), used as the ground on chapter openers so the paper feels like tape-box lining.
- **Pattern B — Patch Grid:** a precise 5mm grid of Brass Highlight dots on Tape Oxide, echoing a patch bay — used behind gear-detail pages in the book.
- **Pattern C — Waveform Rule:** a single horizontal Brass Highlight rule that gently rises and falls like a tape-recorded waveform, used as a section divider in long-form features.

## 11. Application examples (sketches for the finished PDF)

1. **Monograph opening spread (book interior, 10×12 landscape):** left page is a full-bleed Chase Pierson photograph of the main live room, untinted. Right page is Tape Oxide with a Brass Highlight Waveform Rule across the top, the chapter wordmark `UTOPIA` in Canela centered, `CHAPTER IV — THE BEARSVILLE YEARS` in Söhne Mono underneath, a short Canela Italic epigraph at page bottom.
2. **Photo caption system:** two-line caption under every photo — line one in Söhne Buch Italic (descriptive), line two in Söhne Mono Regular (technical: date, lens, film stock or digital capture, session reference). Captions sit in the 30mm margin, never overlay the photo.
3. **Print portfolio page (for Chase's gallery show):** Tape Oxide mat, photograph centered, `UTOPIA · [PHOTO TITLE] · [YEAR]` in Söhne Mono 10pt Cream Analog at the foot, Chase Pierson's signature and edition number lower-right in Brass Highlight.
4. **Editorial feature in Big Muddy Magazine:** maintains Magazine's typography (Abril Fatface for the feature title) but the feature-opener color is **Tape Oxide** instead of the Magazine's usual Indigo Deep — signaling "this is a Utopia feature" without breaking the Magazine's identity. The Waveform Rule appears as a section divider inside the feature.
5. **Book dust jacket:** Tape Oxide ground, single full-bleed photograph (untinted) on the front, Utopia wordmark top-center with the author's name `CHASE PIERSON` centered below in Söhne small caps, Brass-foiled on matte paper. Spine reads `UTOPIA · BEARSVILLE · PIERSON`.
6. **Invitation to the Utopia team (the cold-open email referenced in `docs/partners/utopia-studios/01-invitation-email.md`):** minimal — Cream Analog letterhead with the Utopia wordmark top, body in Söhne Buch, Chase Pierson Photography secondary mark at the foot (this is a Chase→Utopia outreach, so CPP signs it; Utopia mark is used with respect as the subject, not as the letterhead owner).
