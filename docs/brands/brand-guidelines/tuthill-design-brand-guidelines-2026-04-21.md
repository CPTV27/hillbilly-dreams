# Tuthill Design — Brand Identity Guidelines

**Spec version:** 2026-04-21
**Brand owner:** Tuthill Design LLC (Miles Dubois — operator)
**Voice doc:** `docs/voice/tuthill-design.md`
**Intended designer:** Miles Dubois (in-house — this is his own house)
**Template reference:** Rosemary Hallmark Creative / The Big Muddy Inn + Blues Room, March 2023

> **Positioning premise from the voice doc:** *"Efficient, transparent, empathetic, competent, direct. Tuthill Design is the reliable, efficient partner for real estate media and design."* Tuthill is the **Northern-hemisphere anchor brand** — based in Bearsville / Hudson Valley, serving Woodstock-area real estate professionals and extending into the Big Muddy ecosystem as the design + photography partner for the corridor.

---

## 1. Cover concept

- Background: **Bluestone Navy** (see §5), full bleed.
- Primary mark: `TUTHILL` in a geometric sans (Graphik Semibold or similar), Sage Mist, large, centered upper-third.
- `DESIGN` directly below, letter-spaced wide, Sage Mist, same weight but smaller (60% of the `TUTHILL` size).
- Thin Brick Coral hairline rule between `TUTHILL` and `DESIGN`.
- Bottom-left: `BRAND IDENTITY GUIDELINES`
- Bottom-right: `BY MILES DUBOIS · TUTHILL DESIGN`
- Optional motif: a clean architectural floor-plan line drawing (single-weight Sage Mist) in the lower-right quadrant, subtle, technical, professional.

## 3. Primary logo

A two-line lockup with a technical, drafted feel:

- **Top tier:** `TUTHILL` in Graphik Semibold, Bluestone Navy (default) or Sage Mist (on dark), all caps, tight letter-spacing (tracking 40/1000).
- **Bottom tier:** `DESIGN` in the same typeface but 60% of the top-tier height, all caps, wider letter-spacing (tracking 250/1000).
- **Hairline rule** between the two tiers, 0.5pt, Brick Coral — the single spark of warmth in an otherwise cool, precise system.
- Generous padding at minimum twice the cap-height of `DESIGN`.

## 4. Secondary logo — the TD Monogram

- `TD` in Graphik Semibold, set inside a **4:3 rounded-rectangle frame** (2mm corner radius), Bluestone Navy outline, Sage Mist fill.
- Echoes a real-estate for-sale sign or property-spec sheet.
- Used for: watermarks on photographic deliverables, dashboard favicon, stationery letterhead corner mark, property-signature stamp.
- Color treatments: Bluestone Navy on Sage Mist (primary), Sage Mist on Bluestone Navy (inverse), Bluestone Navy on cream (print-only).

## 5. Brand colors

| Role | Name | Pantone | Hex | CMYK | RGB |
|------|------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Primary dark | **Bluestone Navy** | 2955 C | `#0F3B5F` | 100 / 75 / 35 / 30 | 15 / 59 / 95 |
| Primary light | **Sage Mist** | 5527 C | `#B7C5B8` | 30 / 10 / 30 / 5 | 183 / 197 / 184 |
| Body background | **Drafting Cream** | 11-0907 TPG | `#F3EFE6` | 5 / 6 / 14 / 0 | 243 / 239 / 230 |
| Accent (signature) | **Brick Coral** | 7579 C | `#D2553C` | 15 / 80 / 80 / 5 | 210 / 85 / 60 |
| Utility black | **Drafting Black** | Neutral Black 6 C | `#1A1D1F` | 80 / 70 / 60 / 75 | 26 / 29 / 31 |

Rationale: Tuthill Design is **Hudson Valley architectural restraint.** Bluestone Navy is the color of the bluestone quarries around Woodstock. Sage Mist is the color of hemlock and lichen on the Catskills granite. Drafting Cream is vellum. The whole palette says "quiet, expensive, competent" — a real estate professional looks at it and thinks "my listing will look expensive in their hands." Brick Coral is the one warm note, used as the spark that makes the system human instead of sterile.

Proportions: 40% Drafting Cream (body ground), 30% Bluestone Navy (display + marks), 20% Sage Mist (accent shapes, panels), 5% Drafting Black (body type), 5% Brick Coral (highlights + hyperlinks + rule lines only).

## 6. Incorrect usage

- Do **not** swap Brick Coral for a brighter red or orange — the muted terracotta is intentional.
- Do **not** set the `DESIGN` tier at the same height as `TUTHILL` — the hierarchy is part of the mark.
- Do **not** use Sage Mist as a background for body copy below 14pt — contrast fails.
- Do **not** use stock real-estate photography. Tuthill's photography is Chase's (for Hudson Valley) or Tuthill-commissioned originals (for other markets) — always real.
- Do **not** use scripty or decorative fonts — this is a technical, drafted identity.
- Do **not** combine Tuthill Design colors with Studio C colors on the same deliverable unless the piece is explicitly a **Tuthill × Studio C** co-brand.
- Do **not** describe services with phrases like "unlock potential" or "breathtaking" — voice rules override design.

## 7. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Usage |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Display | **Graphik** | Semibold | Wordmark + primary headlines |
| Headline | **Graphik** | Medium | Section headlines on proposals + web |
| Subhead | **Graphik** | Regular | Subsections, deck copy |
| Body | **Graphik** | Regular / Medium | Web body, proposal body, email body |
| Data / spec sheets | **Graphik Mono** (or IBM Plex Mono as substitute) | Regular | Floor-plan annotations, file lists, EXIF/tech callouts |

A single family (Graphik) carries the whole system. No serifs. No scripts. No exceptions.

## 8. Submark + service marks

1. **TD monogram** (see §4).
2. **Service-tier badges:** three stacked small rounded-rectangle badges labeled `ESSENTIALS · PRO · PREMIUM` in Graphik Mono small caps, Sage Mist on Bluestone Navy, used on the services page and in client proposals.
3. **Deliverables mark:** a small Graphik Mono label `TD · [PROPERTY ID] · [DATE]` used as a watermark on every photographic deliverable.
4. **Partner mark:** a horizontal lockup `TUTHILL DESIGN × STUDIO C` or `TUTHILL DESIGN · BIG MUDDY` used when co-branding — the partner's mark sits at the same cap-height on the right, separated by a `×` or `·`.

## 9. Patterns

- **Pattern A — Grid Sheet:** a faint 8mm × 8mm grid on Drafting Cream (0–3% Drafting Black), behind proposal body pages, referencing architectural drafting paper.
- **Pattern B — Contour Lines:** a topographic line pattern in Sage Mist on Bluestone Navy, used behind deliverables-page title spreads. References the Hudson Valley terrain.
- **Pattern C — Plan Line:** a single Brick Coral horizontal rule (1pt) used as a section divider throughout proposals and web pages.

## 11. Application examples (sketches for the finished PDF)

1. **Real estate media deliverable (client-facing gallery page):** Bluestone Navy header bar with Tuthill Design wordmark, grid of photographic thumbnails on Drafting Cream, property address in Graphik Semibold, spec sheet in Graphik Mono, download button in Brick Coral.
2. **Website home (tuthilldesign.com):** Drafting Cream ground, wordmark top-left, Graphik Semibold headline `REAL ESTATE MEDIA FOR THE HUDSON VALLEY AND BEYOND`, hero photo 60% page height, three service tier badges in a row, client logo ribbon at foot.
3. **Business card (2×3.5):** Bluestone Navy face with TD monogram top-center in Sage Mist, reverse is Drafting Cream with name in Graphik Semibold, role in Graphik Mono, phone + email + tuthilldesign.com in Graphik Regular.
4. **Letterhead (8.5×11):** Drafting Cream, wordmark top-left, TD monogram top-right, Brick Coral plan-line footer rule with address/phone/URL in Graphik Mono.
5. **Partner co-brand (deliverable handoff for a Studio C-produced real estate video):** Graphik Semibold title, `TUTHILL DESIGN × STUDIO C` partner mark as sign-off, pattern B Contour Lines behind the opener.
6. **Client dashboard (web app):** Bluestone Navy sidebar, Drafting Cream content area, TD monogram top-left corner, section headers in Graphik Semibold, active-state buttons in Brick Coral.
