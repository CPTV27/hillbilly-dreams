## 3. Primary logo

- **Top tier:** "The Big Muddy" in Nathan script (family-consistent with Inn/Touring).
- **Bottom tier:** `RADIO` in Citrus Gothic Black, wide letter-spacing, cream.
- **Signature element:** a small stylized **concentric arc** (three nested arcs radiating upward from the left edge of `RADIO`) rendered in **Antique Gold** — the "signal" mark. This is Radio's signature visual differentiator from Touring.
- Stack the entire lockup; never set the tiers on one line.

## 4. Secondary logo — the Tower mark

- A minimalist **broadcast tower silhouette** (three triangular sections narrowing upward) with the concentric arcs radiating from the tip.
- Set inside a **circular badge** bounded by the phrase `BIG MUDDY RADIO · NATCHEZ MS ·` in small caps.
- Used for: web favicon, podcast-platform artwork, station swag, sticker sheet.
- Color treatments: Antique Gold on Transistor Maroon (primary), cream on Transistor Maroon, black on cream (print-only).

## 5. Brand colors

| Role | Name | Pantone | Hex | CMYK | RGB |
|------|------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Primary dark | **Transistor Maroon** | 504 C | `#4A1E2C` | 35 / 85 / 55 / 55 | 74 / 30 / 44 |
| Primary light | **Cloud Dancer** | 11-4201 TPG | `#F1F0EC` | 4 / 3 / 6 / 0 | 241 / 240 / 236 |
| Accent (signature) | **Antique Gold** | 871 C | `#8C7B4E` | 40 / 45 / 75 / 25 | 140 / 123 / 78 |
| Warm glow | **Tube Amber** | 1375 C | `#F39426` | 0 / 50 / 95 / 0 | 243 / 148 / 38 |
| Family tie-in | **Natchez Magenta** | 682 C (inherited) | `#994878` | 35 / 85 / 30 / 15 | 153 / 72 / 120 |

Rationale: Radio lives in the moment just after the tube warms up — **Transistor Maroon** (the fabric of a 1950s console radio grille) is the ground. **Antique Gold** is the brass trim and the signal. **Tube Amber** is the glow when the station's live, used sparingly as a "ON AIR" indicator. Natchez Magenta keeps the station tied to the Blues Room for cross-promotion moments.

Proportions: 55% Transistor Maroon, 25% Cloud Dancer, 10% Antique Gold, 5% Tube Amber (on-air only), 5% Natchez Magenta (cross-brand only).

## 6. Incorrect usage

- Do **not** use bright FM-radio-station-style red-white-and-blue — we are not a commercial top-40 station.
- Do **not** show the tower mark without the signal arcs (they are the mark's soul).
- Do **not** place Tube Amber near a large block of Transistor Maroon without a Cloud Dancer buffer — contrast fails.
- Do **not** use any sans-serif other than Citrus Gothic for the `RADIO` subhead.
- Do **not** let the station drift into "Delta Blues nostalgia cliché" — no sepia overlays, no whiskey-glass photos, no stereotyped imagery. Real Chase Pierson photography only.
- Do **not** use copyrighted album art on social posts without label clearance.

## 7. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Usage |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Display (wordmark) | **Nathan** | Regular | Wordmark only |
| Headline | **Citrus Gothic** | Black / Bold | `RADIO`, show titles, daypart labels |
| Subhead / feature | **Abril Fatface** | Regular | Long-form editorial (when Radio publishes a companion article) |
| Body | **Montserrat** | Regular / Medium | Now-playing strip, program guide, web copy |
| Data / time | **GT America Mono** (or similar mono) | Regular | Show times, track metadata, timestamps |

## 8. Submark + brand marks

1. **Tower badge** (see §4) — the workhorse mark.
2. **On-air mark:** a Tube Amber dot + `ON AIR` in Citrus Gothic small caps, used only when the station is actively broadcasting live content.
3. **Call mark:** `BM · RADIO · 24/7` in a single line of small caps on Transistor Maroon — faux call letters since we're streaming, not licensed.
4. **Playlist card:** a standardized template for the "What's on now" card that goes everywhere — web player, social, Instagram stories — consisting of the Tower badge, track title in Abril Fatface, artist in Montserrat Medium, timestamp in GT America Mono.

## 9. Patterns

- **Pattern A — Signal Rays:** the concentric arc signal element repeated in a staggered grid on a Transistor Maroon ground. Used behind station-swag prints and the "What's on the Radio" section of the Magazine.
- **Pattern B — Dial Bar:** a horizontal Antique Gold bar with tick marks in Cloud Dancer, mimicking an FM tuner dial, used as a footer decoration on program guides.

## 11. Application examples (sketches for the finished PDF)

1. **Web player (bigmuddyradio.com):** Transistor Maroon top bar, wordmark on left, Tower badge on right, central "now playing" card, Antique Gold play button, Tube Amber ON AIR indicator when live.
2. **Show promo graphic (Instagram 1080×1080):** Pattern A Signal Rays ground, host's portrait cropped circular center, show title in Abril Fatface, day/time in GT America Mono, Tower badge lower right.
3. **Studio swag (tote + sticker):** Tower badge alone, Antique Gold on cream (tote) and cream on Transistor Maroon (sticker).
4. **Podcast cover art (3000×3000):** Pattern A ground, show title in Abril Fatface centered, Tower badge bottom, `BIG MUDDY RADIO` in Citrus Gothic small caps as footer lockup.
5. **Daypart program guide (letter):** Cloud Dancer ground, wordmark top, Dial Bar footer, program slots as a grid in Montserrat with show titles in Abril Fatface, Tube Amber marks on live-broadcast slots.
6. **Email newsletter header:** 600px wide Transistor Maroon bar, wordmark left, Tower badge right, Antique Gold hairline rule below.
