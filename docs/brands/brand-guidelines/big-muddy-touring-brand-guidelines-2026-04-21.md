## 1. Cover concept

- Background: **Asphalt Black**, full bleed.
- Primary mark: "The Big Muddy" in Nathan-style script, Cloud Dancer cream, top-center, slightly smaller than the Inn's wordmark.
- Directly below, `TOURING` set in a heavy condensed sans (Citrus Gothic Black or similar), letter-spaced wide, cream, acting as a banner.
- Hairline horizontal rule in **Highway Sodium** (see §5) under the `TOURING` lockup.
- Bottom-left: `BRAND IDENTITY GUIDELINES`
- Bottom-right: `BY HILLBILLY DREAMS INC.`
- Optional motif: a silhouette of a Sprinter van in Highway Sodium lower left, small, hinting at the road.

## 3. Primary logo

A two-tier lockup that mirrors the Inn's structure so the family reads as family:

- **Top tier:** "The Big Muddy" in Nathan script, identical proportions to the Inn wordmark.
- **Bottom tier:** `TOURING` in **Citrus Gothic Black**, letter-spacing 200/1000, cream on Asphalt Black (primary) or Asphalt Black on cream (inverse).
- The two tiers are locked — don't separate them in a primary-logo context.
- Underline rule in Highway Sodium, 2pt, drawn the full width of `TOURING`.

## 4. Secondary logo

- **"BMT" monogram** set in Citrus Gothic Black, stacked with a thin Highway Sodium rule underneath.
- Used for: van wrap door roundel, stage-front banner, merch labels, settlement-folder cover.
- Color treatments: cream on Asphalt Black (primary), Asphalt Black on cream (print-only), cream on Deep Indigo (for any piece shared with Inn materials so the two brands sit next to each other cleanly).

## 5. Brand colors

| Role | Name | Pantone | Hex | CMYK | RGB |
|------|------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Primary dark | **Asphalt Black** | Black 6 C | `#0A0D12` | 85 / 75 / 60 / 85 | 10 / 13 / 18 |
| Primary light | **Cloud Dancer** | 11-4201 TPG | `#F1F0EC` | 4 / 3 / 6 / 0 | 241 / 240 / 236 |
| Accent (signature) | **Highway Sodium** | 1235 C | `#FFB81C` | 0 / 30 / 100 / 0 | 255 / 184 / 28 |
| Family tie-in | **Indigo Deep** | 2119 C (inherited) | `#1A1A3E` | 100 / 95 / 35 / 45 | 26 / 26 / 62 |
| Utility gray | **Load-In Gray** | Cool Gray 10 C | `#63666A` | 40 / 30 / 20 / 66 | 99 / 102 / 106 |

Rationale: Touring is **the road at night.** Asphalt Black and Highway Sodium — sodium-vapor street lamps over a highway off-ramp — are the signature. Indigo Deep is kept in the palette so a Touring graphic can sit next to an Inn graphic and clearly belong to the same family. Cream holds. Gray is strictly utility (forms, wayfinding, small print).

Proportions: 65% Asphalt Black, 20% Cloud Dancer, 10% Highway Sodium, 5% Indigo Deep + Load-In Gray combined.

## 6. Incorrect usage

- Do **not** drop the `TOURING` banner from the primary lockup.
- Do **not** place the wordmark on a warm, bright, or sun-drenched photo — Touring lives after dark.
- Do **not** rotate, stretch, or outline the wordmark.
- Do **not** use Highway Sodium for anything longer than 4 words (it's an accent, not a headline color).
- Do **not** substitute Comic Sans, Bebas Neue, or other over-used "poster" fonts in place of Citrus Gothic Black.
- Do **not** use photos with talent/artist portraits unless the artist has a signed publicity release on file.
- Do **not** combine with the Blues Room magenta unless explicitly co-branding with the Inn.

## 7. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Usage |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Display (wordmark) | **Nathan** | Regular | Wordmark only |
| Headline | **Citrus Gothic** | Black / Bold | `TOURING` lockup, section heads, tour-poster headlines |
| Subhead | **Abril Fatface** | Regular | Occasional feature-poster subheads where we need a dramatic contrast |
| Body | **Montserrat** | Regular / Medium / SemiBold | Show dates, offer sheets, settlement pages, web body |

Rule: on any piece of show paper (poster, settlement envelope, advance email), **Citrus Gothic Black is the workhorse.** Abril Fatface is used sparingly.

## 8. Submark + brand marks

1. **BMT monogram** (see §4).
2. **Pennant mark:** a Cloud Dancer pennant with `BIG MUDDY TOURING` in Citrus Gothic small caps — used on stage-front banners and dressing-room signage.
3. **Corridor mark:** `NATCHEZ · MEMPHIS · CLARKSDALE · NEW ORLEANS · MEMPHIS · NASHVILLE` in single-line small caps — the actual routing corridor.
4. **Hard-out mark:** a Highway Sodium chevron "⟩" with "HARD OUT" in Citrus Gothic below — used in-house on stage schedules so everyone knows the no-negotiation curfew.

## 9. Patterns

- **Pattern A — Highway Stripe:** horizontal Highway Sodium dashes on Asphalt Black, mimicking the broken white-yellow lane line. Used behind Tour Date lockups on posters.
- **Pattern B — Amp Stack:** a grid of square Cloud Dancer rectangles (speaker grille cloth) on Asphalt Black — used as a ground for settlement folder interiors and merch-table mats.

## 11. Application examples (sketches for the finished PDF)

1. **Tour poster (11×17 letterpress):** Pattern A Highway Stripe ground, wordmark top-center, headliner in Citrus Gothic Black 90pt, routing in Montserrat SemiBold two-column, Highway Sodium footer bar with venue contact.
2. **Sprinter van wrap:** Asphalt Black body, wordmark full-width on both sides, BMT monogram on rear double doors in Highway Sodium, Corridor mark on rear bumper line. Full spec lives in `docs/VAN_WRAP_SPEC.md`.
3. **Offer sheet (letter size, digital):** cream ground, wordmark top-left, Citrus Gothic section heads ("OFFER," "BILLING," "TECH," "SETTLEMENT"), Montserrat body, Highway Sodium underline on section heads, Load-In Gray footer with contact.
4. **Backstage laminate (3×4):** Asphalt Black background, wordmark top, `BACKSTAGE` in Citrus Gothic Black centered, Highway Sodium chevron `⟩` underneath, printed access dates on reverse.
5. **Web home hero:** full-bleed performance photo tinted Asphalt Black duotone, wordmark overlaid upper-left in cream, `BIG MUDDY TOURING` set as page title, Highway Sodium CTA button "Book a show."
6. **Email signature:** 220px wide BMT monogram, Road Manager contact block in Montserrat, "`WE RUN THE ROAD`" tagline in Citrus Gothic small caps underneath.
