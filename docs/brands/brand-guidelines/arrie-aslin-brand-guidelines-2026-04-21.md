- Background: **Indigo Deep** (see §5), full bleed.

- Subline directly below wordmark: `SINGER · NATCHEZ · BIG MUDDY RECORDS` in tight small-caps sans, cream.

- Bottom-right small text: `BY HILLBILLY DREAMS INC.`

## 3. Primary logo

A two-line lockup:

- **Line 1:** "Arrie" in flowing connected script (Nathan or a compatible warm script), letter height sets the scale.
- **Line 2:** "Aslin" same script, slightly offset left so the two lines form a gentle diagonal, mimicking a signature.
- **Underline rule:** a thin horizontal line in the accent color (see §5) drawn the full width of the longer word.
- **Crop:** generous padding on all four sides — never less than the height of the "A."

Primary color treatment: cream (Cloud Dancer) on indigo. Secondary: indigo on cream. The magenta accent color is reserved for the underline rule and one-color merch.

## 4. Secondary logo

- **Stacked monogram "AA"** — two capital letters in a condensed display serif, tight kerning, optionally tilted 3–5° to the right so they feel handwritten rather than geometric.
- **Use cases:** social avatars, favicons, merch hang tags, vinyl center labels, pick picks, coasters.
- **Color treatments:**
  - Cream on indigo (primary)
  - Magenta on cream (accent)
  - Black on cream (print-only fallback)

## 5. Brand colors

| Role | Name | Pantone | Hex | CMYK | RGB |
|------|------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Primary dark | **Indigo Deep** | 2119 C (inherited from Big Muddy Inn palette) | `#1A1A3E` | 100 / 95 / 35 / 45 | 26 / 26 / 62 |
| Primary light | **Cloud Dancer** | 11-4201 TPG | `#F1F0EC` | 4 / 3 / 6 / 0 | 241 / 240 / 236 |
| Accent (signature) | **Natchez Magenta** | 682 C (inherited) | `#994878` | 35 / 85 / 30 / 15 | 153 / 72 / 120 |
| Warm alt | **Burnished Amber** | 1385 C | `#C8852A` | 20 / 50 / 100 / 5 | 200 / 133 / 42 |
| Photo-treatment black | **River Black** | Black 6 C | `#0A0D12` | 85 / 75 / 60 / 85 | 10 / 13 / 18 |

Rationale: Arrie Aslin lives inside the Big Muddy Inn palette (she is artist-in-residence there) but her signature color is **Natchez Magenta** — the Blues Room magenta — making her the most "connected to the Inn" of any of the sub-brands. **Burnished Amber** carries her into the Big Muddy Records + Iron & Earth warm-amber family so vinyl sleeves and tour posters don't feel hemmed into one room.

Proportions in a typical layout: 60% Indigo Deep, 25% Cloud Dancer, 10% Natchez Magenta, 5% Burnished Amber (accent only).

## 6. Incorrect usage

- Do **not** use the wordmark on a busy photo without a solid color band behind it.
- Do **not** rotate the wordmark more than 5°.
- Do **not** stretch the wordmark disproportionately.
- Do **not** swap the magenta for hot-pink, lavender, or any other off-family pink.
- Do **not** use Comic Sans, Zapfino, or other "handwritten-cute" fonts anywhere in her materials.
- Do **not** use AI-generated images of Arrie Aslin or stand-in performers — real Chase Pierson photography only.
- Do **not** place the wordmark on any background with a contrast ratio below WCAG 2.1 AA.
- Do **not** spell the name wrong. Arrie Aslin. Period.

## 7. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Usage |
|------|----------|---------|-------|
| Display (script) | **Nathan** (or Magnolia Script as close substitute) | Regular | Wordmark only + accent pulls on posters/merch |
| Headline | **Abril Fatface** | Regular | Section titles on EPK, album liners, show posters |
| Subhead | **Citrus Gothic** (or GT America Mono as alt) | Regular | Subheadlines, track numbers, run-time |
| Body | **Montserrat** | Light / Regular / Medium | Bios, press copy, show listings, website body |

Pairing rule: Nathan + Abril on the same page is fine; Nathan alone is the wordmark. Body copy below ~18pt is always Montserrat.

## 8. Submark + brand marks

Three marks beyond the logo:

1. **"AA" monogram** (see §4).
2. **Signature mark:** a single thin-weight line drawing of a vintage ribbon microphone, no shadow, in the active accent color. Used at the foot of emails, at the end of a press release, on merch hang tags.
3. **Corridor mark:** the phrase `NATCHEZ · MEMPHIS · CLARKSDALE · NEW ORLEANS` in tight small caps on a single line — her touring corridor, reads like a tombstone.

## 9. Patterns

- **Pattern A — Mic Field:** the ribbon microphone mark repeated in a staggered grid on a magenta ground, overlaid with Cloud Dancer "BLUES HIGHWAY" lockup (see Big Muddy Inn "BEDS and BLUES" page for the structural analog).
- **Pattern B — Indigo Stripe:** thin vertical bars alternating Indigo Deep and River Black, 6mm wide, behind type for EPK back covers and vinyl jacket inners.

Never put type over the Mic Field pattern except the "BLUES HIGHWAY" or "ARRIE ASLIN" wordmark.
