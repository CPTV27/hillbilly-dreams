## USDA Programs

### Rural Business Development Grants (RBDG)
- $10K-$500K per award, ~$30M nationally
- FY 2027 deadline expected ~Feb 2027
- Requires public/nonprofit partner (City of Natchez, Adams County)
- "Technical assistance to small businesses" — exactly what DSD does
- **Action:** Start partnership conversation with City of Natchez now

### RISE (Rural Innovation Stronger Economy)
- Up to $2M per grant
- Requires "jobs accelerator" partnership (university + local gov + private)
- MBT as jobs accelerator providing AI tools + training
- **Action:** Pitch to Alcorn State as joint application

### REDLG (Rural Economic Development)
- Quarterly deadlines through June 2026
- Must go through a USDA RD borrower (electric or telephone utility)
- Frame Sovereign Pi as telecom/digital infrastructure

## The Banking Model (CRA + CDFI)

Regional banks taking equity in MBT deployments could qualify as:
- **CRA-eligible investment** (Community Reinvestment Act) — investing in digital infrastructure in LMI areas
- **New Markets Tax Credit** eligible — Adams County likely qualifies
- **CDFI partnership** — 3 Mississippi CDFIs approved for SSBCI funding

Business health signals from MBT (review velocity, social engagement, revenue proxies) become supplemental underwriting data. The bank gets equity. The businesses get AI. The town gets infrastructure.
