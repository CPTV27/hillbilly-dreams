## WHY NOT "READS YOUR BOOKS"
"Same price as ChatGPT, except this one reads your books" was clever but wrong:
- Nobody under 50 calls their accounting "books"
- It frames the product as a bookkeeping tool — it's not
- It undersells the product — it does marketing, CRM, research, social, reputation, everything
- Clever loses to clear when selling to busy people

The product does ALL the stuff the owner doesn't know how to do:
- Marketing? It handles that.
- Bookkeeping? It watches your numbers.
- Reviews? It monitors them.
- Competition? It tracks them.
- Social media? Take a photo, it does the rest.
- You just run your business. It does the rest.

## DEEP SOUTH DIRECTORY — THE MAIN STREET PLAY

Deep South Directory is how Measurably Better reaches small businesses. A business owner doesn't sign up for "Measurably Better." They join the Deep South Directory. The listing comes with the dashboard.

**The flow:**
1. Business joins Deep South Directory (free listing — public profile, 100 AI queries, competitive snapshot)
2. They see their data and think "I want more"
3. They upgrade to $20/mo — connects QuickBooks, daily briefings, unlimited AI, guided learning path
4. They outgrow that and upgrade to $99/mo — all sources, marketing automation, alerts, 3 team seats

**Three distribution channels (no ad spend):**
1. **Municipality sponsors businesses** — city buys a block of licenses, funded by ARPA/CDBG. Instant trust, zero sales friction.
2. **Boots on the ground** — "I run the hotel down the street. This is what I use. Want to try it for free?"
3. **CVB / Chamber as distributor** — directory listing bundled with chamber membership.
