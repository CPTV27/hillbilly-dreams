# DSD Google Workspace Bundle Specification

## Product Overview
By registering as a Google Cloud Partner Advantage Reseller, Deep South Directory transforms from a discretionary advertising subscription into a mandatory operational utility.

For a local business owner currently using `townplumber@gmail.com` to send invoices, providing a professional `@domain.com` Workspace account natively within the DSD environment proves immediate, tangible value.

## The Delivered Product (What the Client Gets)
A fully provisioned Google Workspace Business Starter seat including:
*   **Professional Email:** `user@theirbusiness.com` (instantly killing their generic Gmail dependency).
*   **Google Calendar:** Synchronized calendar functionality for reservations and service bookings.
*   **Google Drive:** 30GB of secure cloud storage for business documentation, inventory sheets, and media assets.
*   **Google Meet:** Native video conferencing for remote client consultations.

## Tier Integration & Pricing Matrix
To preserve unit economics, this hard cost is abstracted securely behind our deeper tiers.

*   **Free Tier ($0/mo):** No inclusion.
*   **Essentials ($25/mo):** No inclusion.
*   **Pro ($50/mo):** *Includes 1 Workspace Seat.* 
    *   *HDI Cost:* $5.76/mo
    *   *Margin:* $44.24/mo (88% Gross Margin)
*   **Marketing ($99/mo):** *Includes up to 3 Workspace Seats.*
    *   *HDI Cost (Max):* $17.28/mo
    *   *Margin:* $81.72/mo (82.5% Gross Margin)

## Strategic Objective
The inclusion of Workspace creates absolute subscriber rigidity. If a business owner wishes to cancel their $99/mo DSD subscription during a slow quarter, they must fundamentally accept severing their primary business email address. When DSD becomes structural IT plumbing, churn plummets to near zero.
