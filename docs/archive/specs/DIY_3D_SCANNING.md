# DIY 3D scanning — Deep South Directory ($99 / self-serve tier)

Guide for **business owners** who want a lightweight 3D preview on their listing without a truck roll. Built for **iPhone / iPad with LiDAR** (Polycam or equivalent).

## Step by step

1. **Download Polycam** (or approved alternative we list in onboarding).
2. **Clear the floor** — bags, rolling racks, clutter reads as geometry noise.
3. **Open “Room” or “LiDAR” mode** — follow the on-screen path; move like you are painting the walls with the camera.
4. **Overlap every doorway** — walk through, pause, walk back, scan again from the other side.
5. **Export** as **USDZ** or **GLB** per the DSD upload prompt (we standardize in product).
6. **Upload** from `/directory` owner portal (link texted after tier upgrade).

## Good scan vs. bad scan

| Good | Bad |
|------|-----|
| Slow, steady pans | Fast “whip” turns |
| Even lighting | Single bare bulb + deep shadow |
| Both sides of glass cases | One pass on mirror wall |
| Second pass on busy retail | One loop and done |

## What shows on the listing

- **Thumbnail** from first usable frame.
- **Embed** lightweight viewer (WebGL / USDZ quick-look on iOS).
- **Badge:** “Owner-captured 3D — refreshed {date}”.
