## Multi-Channel Distribution

Every episode produces:

| Format | Channel | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Full stream | TikTok Live | Real-time |
| Full replay | YouTube | Same day |
| 60-sec clip | TikTok, Reels, Shorts | Within 2 hours |
| 15-sec teaser | Stories (IG, FB) | Within 1 hour |
| Photo still | Instagram feed | Same day |
| Write-up | Big Muddy Magazine | Next day |
| Radio mention | Big Muddy Radio | Next rotation |
| Newsletter | Weekly Dispatch | End of week |

One stream → 8 pieces of content across 6 channels. That's the flywheel in content form.
