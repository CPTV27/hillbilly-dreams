# Weekly Regional Narrative (Editorial Cadence)

## The Cycle
- **Monday:** Capture
- **Tuesday:** AI Draft
- **Wednesday:** Red Pen
- **Thursday:** Approve
- **Friday:** Publish

## Ownership & Publishing
- **Owners:** 
  - Chase handles raw capture (interviews/photography) and final approval (Red Pen).
  - Agents manage the AI drafting, content structuring, and scheduling.
- **Publishing Hierarchy:**
  - Big Muddy Magazine publishes the core feature first.
  - Big Muddy Radio subsequently streams associated interviews and sessions.
  - Deep South Directory and other ecosystem brands amplify and contextualize the feature for local impact.
