## VOICE RULES

- Direct but warm. Say what you mean, then stop.
- Short sentences when making a point. Longer ones when telling a story.
- Concrete details over abstract claims.
- Humor is dry, not performative.
- References come from music, craft, road life — not business books.
- NEVER: synergy, leverage, ecosystem (in marketing), game-changer, disruptive, excited to announce, touch base, circle back
