# Big Muddy Touring — The Plan

**Today + the proposed expansion. One page.**

Big Muddy Touring is a Mississippi-corridor music + hospitality + media ecosystem run by three equity partners (Tracy Alderson-Allen, Amy Allen, Chase Pierson). We are proposing to add **Sean Davis** as a Season One collaborator — with **Doug Duffy and Badd** as a new label artist and the **Arcade Theater in Ferriday, LA** as a second programmed venue paired with the Inn.

---

| | |
|---|---|

| **Anchor** | Big Muddy Inn — Natchez, MS (6 rooms + the Blues Room as a listening stage) |

## The proposed expansion (Sean Davis + Doug Duffy and Badd)

| | |
|---|---|
| **New collaborator** | Sean Davis — year-round Natchez resident, ex-director of the Arcade Theater (5 years), one month into managing Doug Duffy and Badd |
| **New venue** | Arcade Theater (Delta Music Museum, Ferriday LA) — 300-cap, ~15 min from Natchez, rent ~$225/show with Friends of the Arcade sponsorship |
| **New artist** | Doug Duffy and Badd — non-exclusive promotional signing on Big Muddy Records |
| **Season One scope** | 6–8 Arcade shows aligned with the Inn calendar + at least one Doug Duffy radio + Records promo cycle |
| **Structure** | Non-exclusive, project-based, per-show economics. No equity. No retainer. No exclusivity. Phase 2 (post-Season One) deliberately open in shape. |
| **Cost to BMT** | $0 added. Each Arcade show stands on its own per-show P&L. |

## How it amplifies

- **Year-round Natchez coverage** when Chase is in the Hudson Valley (May–October).
- **A second 300-cap stage** paired with the Inn's intimate room — develop at the Inn, headline at the Arcade; Arcade headliners do late-night Inn sets.
- **Stronger artist roster** — Doug Duffy and Badd joins, plus future artist introductions Sean brings.
- **Local sponsor + board reach** — Friends of the Arcade, the Delta Music Museum board, Ferriday network.
- **One audience, two venues** — the route deepens without doubling the operation.
