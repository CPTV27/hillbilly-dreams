# The Natchez Compass
## A Big Muddy Magazine Special Feature — Editorial Outline

**Aesthetic:** "Broadside" — High-fidelity photography, Playfair Display headlines, DM Sans body text, eggshell paper background. Premium feel that justifies the $5K/mo investment.

---

### I. The Cover: "The Porch of the South"
- **Visual:** Full-bleed, high-contrast photograph of sunset over the Mississippi River from the bluff, shot by Chase.
- **Headline:** Natchez: Where the River Meets the Soul.
- **Sub-headline:** A guide to the sovereignty, density, and local velocity of Mississippi's oldest city.

### II. The "Why": Sovereignty & Density
- **Philosophy:** Based on Outsider Economics — Natchez isn't just a museum; it's a living ecosystem where history fuels modern entrepreneurship.
- **The Narrative:** Moving beyond the "Greatest Hits" to show the dense network of makers, musicians, and chefs that make the city self-sovereign.

### III. The Anchor: 411 North Commerce
- **Feature:** The Big Muddy Inn.
- **Content:** A photo essay of the 6-suite boutique hotel, focusing on the intimate guest experience and the "Blues & Beds" show packages.
- **The Link:** Direct "Book Now" integration for seamless conversion from reader to guest.

> **COMPLIANCE NOTE:** Cloudbeds QR code integration is blocked on Inn pilot scoping with Tracy/Amy. Use a generic booking link until Cloudbeds is confirmed live. Do not reference Cloudbeds by name on customer-facing materials.

### IV. The Soundtrack: Live from the Blues Room
- **Feature:** Big Muddy Radio.
- **Content:** Interviews with residency artists and profiles of the live music scene.
- **The Link:** Sidebar with "Listen Now" link to Natchez live sessions on Big Muddy Radio, turning the reading experience into a multi-sensory one.

> **COMPLIANCE NOTE:** JP Houston deal not finalized as of 2026-03-27. DO NOT name JP or any unconfirmed personnel. Feature the venue, the music, and the program — not specific artists who aren't under contract. Arri Aslin is confirmed and can be named.

### V. Main Street Heartbeat: The Directory
- **Feature:** Deep South Directory (DSD).
- **Content:** A curated map of "Hidden Gems" — local businesses in the ecosystem.
- **The Pitch:** Highlighting how these businesses are part of a media network — featured in the Magazine, mentioned on Radio, visible in AI search — not just listed in a database.

> **NOTE:** Frame as ecosystem participation, not "AI marketing tools." Match the reframe: the value is the media network, the tools are the delivery mechanism.

### VI. The Call to Action: "Support Local Velocity"
- **Closing:** A letter from the founders (Chase, Tracy, Amy) on the importance of local investment.
- **Conversion:** Links to BuyCurious Art Gallery to purchase prints of Natchez featured in the guide.

---

## Why This Outline Closes the Deal

1. **High Value:** Looks like a $20 magazine but acts as a lead generation machine for every business mentioned.
2. **Ecosystem Proof:** Demonstrates exactly how the framework works in practice: Content (Magazine) → Hospitality (Inn) → Tech (Directory).
3. **Repeatable:** This isn't a one-time brochure. It's a quarterly publication that gets better with each issue as more businesses join the ecosystem.
