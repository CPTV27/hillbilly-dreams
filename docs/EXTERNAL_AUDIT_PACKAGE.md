# PART 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## What We Are

Hillbilly Dreams Inc is a media-hospitality ecosystem anchored in Natchez, Mississippi (pop. 14,000; 700,000 annual visitors). We operate a music venue/boutique hotel (The Big Muddy Inn), a touring circuit, a magazine, a radio station, a record label, a business directory, and a photography studio — all running on a single technology platform we built.

As of April 2026, we are repositioning the technology platform (MBT — Measurably Better Things) as a **licensed civic-commerce operating system** sold to institutions: towns, tourism bureaus, chambers of commerce, Main Street organizations, real estate brokerages, and hospitality portfolios.

## The One-Sentence Pitch

MBT is a licensed local growth system for towns, districts, brokerages, and cultural operators — combining directory infrastructure, publishing, media distribution, business onboarding, and service delivery into one operating model.

## What Makes This Different

We're not selling software. We're selling coordination capacity. The software is the delivery mechanism; the value is the program — making every participating business findable, promotable, and measurable, with real reporting that proves ROI to funders and stakeholders.

The competitive moat: we own the media. No competitor has a magazine, radio station, record label, touring circuit, and business directory operating in the same market. The media creates the audience. The directory monetizes the audience. The platform measures both.

# PART 3: BUSINESS MODEL

## The Pivot (April 8, 2026)

**Previous model:** Self-serve SaaS with individual tier pricing (Free/$25/$50/$99/$250). Low-ticket, high-churn, requires polished UI and massive marketing spend.

**New model:** Licensed civic-commerce operating system sold to institutions. High-ticket program sales, implemented with services and software. Fewer customers, higher trust, longer cycles, stickier relationships, stronger moat.

## Revenue Streams

### Stream 1: Hospitality (Operating Now)
- The Big Muddy Inn — 6-room boutique hotel, Natchez
- The Blues Room — music venue attached to the Inn
- Bar operations (liquor license pending/active)
- Revenue: room nights + bar + event tickets

### Stream 2: Entertainment / Touring (Operating Now)
- Big Muddy Touring — books bands along a 13-city Mississippi corridor
- Sprinter van transportation (owned, operational)
- Show promotion through Magazine, Radio, social
- 2:1 ecosystem multiplier: every $1,000 show generates $500+ in downstream Inn + Directory revenue
- 12 show nights/month target

### Stream 3: Media (Operating Now)
- Big Muddy Magazine — editorial content, city guides, photo essays (18 articles live)
- Big Muddy Radio — streaming via Icecast, 18 shows programmed, 7 DJs
- Big Muddy Records — non-exclusive artist services, catalog (31 artists seeded)
- Photography — Chase Pierson Photography, 45 prints ($150-$1,800)

### Stream 4: MBT Institutional Licensing (Building — Primary Growth Engine)
- Licensed civic-commerce OS sold to towns, chambers, brokerages, districts
- Setup/implementation fee (one-time)
- Annual platform license (recurring)
- Optional service packages: content/media, merchant onboarding, training

### Stream 5: DSD Self-Serve (Operating — Secondary)
- Deep South Directory — business listings for walk-in SMBs
- $99/mo Marketing tier, $250/mo Engine tier
- Stripe Payment Links, no auth required
- Fallback for businesses not in an institutional program

## The Multi-Stakeholder Model

Multiple institutions can operate in the same market simultaneously. Programs stack, not compete.

| Layer | Buyer | What They Get | Their Incentive |
|---|---|---|---|
| Municipal | Town, tourism bureau | City-wide program, outcome reporting, grant justification | Tourism dollars, funder ROI |
| Business Leaders | Broker, Main Street org | Sponsor businesses, monthly vitality reports | Property values tied to street vitality |
| Hospitality | Inn operators, venue networks | Coordinated promotion, event pipeline | Cross-promotion, tourism capture |
| Participants | Individual businesses | Listing, promotion, media, measurement | $0 (sponsored) or $99-$250/mo self-serve |

## Pricing

**Institutional:** Not a tier chart. Each engagement is a tailored proposal: setup fee + annual license + optional service packages.

**Self-serve:** Free / $99 Marketing / $250 Engine.
