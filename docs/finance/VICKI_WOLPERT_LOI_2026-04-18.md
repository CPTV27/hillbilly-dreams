## 1 · Purpose

This Letter of Intent outlines the terms under which MBT will provide Client with digital platform services ("the Services") on a month-to-month basis beginning **May 1, 2026**.

## 2 · Services provided at base rate

For **$500 per month**, Client receives:

**a. Upstate Directory ("Vicki's List")** — a curated digital directory of local businesses in Client's service area (Athens NY, Catskill, Hudson, Coxsackie and ~25 mile radius), covering:
- Restaurants and dining
- Attractions and destinations
- Household and property services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, internet, pool, lawn)
- Contractors and builders
- Handymen and local trades
- Real estate adjacent services (inspectors, surveyors, attorneys, mortgage brokers, insurance, stagers)
- Local recommendations (cleaning, pet sitters, snow removal)

MBT will seed the directory with approximately 200 verified entries prior to launch at no additional charge.

**b. Branded landing page** hosted on Client's designated domain, including about, listings preview, directory preview, and contact form.

**c. Social media automation** across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn — 2 to 3 posts per week mixing directory spotlights, seasonal content, and Client's own listings.

**d. Email newsletter** — monthly, directory-driven.

**e. Monthly performance dashboard** — directory additions, social engagement, newsletter opens, inbound contact counts.

**f. Weekly 20-minute sync during onboarding** (first 4 weeks), transitioning to monthly sync thereafter.

## 3 · Add-on services

Services beyond the base scope are available on a professional-services basis at rates to be agreed in writing. Examples include:
- Custom module development
- Professional photography for directory entries (via Studio C, at Studio C rates)
- Integration with third-party CRM or listing platforms
- Expanded directory coverage outside the base region

## 4 · Term and termination

**Term:** Month-to-month, beginning May 1, 2026.

**Notice:** Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.

**No auto-renewal mechanism** beyond the month-to-month structure.

**Prepayment policy:** Monthly fee is billed in advance on the 1st of each month; prorated for any partial month.

## 5 · Data and content ownership

**Client content** (photos, notes, descriptions Client provides) remains Client's property. Client grants MBT a license to use this content within Client's tenant and, with explicit permission, as examples in MBT's own marketing.

**Directory data MBT seeds** (research-sourced business information) is shared commons — Client has full use within the tenant; MBT retains rights to use the same entity data in other tenant directories.

**Platform IP** (directory engine, canonical entity store, site templates, automation workflows) is and remains MBT's property. Client receives a license to use; Client does not acquire ownership of the underlying platform.

## 6 · Service level and support

MBT commits to best-effort service. No formal SLA at this stage. Critical issues (site down, data loss) addressed within one business day.

## 7 · Assignment and restructure

Client acknowledges MBT LLC is in formation in Mississippi. MBT does not maintain a New York footprint — Client-facing services in Client's operating region (Upstate NY) are delivered with operational support from Tuthill Design (a NY entity affiliated with MBT). Upon MBT LLC filing, all rights and obligations under this agreement assign automatically to MBT LLC. Until filing, the agreement is executed under FarleyPierson LLC d/b/a Measurably Better Things.

## 8 · Signatures

This LOI is non-binding with respect to specific deliverables beyond the scope defined above, but represents a good-faith commitment by both parties to proceed on these terms.

A full services agreement will be executed within 30 days of MBT LLC filing.
