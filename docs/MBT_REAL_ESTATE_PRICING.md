# MBT Real Estate Product — Pricing & Offers

## Broker Product (Vicki Wolpert = Pilot #1, Woodstock)

**Setup:** $1,500
- Professional video shoot (top 3 listings)
- Branded directory profile
- Magazine feature about her market
- Platform configured and branded

**First Month:** $500 (full Marketing tier)
- Content going out within the first week
- After 30 days, pick your tier

**Ongoing Tiers:**

| Tier | Price | What They Get |
|---|---|---|
| Platform | $199/mo | Directory listing, AI social content (8 posts/mo), analytics, review management |
| Marketing | $500/mo | Everything above + monthly video, magazine feature, professional photography, radio mention |
| Production | $1,500/mo | Everything above + dedicated monthly shoot day, custom video per listing, neighborhood mini-docs, full social management |

**No contract.** Pick your tier after 30 days. Walk away if it's not working.

**The pitch:** "Your listings will be in a magazine, on the radio, and across social media — produced by the same team that shoots for Brown Harris Stevens and Berkshire Hathaway."

## Town / Municipal Product (Natchez = Pilot #1)

**Kickstart:** $10,000
- Brings Studio C crew to town
- One week of intensive content production
- Shoots: downtown, river, architecture, businesses, food, music, people
- Deliverables: video sizzle reel, 200+ professional photos, social media content library, 10 business profiles
- Sets up the Deep South Directory for the town
- Town gets a content library they've never had

**Ongoing:** $500/mo network management SLA
- Keeps the directory running
- AI generates ongoing content
- Bugs get fixed immediately
- New businesses get onboarded

**Individual businesses subscribe separately** at DSD tiers ($25/$50/$99/$250)

**The pitch to the town:** "For $10,000 you get a professional content library, a live business directory, and an ongoing marketing engine. The businesses pay for themselves. The content makes the town look like the most photogenic place in America — because it is."
