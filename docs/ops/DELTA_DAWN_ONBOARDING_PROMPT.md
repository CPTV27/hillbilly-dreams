## How to Set This Up in Google AI Studio

1. Go to https://aistudio.google.com/
2. Click "Create new prompt" → "Chat prompt"
3. Paste the entire system instruction above into the "System instructions" field
4. Set the model to **Gemini 2.5 Flash** (fast, cheap, good enough for conversation)
5. Click "Share" → "Anyone with the link can use"
6. Send the share link to Tracy and Amy

They open it, they're talking to Delta Dawn. She walks them through everything at their own pace.
