## 1. Executive Summary

Hillbilly Dreams Inc. (HDI) is a holding company that owns the intellectual property, technology platform, and brand portfolio for a vertically integrated media, hospitality, and technology ecosystem in the Deep South.

**What HDI owns:**
- A multi-tenant AI-powered technology platform (Measurably Better / MBT)
- 11 live domains serving hospitality, media, SaaS, education, and civic markets
- The Big Muddy Inn (6-suite boutique hotel, Natchez, MS)
- Big Muddy Magazine, Radio, Records, Entertainment, Touring, and Gallery brands
- Deep South Directory (regional SMB SaaS product)
- Outsider Economics (thought leadership / publishing brand)

**The opportunity:** Build regional technology infrastructure for SMBs, municipalities, school districts, and tourism boards across the Mississippi corridor. MBT replaces an 11-tool software stack ($2,839/mo) with a single platform starting at $20/mo. The competitive moat is the integrated media ecosystem — no SaaS company offers editorial coverage, radio mentions, and a regional directory because they don't own media properties.

**Current state:** Pre-revenue on SaaS. Inn generating ~$7,664/mo ($92K/yr) but operating at a loss (NOI -$22K/yr). 49 opportunities in pipeline totaling $1.4M annual / $117K monthly. Platform is built, live, and demoable across all 11 domains. Launch date: April 27, 2026 (Chase's birthday).

**6-week runway.** Every decision is filtered through: "Does this generate revenue before June?"
