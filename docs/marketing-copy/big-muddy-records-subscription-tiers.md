### Silver Tier

**Pitch:**
The Silver Tier is for independent artists ready to professionalize their music releases and establish a foundational presence under a recognized indie label. This tier provides the essential infrastructure to distribute your music globally, track your audience's engagement, and maintain a consistent brand identity. It's designed to give you a solid, reliable platform to share your art and begin building a more robust music career with fundamental label support.

**What's Included:**
*   **Digital Distribution:** Your music distributed to all major streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, etc.) and digital storefronts via Big Muddy Records' robust distribution network.
*   **Basic Analytics Reporting:** Monthly reports detailing your streaming performance, listener demographics, and geographic reach across platforms to help you understand your audience.
*   **Social Media Template Library Access:** A curated collection of editable post templates, graphic assets, and best practice guidelines to help you maintain a consistent and professional social media presence.
*   **Big Muddy Records Artist Roster Listing:** A dedicated profile page for you and your music on the official Big Muddy Records website, increasing your visibility within our label community.
*   **Quarterly Check-In Call:** One 30-minute virtual call per quarter with a Big Muddy Records team member to review your progress, answer questions, and discuss upcoming plans.

**What This Is NOT:**
This is not a full-service marketing or artist management package; it provides core distribution and foundational support, with the artist primarily driving their own promotional efforts.

**Recommended Price:** $75/month

**Sample Artist Profile:**
This tier is ideal for a dedicated singer-songwriter or band who has been self-releasing music or is new to professional distribution. They have a growing catalog of recorded material, a small but engaged local following, and are actively performing. They are comfortable managing most of their own social media and booking, but they recognize the value of professional distribution, basic performance data, and the legitimacy that comes with being part of an established independent label.
