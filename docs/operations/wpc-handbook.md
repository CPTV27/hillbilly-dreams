# Weekly Partner Commitments — Handbook

## What this is

This is how we stay aligned without chaos.

Each person has:

- 2–3 commitments per week
- visible to everyone
- updated in one place

If it's not here, it's not real.

---

## Where to look

Go to:

→ Asana
→ Project: Weekly Partner Commitments

That's it. Nothing else matters for weekly execution.

---

## What you'll see

Each week:

- Your name
- 2–3 tasks
- Due dates
- Clear next actions

---

## Your job (simple)

### 1. Check once per day (30 seconds)

Look at your commitments.

Ask:

- Am I on track?
- Do I need to move something to "Waiting"?
- Is something blocked?

### 2. Keep it to 2–3 things

Hard rule: if you have more than 3, something is wrong.

If something new comes in, ask: what gets dropped?

### 3. Update status honestly

Move tasks:

- Committed This Week → active work
- Waiting → blocked / dependent
- Done → finished
- Dropped → not doing

No silent carryover.

### 4. Friday reset

At the end of the week, every task must be:

- Done
- Moved to next week intentionally
- Or dropped

Nothing rolls over automatically.

---

## What good looks like

- You know your week in under 10 seconds
- No one asks "what are you working on?"
- Work actually finishes

## What NOT to do

- Don't add extra tasks "just in case"
- Don't leave things vague
- Don't track work anywhere else
