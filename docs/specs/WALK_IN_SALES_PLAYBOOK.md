# Walk-in sales playbook — Deep South Directory + Sovereign Pi

Step-by-step for **Chase** (or trained scout) walking cold into a business with phone + Pi demo kit.

## The six beats

1. **Pull the audit** — `/admin/scout/audit/{slug}` on your phone (pre-seeded slug or create on the spot after quick GMB search).
2. **Show the score** — “Here is what is broken on Google right now.” Point to **critical** rows first, not the fluff.
3. **Plug the Pi demo** — HDMI to their TV if they will let you; otherwise hand them the small screen rig. Show **menu / reviews / local AI** offline-capable story.
4. **Drop the flyer** — printed `sandbox/dsd-flyer.html` + `sovereign-pi-flyer.html` if hardware is in play.
5. **Sign on the spot** — `/directory/onboard` on your phone; you type, they talk. **Do not** make them hunt for the form.
6. **Schedule delivery** — Pi ship window + install call; calendar invite before you leave the parking lot.

## Objections

**“We already have a website.”**  
“We are not replacing your site. We are fixing what Google shows when someone searches *open now near me* — that is a different door.”

**“I can’t afford it.”**  
“Start at **free to list**. If you hate us, you are out zero dollars. If you want the computer on the counter, **$25/mo** cancels a ChatGPT sub and pays for itself on one saved bad-review weekend.”

**“What if I cancel?”**  
“Service stops. You keep the relationships we built — reviews, photos, copy. Hardware return policy is in writing; most people keep the Pi because it runs their board.”

## Gear checklist

- Phone + charger + hotspot backup.
- HDMI + USB-C adapter nest.
- Business cards + two flyers.
- Sanitized demo Pi or hardened loaner.
