# DeFacto Media Origin Details

This document outlines the archival context of the DeFacto Media Proposal deck, mapping its evolution into the current Big Muddy / HDI infrastructure.

## Original Proposal Pitch
The initial DeFacto framework was presented heavily focusing on native programming inside the AIC ecosystem:
- Installation of a dedicated podcast studio
- Syndication of "Troy Tech Talks"
- Broad cultural programming generated at the AIC hub

## Resource Allocation & Budget
The requested funding thesis was capitalized as follows:
- **Consulting / Strategy:** $60,000
- **Marketing / Distribution:** $20,000
- **Equipment Build-out:** $10,000

## Target ROI Metrics
The investment was pitched to yield:
- 1,000,000+ total digital impressions
- 10,000 core verified subscribers
- Complete standing transition from standalone creator pipeline into a durable *Media Company*.

## The Bob Bedard Connection & The HDI Pivot
The structural goals outlined in the DeFacto phase were fundamentally supported by the relationship with Bob Bedard. While the DeFacto branding remained localized to earlier conceptual phases, the underlying operational infrastructure—the tech stack, the media capture principles, and the target impression metrics—are what ultimately incubated and scaled directly into the present-day **Hillbilly Dreams Inc. (HDI) / Big Muddy** portfolios.
