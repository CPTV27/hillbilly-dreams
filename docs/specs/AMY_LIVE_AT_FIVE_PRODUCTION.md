# Amy's "Live at Five" Production Spec
**Event Date:** May 8  
**Format:** Live Acoustic/Honky-Tonk Showcase + Multi-Cam Content Capture  
**Location:** The Inn (Natchez, MS)  

## 1. Budget Breakdown (NOLA Session Talent)
Sourcing a cohesive backing band (e.g. Gal Holiday ecosystem) out of New Orleans reduces rehearsal friction compared to disjointed session players.

| Line Item | Scope | Est. Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Band Fees** | 4 players (Drums, Bass, Telecaster, Pedal Steel) @ $250/day x 2 days | $2,000 |
| **Rehearsal Facility** | NOLA Lockout (Fountainbleau or Music Shed) for 2 days | $400 |
| **Travel Stipend** | Gas/Mileage for NOLA ↔ Natchez (2 vehicles) | $150 |
| **Lodging** | Accommodation at The Inn | $0 (Soft Cost) |
| **Food & Beverage** | Per diem catering (rehearsals + show day) | $200 |
| **Total Cash Outlay** | | **$2,750** |

## 2. Production Timeline
*   **Week 1 (Apr 6 - Apr 12):** Secure NOLA band commitment. Lock in exact players and setlist.
*   **Week 2 (Apr 13 - Apr 26):** Band receives charts/demos. Self-guided practice.
*   **Week 3 (Apr 27 - May 3):** Two 6-hour full band rehearsals at a NOLA lockout facility.
*   **Show Week (May 6):** Band travel day to Natchez. Check into the Inn.
*   **Show Week (May 7):** Tech rehearsal and FOH sweep at the venue.
*   **Show Day (May 8):** Load-in, line-check, downbeat at 5:00 PM.

## 3. Venue Setup at The Inn
*   **Staging:** Floor-level intimacy.
*   **PA System:** Clean, warm vocal forward mix tailored for Americana. 
*   **Lighting:** Ambient gold/amber wash. Cinematic low-light aesthetic. No aggressive strobes.

## 4. Content Capture Plan (Studio C)
This is not just a gig; it is a bulk content origin point directed by Chase/Studio C.
*   **A-Cam:** Locked off wide (Ronin 4D on tripod) capturing the full stage.
*   **B-Cam & C-Cam:** Roaming tight shots / gimbal work focusing on emotive close-ups and instrument isolation.
*   **Audio:** Multi-track split directly from the console to capture raw stems for post-mixing and potential "Live from Natchez" EP release on Big Muddy Records.

## 5. Promotion Timeline
*   **T-Minus 4 Weeks:** Announce show on all respective IG/FB brand accounts (Magazine, Radio, Touring). Announce NOLA backing band to leverage regional crossover.
*   **T-Minus 3 Weeks:** Distribute print flyers natively designed via Canvas to key downtown Natchez hubs, coffee shops, and partnering DSD businesses.
*   **T-Minus 2 Weeks:** Pitch targeted local press (Natchez Democrat) framing the event as the kick-off for the broader Big Muddy Touring season.
*   **Post-Show:** Export highlights to the Weekly Regional Narrative editorial track for next-day DSD / Magazine distribution.
