# Chase Pierson: Consulting & Strategy Product Spec

While HDI operations (DSD, Tuthill, Studio C) are built to scale via automated platforms or delegated regional operators, this product is the unscalable, premium tier. It is direct extraction of Chase’s diagnostic capability. 

## 1. The Service (The "Ego Audit")
This is not standard business consulting. It is an aggressive, high-velocity organizational diagnosis.
*   **The Target:** Mid-market business owners ($1M–$10M ARR) trapped in operational gridlock, or legacy institutions failing to map to modern digital infrastructure.
*   **The Delivery:** An immediate, unvarnished physical audit of their systems, software layer, media narrative, and team structure.
*   **The Output ("The Burn Book"):** Chase delivers a concise, brutal tactical document highlighting exactly where the operation is hemorrhaging capital due to ego, outdated tech, or managerial bloat, immediately followed by the exact structural plan required to pivot.

## 2. Pricing & Economics
The price acts as a filter to repel low-intent window shoppers and secure businesses with the capital to actually execute the advised pivot.
*   **The Baseline Strategy Retainer:** $5,000.00 / month.
*   **Scope Boundaries:** This is purely for advisory diagnosis and the architectural mapping of the tech stack / media narrative. *Execution* of that map is not included in the $5k.
*   **Upsell Routing:** If the audited business requires actual execution, they are seamlessly routed back into the HDI ecosystem (e.g. forced onto the $250 DSD Engine tier, or handed a $5,000 Studio C video invoice).

## 3. Operations & Funnel
*   **Positioning:** Chase operates as an independent node attached to the holding company.
*   **Inbound Channel:** The field manual *Outsider Economics* (outsidereconomics.com) acts as the primary top-of-funnel magnet. Readers who consume the philosophy naturally filter up to the consulting tier.
*   **Capacity Limit:** Capped at exactly 3 active consulting clients per quarter to prevent distraction from overarching HDI platform development.
