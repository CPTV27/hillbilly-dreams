# Banking pilot framework — platform data as underwriting signal

**Internal prep only.** Tracy owns institutional relationships. **No specific bank names** in customer materials.

## Thesis

Small businesses already generate **durable signals** inside HDX-class stacks: review integrity, posting cadence, on-prem device heartbeat (“Pi activity”), time-on-platform, and campaign completion. A pilot treats those signals as **supplemental** to cash-flow underwriting — not a replacement for credit files.

## Candidate data points

| Signal | Why it matters | Privacy tier |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| Review velocity + reply latency | Operations discipline | Aggregated + consent |
| Social / listing consistency | Marketing follow-through | Aggregated |
| Sovereign Pi uptime / job success | Physical commitment to stack | Opt-in telemetry |
| DSD tier tenure + payment history | Willingness to pay for ops tooling | Merchant of record |
| Scout audit score trend | Digital hygiene trajectory | With merchant consent |

## Business health score (0–100)

Extends the **Google Business Profile audit** already in production:

- **Critical issues** weight heavier than cosmetic gaps.
- **Trend** (30 / 90 / 180 day) matters more than a snapshot.
- **Manual override** flag for known disasters (flood, fire) — human in the loop.

## Revenue models (pilot stage)

1. **Referral fee** — qualified intro when a loan closes.
2. **Data licensing** — anonymized regional benchmarks (no raw PII).
3. **Subscription subsidy** — bank funds first N months of DSD for approved merchants.

## Regulatory guardrails

- **FCRA:** if scores influence credit decisions, map to permissible purpose + adverse action path — **counsel required** before live.
- **State lending laws:** MS + partner states — usury / broker licensing review.
- **UDAAP:** marketing cannot imply guaranteed approval.

## Entity structure options

- Data stays in **operator LLC** with **subprocessor agreements**.
- Alternatively **new analytics SPV** with bank as limited partner — legal to model with Tracy + counsel.
