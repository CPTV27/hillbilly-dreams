# B2B Field Sales & Onboarding Checklist

This document operationalizes the moment a local business agrees to the $99/mo DSD Marketing Tier following a successful physical walk-in.

## Step 0: The Point of Sale (Field Ops)
Chase requires only his iPhone to execute the full sales loop.
1.  Navigate to `deepsouthdirectory.com/admin/scout/audit/[slug]`.
2.  Review the aggregated data gaps with the owner in real-time.
3.  Execute the close: Secure verbal agreement and swipe a credit card via the Stripe mobile app or a dynamic payment link.

## Day 1: Intake & Capture
Immediate stabilization of the client's raw data.
*   [ ] Run the automated data intake form on the iPad to collect: 
    *   Exact Legal Business Name
    *   Verified Physical Address
    *   Correct Operating Hours
    *   Relevant High-Res Interior/Exterior Photos
*   [ ] Secure proxy credentials or managerial access to their existing Google account/Facebook page.

## Day 2: The Google Core Fix
The single highest-leverage ROI action we can take for a local brick-and-mortar.
*   [ ] Claim or recover the Google Business Profile (GBP).
*   [ ] Standardize Name, Address, and Phone (NAP) parity against what is listed in the DSD directory.
*   [ ] Upload the high-res photos captured on Day 1.

## Day 3: Algorithmic Priming
*   [ ] Deploy the first automated social post via the SocialPublisher matrix, tagging `deepsouthdirectory` to generate immediate local cross-pollination.
*   [ ] Submit the DSD canonical page to search engines for immediate indexation.

## Week 1: Closing the Loop
*   [ ] Activate the Review Monitoring cron job to scrape and aggregate inbound reviews natively to the client's DSD dashboard.
*   [ ] Configure their automated monthly ROI reporting (Pulse Report) via email.

## Week 2: Full Autopilot
*   [ ] The client has been successfully abstracted. Local DSD operations assume full passive management of their localized SEO and directory syncs. The $99 MRR is now cemented infrastructure.
