# Summer 2026 Touring Route Protocol

This document establishes the Big Muddy Touring regional circuit, bridging the Deep South with our Northeast incubation hub.

## 1. Woodstock Activation (Summer)
Targeting the Northeast summer traffic via our primary production partner, Studio C.
*   **Location:** Utopia Studios / Bearsville, NY.
*   **Format:** Ticketed, hyper-intimate "Studio Sessions." Live audience capped to maintain exclusivity, while Studio C multi-tracks and shoots the entire event for wide digital distribution via Big Muddy Radio.
*   **Economics:** Zero venue overhead due to our partnership stake. Primary costs are talent guarantees and technical labor.

## 2. Northeast Leg (Late Summer)
Routing outward from the Woodstock anchor to establish regional density.
*   **Venues (100–300 Cap):** The Colony, Towne Crier (Beacon).
*   **Venues (500+ Cap):** Bearsville Theater, UPAC.
*   **Strategy:** Funnel Americana / Folk talent that draws heavily from the Hudson Valley / Catskills secondary home market.

## 3. The Big Muddy Route (Fall/Winter)
Tracing Highway 61 northward to create an airtight, historically resonant routing schedule for traveling acts.
*   **Stops (South to North):** Natchez → Vicksburg → Clarksdale → Memphis.
*   **Economics:** Securing 100-300 cap rooms in these secondary markets provides bands with consistent, predictable payouts on standard 70/30 door splits or solid guarantees backstopped by our promotional engines.

## 4. Tour Logic & Transportation Budget
Rather than relying on fragmented sedans, Big Muddy Touring standardizes transportation for primary label acts using dedicated Sprinter/Splitter vans.

### Sprinter Economics
*   **Standard Monthly Lease:** $3,000 to $7,500+ per month depending on the configuration (passenger vs custom band splitter).
*   **Mileage Variables:** High-mileage caps are necessary when tracing Natchez to Memphis or pushing up into the Northeast. Overage fees (typically $0.35/mile) must be budgeted.
*   **Per-Show Yield:** To offset a $5,000 monthly Sprinter lease, the act must execute roughly 15 shows a month generating a minimum underlying net of $333 per night *just* to cover the vehicle, before talent guarantees or lodging. This validates the density of the 4-city Big Muddy Route.
