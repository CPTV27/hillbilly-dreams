## 3. Per-Agent Rollout: Strategic Adoption for Maximum Impact

A phased rollout strategy ensures a smooth transition, gathers valuable feedback, and maximizes agent adoption. We recommend a pilot phase followed by a full organizational deployment.

### 3.1 Pilot Program: 5 Agents in 30 Days

The initial 30 days are dedicated to a focused pilot program with a select group of agents. This allows for fine-tuning, gathering agent feedback, and establishing best practices before a broader rollout.

*   **Objectives:**
    *   **Proof of Concept:** Demonstrate the platform's value and ease of use to a small, diverse group of agents.
    *   **Feedback Loop:** Collect direct input from agents on usability, features, and content needs to inform broader training and support.
    *   **Process Refinement:** Identify and iron out any logistical or technical challenges in the agent onboarding process.
    *   **Internal Champions:** Cultivate early adopters who can become advocates and peer mentors for the wider agent base.
*   **Brokerage Admin Steps (Pilot Phase):**
    1.  **Select Pilot Agents:** Choose 5 agents representing a mix of tech-savviness, experience levels, and marketing needs. Ideally, these agents are enthusiastic about adopting new tools.
    2.  **Inform Pilot Agents:** Communicate the purpose and benefits of the pilot program, setting expectations for their involvement and feedback.
    3.  **Invite Agents to MBT:** Using the established tenant creation flow (Section 2.3), send invitations to the 5 pilot agents.
    4.  **Coordinate Initial Training:** Schedule a dedicated initial training session with MBT for these 5 agents (see Section 7.1).
    5.  **Monitor & Support:** Act as the primary point of contact for pilot agents, collecting their feedback and escalating any issues to your MBT Account Manager.
    6.  **Review & Adjust:** Participate in a debrief session with MBT at the end of the 30-day pilot to review findings and refine the rollout plan.

### 3.2 Full Rollout: All Agents in 90 Days

Following a successful pilot, the next 60 days are dedicated to onboarding the remainder of your agents. This accelerated phase leverages the lessons learned from the pilot.

*   **Objectives:**
    *   **Broad Adoption:** Equip all agents with the MBT platform to standardize marketing efforts and elevate the brokerage's overall market presence.
    *   **Maximized ROI:** Ensure the entire agent base is leveraging the platform and the production retainer, maximizing your investment.
    *   **Consistent Branding:** Achieve widespread adoption of your brokerage's white-labeled marketing collateral.
*   **Brokerage Admin Steps (Full Rollout Phase):**
    1.  **Internal Announcement:** Officially announce the MBT platform's availability to all agents, highlighting successes from the pilot and the benefits for every agent. Utilize MBT-provided marketing assets (Section 6).
    2.  **Staggered Invitations:** Invite agents in batches (e.g., 10-20 agents per week) to ensure manageable training and support.
    3.  **Schedule Group Trainings:** Coordinate multiple group training sessions with MBT to accommodate all agents (see Section 7.1).
    4.  **Promote Office Hours:** Encourage agents to attend monthly MBT office hours for ongoing support and Q&A (Section 7.2).
    5.  **Designate Internal Support:** Identify and train an internal "MBT Champion" or a few power users within your brokerage who can provide first-line support to peers.
    6.  **Continuous Monitoring:** Track agent onboarding progress and platform activity through your broker-level dashboard.
    7.  **Feedback Collection:** Continue to gather agent feedback and share it with your MBT Account Manager for ongoing platform optimization and support.
