## 6. TikTok Live Workflow

2.  **Scene Selection:** OBS is pre-configured with multiple scenes. You'll typically use:
    *   **"Performance Stage":** Full-screen view of you performing with a high-quality mic input and instrument input.
    *   **"DJ Set":** Displays album art or a visualizer while playing music, with your webcam inset.
    *   **"Chat Overlay":** A scene that overlays live TikTok chat onto the screen, allowing you to easily interact with comments.
    *   **"BRB / Intermission":** A static graphic indicating a short break.
3.  **Scene Flipping:** Use the pre-configured hotkeys (or click within OBS) to seamlessly transition between scenes during your broadcast. Practice these transitions to ensure a smooth viewer experience.
4.  **Audio Monitoring:** Always wear headphones to monitor your audio mix (vocals, instrument, backing tracks) to prevent feedback and ensure optimal levels. The Mac mini's audio interface (e.g., Focusrite Scarlett) will manage inputs.
5.  **Start Stream:** Once your scene is set and audio levels are good, click "Start Streaming" in OBS. This connects to TikTok Live.
