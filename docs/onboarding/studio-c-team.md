## 2. MBT Bucket-Hours Model

A core operational model for Studio C, particularly concerning our work for the Big Muddy Tuthill (MBT) platform, is the **MBT bucket-hours model**. This system is designed for efficient resource allocation and transparent accounting between the LLC and the various entities it serves.

The MBT platform, along with its specific initiatives like big-muddy-natchez and big-muddy-touring, pre-purchases "buckets" of fungible Studio C hours from Tuthill Design LLC. "Fungible" means that these hours are interchangeable; once purchased, they represent a pool of available production time that can be drawn upon by any of the MBT-related entities as needed. This allows for flexibility in project planning and execution, ensuring that resources are available when creative opportunities arise, without requiring individual project-based invoicing for every piece of content.

**Logging Your Hours:**

## 3. Day-to-Day Workflows

Your daily activities will largely depend on the current project load and your specific role (videographer, editor, production assistant, coordinator). However, the work generally falls into three main categories, each with its own distinct rhythm and requirements:

### Real Estate Shoots (Tuthill Design DBA)

These projects are typically fast-turnaround and highly client-centric. You'll be working to capture properties in their best light, often against tight deadlines.

*   **Pre-Production:**
    *   **Scheduling:** Projects are assigned via Asana. You'll confirm shoot times with agents/clients, ensuring property readiness.
    *   **Briefing:** Review property details, agent requests, and specific selling points. What are the key features to highlight?
    *   **Gear Prep:** Ensure all necessary equipment (camera, lenses, drone, gimbal, lighting, 3D scanner like Matterport) is charged, packed, and ready.
*   **On-Site Production:**
    *   **Photography:** High-resolution interior and exterior shots, often using bracketing for HDR.
    *   **Videography:** Smooth, cinematic walkthroughs, drone footage for aerial views, and neighborhood context.
    *   **3D Tours:** Capturing Matterport or similar scans for immersive virtual tours.
    *   **Client Interaction:** Professionalism is key. You'll often be the direct point of contact with the agent or homeowner.
*   **Post-Production:**
    *   **Ingest & Backup:** Securely transfer all media, backing up to our designated server.
    *   **Editing:**
        *   **Photos:** Lightroom/Photoshop for color correction, exposure blending, perspective correction, sky replacement, and retouching.
        *   **Video:** Premiere Pro for assembly, color grading, stabilization, and adding licensed music.
        *   **3D:** Processing and publishing Matterport tours.
    *   **Delivery:** Uploading final assets to client portals or our delivery platform, adhering to specific file formats and naming conventions. Turnaround times are often 24-48 hours.

### Event/Concert Shoots (Studio C DBA)

These projects often require more extensive planning and execution, capturing the energy and emotion of live performances and gatherings.

*   **Pre-Production:**
    *   **Briefing & Scope:** Understand the event's goals, key moments, and deliverables (e.g., full concert film, highlight reel, social media clips).
    *   **Logistics:** Site visits, power requirements, camera positions, audio feeds, lighting plans.
    *   **Crewing:** Coordinating with additional camera operators, audio engineers, or PAs if needed.
    *   **Gear Prep:** Multi-camera setups, professional audio recording equipment (recorders, mics, DI boxes), specialized lighting, gimbals, and potentially live-streaming equipment.
    *   **Shot List & Run of Show:** Developing a detailed plan for capturing all essential elements.
*   **On-Site Production:**
    *   **Setup:** Rigging cameras, lighting, audio, and monitoring stations.
    *   **Live Capture:** Operating multiple cameras, managing audio levels, directing talent (if applicable), and adapting to the live environment.
    *   **Flexibility:** Events are unpredictable; quick problem-solving and adaptability are crucial.
*   **Post-Production:**
    *   **Ingest & Sync:** Consolidating and synchronizing multi-camera footage and audio.
    *   **Editing:** Crafting a compelling narrative or performance piece, often involving extensive multi-cam editing.
    *   **Color Grading & Audio Mix:** Professional-grade color correction and a clean, dynamic audio mix are essential for concert/event work.
    *   **Motion Graphics/Titles:** Adding branding, lower thirds, or intro/outro sequences.
    *   **Delivery:** Exporting in various formats for web, social media, or broadcast, as per client specifications.

### Platform Work (MBT-Funded via Bucket Hours)

This is the heart of Studio C's creative output, directly feeding the MBT platform. These projects often allow for more creative freedom and deeper storytelling.

*   **Concept & Development:**
    *   **Creative Briefs:** Working closely with Chase and Elijah to understand the vision for artist features, documentaries, or performance pieces.
