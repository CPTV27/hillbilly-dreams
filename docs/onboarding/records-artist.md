### 4. Distribution

Getting your music out there efficiently and transparently is a core service.

*   **How it Works (DistroKid / Buzzsprout):**
    *   **Music Distribution (DistroKid):** We use our established DistroKid account to distribute your music (singles, EPs, albums) to virtually all major digital streaming and download platforms worldwide. You provide us with your high-quality audio files, artwork, and metadata, and we handle the upload, ensuring all technical requirements are met.
    *   **Big Muddy Radio (Buzzsprout):** Any audio content we produce for Big Muddy Radio featuring you (e.g., interviews, special features) is distributed via our Buzzsprout account, reaching podcast platforms like Apple Podcasts, Spotify Podcasts, etc.
*   **Royalty Timing:** Digital service providers (DSPs) and stores typically report sales and streams to distributors on a quarterly or monthly basis, with a significant delay. This means it usually takes **60-90 days** from the end of a reporting period for those royalties to be processed by DistroKid and then by us. For example, streams from January might be reported in April and paid out in May.
*   **Reporting:**
    *   **Monthly Statements:** We will provide you with a clear, itemized monthly statement detailing all royalties generated from your distributed music. This statement will break down earnings by platform and territory where possible.
    *   **Access to Raw Data (Upon Request):** For Gold and Platinum tiers, or upon specific request for Silver, we can provide access to the more granular data directly from DistroKid's analytics portal, allowing you to dive deeper into your performance metrics.
*   **Royalty Payouts:** You will receive **100% of the royalties** generated by your music, after any applicable bank transfer fees. Big Muddy Records does not take a percentage of your distribution royalties. We operate on a subscription model, not a royalty split. Payments will be made directly to your designated bank account via electronic transfer.

### 5. Sync Licensing Pipeline

Opening doors to new revenue streams and wider exposure through sync is a key focus.

*   **When Sync Agencies Look at the Catalog:** Our sync partners (music supervisors, sync agents, production companies) regularly review our catalog for suitable tracks. They often work on specific briefs for upcoming projects (film, TV, advertising, video games, trailers, podcasts). We also proactively pitch tracks that we believe have strong sync potential, even without a specific brief.
*   **What We Pitch:** We pitch tracks that are well-produced, professionally mixed and mastered, and have clear, compelling emotional content. Instrumental versions are highly valuable. We'll discuss with you which tracks from your catalog are best suited for sync, taking into account genre, mood, lyrical content, and production quality. We always ensure your music is properly tagged and metadata-rich for easy discovery.
*   **What Fees Big Muddy Takes:** Should your music be successfully placed in a sync opportunity we secure, the sync license fee is typically split. After any third-party sync agency fees (which are standard in the industry and can range from 10-25% of the gross fee), Big Muddy Records will take a **25% administration fee** of the remaining net sync fee. The artist will receive **75%** of the remaining net sync fee. We will always seek your explicit approval for any sync deal before it is finalized, ensuring you are comfortable with the terms and usage.
