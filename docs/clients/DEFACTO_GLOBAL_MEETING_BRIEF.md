## What deFacto Global Actually Is

**NOT what we assumed.** deFacto Global is an **enterprise financial planning platform** (Integrated Business Planning / FP&A). Their clients are AT&T, Disney/Hulu, PBS, Goodwill, Warburg Pincus. They replace SAP BPC and Oracle Hyperion, not small-business spreadsheets.

- **HQ:** Troy, New York
- **Team:** ~15 people
- **Revenue:** ~$1.3M estimated
- **Stack:** 100% Microsoft-native (Excel, Power BI, Dynamics 365, Azure, Copilot)
- **Sweet spot:** $50M-$1B+ revenue companies, 100-1000+ employees
- **Pricing:** Enterprise contracts, likely $50K-$200K+/year

**The "spreadsheet" connection is real but different than we thought:** deFacto lets enterprise users keep working in Excel while connecting it to a centralized database with formulas, security, and business rules. "Keep using Excel. We just make it not break."

## What NOT to Do
