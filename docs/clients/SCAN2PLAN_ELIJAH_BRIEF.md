## Why This Works

**For them:**
- Zero risk. They pay nothing until they see value.
- No vendor lock-in fear. They define the exit criteria.
- They control the economics. Nobody's going to surprise them with a price increase.

**For us:**
- Real production data proves the product.
- Customer-defined pricing tells us what the market will bear — better than any market research.
- A working deployment is the best sales demo for the next customer.
- If they won't pay after seeing results, the product isn't good enough — and we should know that.

**The logic:**
The product is built. The marginal cost of one more deployment is near zero. The alternative is the codebase sitting in an archive folder doing nothing. Every day it sits there, the technology gets staler and the market moves on. A free deployment that converts to paid is infinitely better than a paid pitch that never closes.
