## Core Promise: "Zero-IT Infrastructure"

The unifying thread across every tier is friction elimination. The customer doesn't string together Stripe, Mailchimp, Asana, and ChatGPT. They plug into Hillbilly Dreams, and the AI agent syndicate (AG, CC, PC) handles the routing in the dark.

One engine. Three scales. Zero employees.

---

## Stage 1: The Solo Operator (Entry Level)

- **Product:** KioskMode Pro ($99/mo)
- **Target:** Independent contractor, single-location retailer, solo creative
- **Experience:** Turnkey. iPad locked to our ecosystem. AI Concierge, basic CRM, payment routing. Kills $658/mo in software bloat.
- **Hook:** *"You don't need an IT department; you just need to turn on the screen."*

## Stage 2: The Local Ecosystem (Mid-Market / SMB)

- **Product:** Operator Dashboards (Big Muddy Touring, Deep South Directory, BuyCurious)
- **Target:** Venues, hospitality, local hubs (Tracy and Amy running Big Muddy Inn)
- **Experience:** Multi-player mode. Role-based access, inventory, vendor/artist applications, automated marketing. Interfaces with Stage 1 (KioskMode iPads at the front desk).
- **Hook:** *"The operating system for physical spaces and cultural hubs."*

## Stage 3: The Industrial Engine (Enterprise)

- **Product:** S2PX ($134K/year Licensing)
- **Target:** High-volume service businesses (Scan2Plan, Twinner)
- **Experience:** Raw, unfiltered engine. Custom Vertex AI models, 106-table databases, 3D spatial data. Dedicated GCP clusters, full white-labeling — still running on the exact same Hillbilly Dreams bloodline as the $99/mo iPad.
- **Hook:** *"Silicon Valley infrastructure, built for Main Street reality."*

---

## The "Under One Roof" Magic

Because the backend is unified (Stripe Connect platform splits, GCP Cloud Run, shared AI schemas), the upgrade path is invisible:

1. A local florist buys **Stage 1** (KioskMode) to run her checkout.
2. She grows, opens a venue, clicks a button to upgrade to **Stage 2** (BMT/Directory) to manage events. Her data is already there.
3. She franchises to 50 locations, we flip the switch to **Stage 3** (Enterprise), building her a custom S2PX-style portal.

**She never migrates. She just unlocks more of the Engine.**
