## Three Deployment Vectors (One Platform)

### 1. Universal Web App (Zero Friction)
- **What:** The PWA built tonight
- **How:** Type a URL on any browser, log in, the terminal becomes a Hillbilly Dreams dashboard
- **Pitch:** *"If it has a screen and Wi-Fi, it runs your business."*

### 2. BYOD App (Bring Your Own Device)
- **What:** Native iOS/Android app
- **How:** Tracy downloads the app on her iPhone. Toggle **Operator Mode** → UI shifts from consumer to enterprise. Full venue command from her pocket.

### 3. True Kiosk (Cloud Takeover)
- **What:** Dedicated terminal running nothing but Hillbilly Dreams OS
- **Critical constraint:** Apple's Automated Device Enrollment (ADE) requires devices purchased through an authorized reseller linked to your Apple Business Manager (ABM) instance. You CANNOT remote-lock a random Best Buy iPad.
- **Solution — The Vendor Link:**
  1. Partner with CDW, Best Buy Business, or Apple Authorized Reseller
  2. Devices ship pre-enrolled in Hillbilly Dreams ABM instance
  3. Client unboxes → connects to Wi-Fi → cloud instantly locks device
  4. Zero IT. Zero USB cables. Zero phone support.
- **The Judo Move:** Toast, Square, and Lightspeed manufacture proprietary hardware. You hijack Apple's existing enterprise supply chain. Client buys the glass; you own the soul.
- **Affiliate bonus:** B2B affiliate link = tiny hardware commission on top of $99/mo SaaS

> **Marketing Copy (for kioskmode.html):**
> *"Order your iPad through our certified vendor link. It arrives pre-enrolled in our enterprise management system. Unbox it, connect to Wi-Fi, and the cloud instantly locks the device into a dedicated Hillbilly Dreams terminal. Zero IT required."*
