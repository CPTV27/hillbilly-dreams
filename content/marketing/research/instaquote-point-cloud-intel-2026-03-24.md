## Critical Finding: Never Use "Commission" in UI

**Never use the word "commission" in the UI.** Experienced contractors immediately calculate what a 3% fee costs against their 5–10% net margin and reject it.

### The Reframe
This is a **risk allocation dial**, not a commission — the same mental model as a fixed-price vs. time-and-materials contract, which every contractor already understands fluently. Let them set it and they'll feel like they negotiated a deal.

### Slider Anchoring
- **Max: 2.5%** for existing client revenue
- **Reserve 3%** exclusively for jobs generated by the platform's inbound funnel
- This distinction between "your existing clients" and "new clients we brought you" justifies the higher rate cleanly

## Point Cloud Module — "The Receipt You Scan the Job With"

### Positioning (External Copy)
Strip all technical language (LiDAR, point cloud, spatial compute) from external marketing. The buyer needs the **output**, not the mechanism:

> *"You ordered 340 linear feet of copper pipe. The scan shows 290 installed. Here's your $3,200 discrepancy."*

### Hard Statistics
- **10–15%** material waste per project
- **$6,000** average theft loss per incident
- **Under 25%** recovery rate on construction theft
- On a $400K job: **$16,000–$24,000** walking out the door before anyone notices

### Pricing Implication
At these loss numbers, the $1,500/mo Spatial Node tier sells itself. The module pays for itself on the first scan of the first month.
