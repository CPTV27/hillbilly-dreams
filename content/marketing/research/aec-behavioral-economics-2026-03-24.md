## Key Findings

### The Aha! Moment
- Products achieving Aha! within 5 minutes show **40% higher 30-day retention**
- The Aha! cannot be a dashboard — it has to be **a number they've never known**
- QBO users in construction stop logging in because P&L reports are meaningless without WIP analysis
- **The unlock:** Show them their real cash position mapped against outstanding invoices — a number QBO can't produce

### Copy Rules (Research-Backed)
- **Loss framing beats gain framing 2:1** — don't say "grow your business," say "manual processes are draining $35K–$65K/year from contractors your size"
- **$99 is neurologically painless** — digital subscriptions distance buyers from cost; anchor against something they already pay (one hour of accountant time, one tank of diesel)
- **62% of contractors are financially anxious even when profitable** — the emotional core is *clarity*, not growth

### Hard Statistics for Copy
- Subcontractors wait an average of **56 days** for payment
- Small business owners spend **5–15 hours per week** on bookkeeping alone
- Construction industry loses **$177B annually** to paper-based productivity gaps
- Field foremen spend **6 hours/week** on paperwork

### Pricing Psychology
- Annual lock-in with 20% discount prevents seasonal churn (construction shuts down in winter)
- Seat-based pricing caps growth — use outcome-based or flat tiers
- "At-cost compute passthrough" builds trust (0% markup framing)
- Free tier should give exactly 3 diagnostic runs (enough to prove value, not enough to freeload)
