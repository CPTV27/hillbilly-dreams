# The Porch — Episode 1: What Is Deep South Directory?

## Characters
- CHASE: Founder. Direct, practical, uses real numbers.
- DAWN: Co-host. Asks the questions regular people ask.

## Script

DAWN: So Chase, I keep hearing about this Deep South Directory thing. What is it — like a phone book?

CHASE: It is kind of like a phone book, actually. But one that fixes your Google listing, posts to your social media, responds to your reviews, and has an AI that already knows your business. For ninety-nine bucks a month.

DAWN: Ninety-nine. That is cheap. What is everybody else charging?

CHASE: Birdeye charges two ninety-nine. Podium charges three ninety-nine. Thryv charges two forty-four. And none of them have ever been to Mississippi.

DAWN: So what do you actually do for ninety-nine dollars?

CHASE: First thing — I fix your Google listing. Wrong hours, no photos, zero reviews. That is the number one reason people drive past your restaurant and go to the competitor. We fix that on day one. Then we set up social posting — a few posts a week going out automatically. Then we monitor your reviews. When someone leaves a review, the AI drafts a response. You approve it or edit it. Done.

DAWN: That sounds like a full-time job.

CHASE: It is a full-time job. That is why businesses pay a thousand dollars a month for it. We do it for ninety-nine because we built the system once and run everyone through it.

DAWN: And what is this little computer thing I keep hearing about?

CHASE: The Sovereign Pi. It is a small computer — fits in your hand — that we put on your counter. It runs your dashboard, shows your menu on the TV, answers customer questions. And it works without the internet.

DAWN: Without the internet?

CHASE: Without the internet. It has your business data loaded on it. If your WiFi goes down, if the power goes out, if you are running a food truck at a festival — the Pi keeps working. Battery pack lasts six hours. Solar panel keeps it going indefinitely.

DAWN: And it is free?

CHASE: Free with any paid subscription. Twenty-five a month and up. We finance the hardware into the subscription. Like a phone from your carrier.

DAWN: So let me get this straight. For less than a hundred dollars a month, I get my Google listing fixed, social media handled, reviews responded to, and a computer on my counter. What is the catch?

CHASE: The catch is that we are a small team in Natchez, Mississippi, and we can only take on so many businesses right now. But we are from here. I ate at Biscuits and Blues last Tuesday. I know the Anthologist has live music on Fridays. The companies charging three hundred a month have never set foot in Port Gibson.

DAWN: You know what, I think every business owner in Natchez needs to hear this. If somebody is listening and they want to try it — what do they do?

CHASE: Go to deepsouthdirectory.com. Sign up for free. We will take it from there.

DAWN: Deep South Directory. Your digital hygiene — handled. For less than your phone bill.
