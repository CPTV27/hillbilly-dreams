FINDER AGREEMENT
  
THIS AGREEMENT, made and entered into as of __________2008, by and between MHN Management Mike Nelson and Maria Infantine (Finder) and Chase Pierson (Artist), aka ‘Mechanical Bull’  with respect to the following facts: 
A. Artist owns, controls or otherwise has the right to play, produce, license or assign musical work under ‘Mechanical Bull’ ("Music").  All lyrics and musical compositions written by Chase Pierson (Artist). 
B. Finder is a company engaged in marketing and distribution activities in the music /television / motion picture business. 
C. The parties intend to enter an agreement whereby Finder will introduce Artist to third parties (herein collectively referred to as the "Financier" or "Financiers"), who may be interested in lending for, investing in, or in any other way financing all or a portion of development, production and/or distribution of the aforementioned ‘Music’. 
WHEREFORE, for good and valuable consideration, the parties agree as follows: 
1. SERVICES; TERM: Commencing on the date hereof, and continuing for two (2) years with a one (1) year extension upon mutual agreement of all parties, until the earlier of (a) written termination by either party of this Agreement, or (b) written amendment of this Agreement to include exclusive management services not specified herein upon mutual agreement of all parties.  Finder shall use its best efforts to introduce Artist to parties who may be interested in financing, investing, promoting or lending money with respect to the production, distribution, licensing of the “Music” or otherwise in becoming a Financier to promote Artist in all capacities listed herein. The foregoing period of time is hereinafter referred to as the "Term."   
2. COMPENSATION: If at any time during the Term or within 12 months of the termination of this Agreement, Artist enters into any agreement with any Financier (introduced to Artist by Finder) to invest in, lend for, or finance production, licensing, marketing and/or distribution of said “Music”, then Finder shall receive an amount equal to ten (10) percent of the amount of any Gross Funds, defined as all residual payments, credits or other consideration paid or lent by Financiers to Artist and used by Artist in the development and Production, Licensing, Marketing or Distribution of the “Music” provided, that the amounts paid to Finder shall not exceed a total of 10%.  Finder shall receive his Commission within ten business days of when Artist has the right to use the amounts provided by financier(s).   NOTE:  Finder shall also receive 10% net monies on all merchandise that Artist may sell directly or authorize someone to produce and sell that may contain Artist’s name or likeness including any and all product endorsements Artist may acquire during the stated term hereof provided same are related to the “Music.”  Finder shall not receive any merchandising commissions until all reasonable merchandise costs are recouped by Artist.  * Credit will be given to MHN Management for any promotional appearances / tools / marketing directly related to MHN Management efforts.
3.  EXPENSES:  Artist agrees to reimburse Finder currently for any and all reasonable and customary expenses which Finder may incur on Artist’s behalf or on Artist’s account, including but not limited to those in connection with postal correspondence, long distance telephone calls, telegrams, publicity materials, and travel expenses where deemed necessary.  Finder will not incur any single expense in excess of one hundred dollars ($100) without Artist’s prior approval. Aggregate expense balance cannot exceed $1000 without express approval of Artist.
4. NO OBLIGATION: Nothing in this agreement shall obligate Artist to enter into an agreement with any Financiers. 
5. NO SALE OF SECURITIES: Finder agrees not to sell or offer to sell securities related to investing in the development and/or production of the “Music”.  Finder agrees to indemnify and hold Artist harmless from all damage and expense (including reasonable attorneys' fees) upon a breach or claim of breach of this provision. 
6. RELATIONSHIP OF THE PARTIES: Finder is an independent contractor and shall not act as an employee, agent or broker of Artist.  It is understood and agreed by both parties that MHN Management contact information will be given for all promotions related to the four (4) songs.  NOTE:  This is not an exclusive Management Agreement.  This is an agreement to be construed at Artist’s request to be transactional in nature strictly on a deal by deal basis.  Upon mutual agreement, this contract may be renegotiated to include exclusive management services.
7. FINDER'S REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES: 
Finder represents and warrants to Artist that the following statements are true and correct in all respects: 
A. Finder is in the business of arranging marketing, merchandising and international distribution of  music / television shows / motion pictures, has substantial experience in said business, is not insolvent or in any danger of insolvency or bankruptcy, and is not in dissolution proceedings. 
B. Finder has the full and complete authority to enter into this agreement and there are no outstanding claims or litigation pending against Finder. 
8. ADDITIONAL DOCUMENTS: Finder agrees to execute, acknowledge and deliver to Artist and to procure the execution, acknowledgment and delivery to Artist of any additional documents or instruments which Artist may reasonably require to fully effectuate and carry out the intent and purposes of this agreement. 
9. ARBITRATION: Any controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this Agreement or the validity, construction or performance of this Agreement or the breach thereof, shall be resolved by arbitration according to the rules and procedures of the American Arbitration Association, as they may be amended. Such rules and procedures are incorporated herein and made a part of this Agreement by reference. The parties agree that they will abide by and perform any award rendered in any such arbitration and that any court having jurisdiction may issue a judgment based upon the award. Moreover, the prevailing party shall be entitled to reimbursement of reasonable attorney fees and costs. 
10. ASSIGNMENT: Finder shall not have the right to assign this agreement or any part hereof. 
11. SECTION HEADINGS: The headings of paragraphs, sections and other subdivisions of this agreement are for convenient reference only. They shall not be used in any way to govern, limit, modify, construe this agreement or any part or provision thereof or otherwise be given any legal effect. 
12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT: This agreement contains the full and complete understanding and agreement between the parties with respect to the within subject matter, and supersedes all other agreements between the parties whether written or oral relating thereto, and may not be modified or amended except by written instrument executed by both of the parties hereto. This agreement shall in all respects be subject to the laws of the State of New York applicable to agreements executed and wholly performed within such State.
