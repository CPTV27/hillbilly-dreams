# JP's Guide — Shows & Programming

Welcome, JP. You're the reason people walk through the door. The music is the engine.

## Your Responsibilities

- **Booking acts** — Finding, contacting, and confirming artists
- **Sound & stage** — Making sure the room sounds and looks right
- **Scheduling** — Show dates, times, and coordination with Amy and Chase
- **Promotion** — Working with the Content Studio to get the word out

## The Flywheel

Shows generate the audience. The audience generates the bar revenue. The bar revenue funds more shows. Your job is to keep the flywheel spinning.

**Target:** At least 12 show nights per month. Each show night generates $300-500 in bar revenue (Amy's department) plus the 2:1 ecosystem multiplier through downstream effects (rooms, directory, magazine).

## Quick Reference

- **Coordinate with Amy** on bar timing for each show
- **Coordinate with Chase** on promotion (at least 2 weeks before the show)
- **Sound check:** Always. No exceptions.
- **Photos:** Get someone to shoot the show. Every show. Drop photos in the [Photo Workflow](/getting-started/photo-workflow).
