# Booking Acts

How to find, contact, and confirm artists for the Big Muddy.

## Finding Acts

### Where to Look
- **Local scene:** Musicians you already know in Natchez, Vicksburg, Jackson, Baton Rouge, New Orleans
- **Regional touring:** Acts passing through the Mississippi corridor (Memphis to New Orleans)
- **Social media:** Instagram and Facebook are where most musicians post their tour dates
- **Other venues:** Check who's playing at Ground Zero (Clarksdale), Cat Head Delta Blues (Clarksdale), Teddy's Juke Joint (Baton Rouge)
- **Word of mouth:** The best bookings come from "You should talk to..."

### What Fits the Big Muddy
- Blues, roots, Americana, soul, folk, jazz
- Solo artists and small combos (stage size matters)
- Artists who engage the room, not just perform at it
- Touring acts looking for a Wednesday or Thursday stop between bigger gigs

## Making Contact

### The Outreach Template

```
Subject: Show at the Big Muddy Inn — [Date]

Hey [Name],

I'm JP at the Big Muddy Inn in Natchez, Mississippi. We run
a listening room with [capacity] and a full bar. We're booking
[month] and I think you'd be a great fit.

Interested in [proposed date]? We can do [deal structure —
door split, guarantee, etc.].

Here's what we look like: [link to bigmuddytouring.com]

Let me know,
JP
```

### What to Offer
- **Door split:** 70/30 or 80/20 in the artist's favor is standard for a small room
- **Guarantee + door:** Minimum guarantee (e.g., $200) plus a share of the door is more attractive to touring acts
- **Lodging:** We have rooms. Offering a free night is a huge sweetener that costs us relatively little.

## Confirming the Booking

### Get These in Writing (Text or Email is Fine)
- [ ] Date and time (doors, set time, expected end)
- [ ] Deal terms (guarantee, split, lodging)
- [ ] Technical needs (how many instruments, any special gear)
- [ ] Promo materials (press photo, bio, links)
- [ ] Deposit requirements (if any)

### Add to the Calendar
- Tell Chase the date, artist, and time so he can add it to the website
- Tell Amy so she can plan bar operations
- Add it to your Asana board

## After the Show

1. **Pay the artist** — Same night. Don't make them chase you.
2. **Get a photo together** — This is content for the website and their socials.
3. **Thank them publicly** — Tag them on social media.
4. **Log the show in Asana** — How'd it go? Would we book them again? How was the crowd?
5. **Drop all photos** in the [Photo Workflow](/getting-started/photo-workflow).
