# Compliance

The boring stuff that keeps us legal. Do it right and nobody thinks about it. Miss something and everything stops.

## Liquor License

### Status
- **Type:** On-premise (check the specific license class)
- **Where it's posted:** Must be visibly displayed at the bar
- **Renewal:** Check the expiration date — renewals take 30-90 days

### What to Watch For
- License must be current before every show night
- If we change anything about the business structure, the license may need updating
- Keep a copy of the license in a safe place (not just the one on the wall)

### If There's a Problem
Contact the MS Department of Revenue, Alcohol Beverage Control Division. Or text Chase immediately.

## IRS Payment Plan

### The Agreement
- **What:** Installment agreement for 2020 tax liability
- **Amount:** $100/month
- **Due date:** By the 28th of each month
- **How to pay:** IRS Direct Pay at [irs.gov/payments](https://www.irs.gov/payments)

### Monthly Checklist
1. Make the payment by the 25th (3-day buffer before the 28th deadline)
2. Save the confirmation number
3. Mark the Asana task as complete
4. If payment fails or there's an issue, **tell Chase immediately** — missing an installment can void the agreement

## Entity Filings

### Mississippi Secretary of State
- **Annual Report:** Due April 15 each year
- **Filing fee:** $0 (Mississippi doesn't charge)
- **Where to file:** [sos.ms.gov/business-services](https://www.sos.ms.gov/business-services)
- **What to update:** Registered agent, principal address, members/managers

### EIN Tracking
Chase maintains the entity registry with all EINs and formation documents. If you need an EIN for a bank form or tax document, check with Chase.

## Record Keeping

### What to Keep
- All tax correspondence (IRS letters, state notices)
- Liquor license copies and renewal receipts
- Bank statements (reconcile monthly)
- Insurance policies and certificates
- All government filings and confirmation numbers

### Where to Keep It
- Digital copies in Google Drive (shared with Chase)
- Paper originals in the office filing cabinet
- Tax documents also backed up in the financial database (`~/tax-db/`)
