# Tracy's Guide — Business & Finance

Welcome, Tracy. You keep the numbers honest and the paperwork current. Without you, nothing else works.

## Your Responsibilities

- **Financial oversight** — Review dashboards, flag anything that looks wrong
- **Compliance** — Liquor license, IRS payments, entity filings
- **Bookkeeping coordination** — Make sure transactions are tracked

## Your Tools

| Tool | What | Where |
|---|---|---|
| Asana | Your task list | Asana app on your phone |
| Tracy Dashboard | Financial overview built for you | `bigmuddytouring.com/tracy` |

## Your Guides

## Key Deadlines

| What | When | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| IRS installment payment | 28th of every month | $100/month, don't miss it |
| MS Annual Report | April 15 | $0 filing fee, file at sos.ms.gov |
| Liquor license renewal | Check expiration date | Must be current for show nights |

## Quick Reference

- **If numbers look wrong:** Flag it in Asana or text Chase
- **If a payment is due:** Don't wait. Pay it early.
- **If you're not sure about something:** Ask. Better to ask than to guess on compliance.
