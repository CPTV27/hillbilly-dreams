# Show Night Checklist

Print this out or keep it on your phone. Run through it every show night.

## Before the Show (4:00 PM)

- [ ] **Stock the bar** — Full bottles up front, backups underneath
- [ ] **Ice** — Fill all ice bins. Get extra bags if it's a big night.
- [ ] **Check with JP** — What time is sound check? What time do doors open?
- [ ] **Cash drawer** — Start with $100 in small bills ($1s, $5s, $10s)
- [ ] **Glasses/cups** — Clean, stacked, ready
- [ ] **Garnishes** — Limes cut, lemons ready, olives stocked
- [ ] **Trash/recycling** — Empty bins, fresh bags
- [ ] **Bathrooms** — Clean, stocked with paper and soap
- [ ] **Tables & chairs** — Set up for the expected crowd
- [ ] **Lights & ambiance** — Stage lights on, bar lights dim

## Doors Open (Usually 7:00 PM)

- [ ] **Music playing** — Background music until the act starts
- [ ] **Square reader charged** and connected
- [ ] **Wristbands/stamps** ready (if needed for age verification)
- [ ] **Tip jar** out and visible
- [ ] **Greet the first guests** — First impression matters

## During the Show

- [ ] **Keep the bar moving** — Speed over perfection
- [ ] **Watch stock levels** — Switch to backup bottles before you run out
- [ ] **Water for the band** — Keep it stocked on stage
- [ ] **Keep an eye on the room** — Too crowded? Too hot? Handle it before it's a problem.

## After the Show

- [ ] **Last call** — 30 minutes before closing
- [ ] **Close out all tabs** — No open tabs left behind
- [ ] **Count the drawer** — Cash + Square receipts = total take
- [ ] **Record the numbers** — Total sales, cash, card, tips
- [ ] **Clean the bar** — Wipe down, wash glasses, restock what you can
- [ ] **Take out trash** — Don't leave it for tomorrow
- [ ] **Lock up** — All doors, all windows, all lights off except security
- [ ] **Text Chase** — "Show night done. Sold $___." One text is all he needs.

## After a Great Night

Take a photo of the crowd (even if it's just the aftermath). Drop it in the [Photo Workflow](/getting-started/photo-workflow). This is content gold.
