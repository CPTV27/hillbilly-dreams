# How Do I Add a Business to the Directory?

## Steps

1. **Go to the onboarding page** — Open `/directory/onboard` in your browser.
2. **Fill in the business details** — Enter the business name, address, phone number, description, and any photos or links.
3. **Double-check everything** — Make sure the info is accurate. This is what people will see in the Deep South Directory.
4. **Submit** — Hit the Submit button. The listing will go through for review and then appear in the directory.
