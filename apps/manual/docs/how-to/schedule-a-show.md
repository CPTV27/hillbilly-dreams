# How Do I Schedule a Show?

## Steps

1. **Add the show to Asana** — Go to JP's board in Asana and create a new task for the show. Include the artist name, date, time, and any notes about the booking.
2. **Confirm the details** — Make sure the date, time, door price, and artist info are all locked in before you start promoting.
3. **Generate promo materials** — Head to `/admin/studio` and enter the show details. The studio will create a poster, social posts, and a radio spot for you.
4. **Approve and share** — Review everything, approve what looks good, and start sharing the promo across your channels.
