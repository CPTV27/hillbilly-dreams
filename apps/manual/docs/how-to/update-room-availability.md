# How Do I Update Room Availability?

## Steps

1. **Log into CloudBeds** — Go to [cloudbeds.com](https://cloudbeds.com) and sign in with your account.
2. **Open the calendar** — Navigate to the availability calendar for the Inn.
3. **Update the dates** — Click on the room and dates you need to change. Block off dates, open them up, or adjust rates as needed.
4. **Save your changes** — Make sure you hit Save. CloudBeds will sync the availability to all your booking channels automatically.
