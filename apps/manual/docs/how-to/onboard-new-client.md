# How Do I Onboard a New Client?

## Steps

1. **Send them the onboarding link** — Give the new client the link to `/directory/onboard` so they can fill in their own business details.
2. **Let them fill out the form** — They'll enter their business name, description, photos, contact info, and everything else they need in the directory.
3. **Send the Stripe payment link** — Once they've submitted their info, send them the Stripe link for their chosen tier so they can pay.
4. **Confirm everything** — Check that their listing looks good and their payment went through. Welcome them to the family.
