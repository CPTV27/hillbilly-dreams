# How Do I Check Arrivals?

## Steps

1. **Check the dashboard** — Go to `/admin/dashboard` in your browser for a quick occupancy number.
2. **Or go to CloudBeds** — Log into CloudBeds at [cloudbeds.com](https://cloudbeds.com) for the full reservation list.
3. **Look at today's date** — The dashboard or calendar will show you who's arriving today, who's already checked in, and who's departing.

That's it — just three steps.
