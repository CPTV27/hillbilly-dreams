# How Do I Add Photos to an Article?

## Steps

1. **Open the Media Vault** — Go to `/admin/media` in your browser.
2. **Find the photo you need** — Search by filename, tag, or just scroll through. You can use the search bar at the top.
3. **Copy the photo URL** — Click on the photo, then copy the URL it gives you.
4. **Open your article in the editor** — Go back to the article you're working on.
5. **Paste the URL into the image field** — Put your cursor where you want the photo and paste the URL. Save the article.
