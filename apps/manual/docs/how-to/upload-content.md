# How Do I Upload Content?

## Steps

1. **Go to the upload page** — Open `/admin/upload` in your browser.
2. **Drag your files into the drop zone** — Drag and drop photos, videos, or documents right into the upload area on the page.
3. **Or use the Mac drop folder** — You can also drop files into `~/BigMuddy-Dropbox/photos/` on the Mac and they'll get picked up automatically.
4. **Wait for the upload to finish** — You'll see a progress indicator. Once it's done, your files are in the Media Vault and ready to use.
