# How Do I Write an Article?

## Steps

1. **Go to the Content Studio** — Open `/admin/studio` in your browser.
2. **Pick "Magazine Feature"** — From the content type list, select Magazine Feature.
3. **Type in your topic** — Tell it what the article should be about. A sentence or two is fine, or you can write a whole outline if you want.
4. **Review and approve** — Read through the draft it generates. Make any edits you want, then approve it.
5. **Publish** — Hit the Publish button. It goes live on the magazine site.
