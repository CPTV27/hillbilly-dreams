# How Do I Make a Poster?

## Steps

1. **Go to the Content Studio** — Open your browser and head to `/admin/studio` on the Big Muddy site.
2. **Pick "Poster" as your format** — You'll see a list of content types. Choose Poster (or Event Flyer).
3. **Type in the event details** — Enter the show name, date, time, artists, and any other info you want on the poster. The more detail you give, the better it looks.
4. **Review the design** — The studio will generate a poster design for you. Look it over and make sure everything is right.
5. **Export from Canva** — Click the export button to open the design in Canva. From there you can download it as a PNG or PDF to print or share online.
