# What Can Delta Dawn Do?

Delta Dawn is your AI assistant at `/ops/chat`. She has context across the Big Muddy ecosystem—the Inn, the bar, shows, directory, magazine, radio, and touring. Replies are **warm, precise, and professional** (hospitality-grade, not slang-heavy). Here's what she can help with:

- **Draft social media posts** — Tell her the topic and she'll write posts ready to copy and share.
- **Respond to guest reviews** — Paste a review and she'll draft a warm, professional response.
- **Check occupancy** — Ask her how many rooms are booked tonight, this weekend, or any date range.
- **Suggest show topics** — Need ideas for themed nights or programming? Just ask.
- **Create marketing briefs** — She can put together a brief for any campaign or event.
- **Generate poster concepts** — Describe what you need and she'll help you get started.
- **Explain the mastering chain** — If you ever need to understand the audio setup, she knows it.
- **Track revenue** — Ask about bookings, directory income, or how the month is looking.
- **Connect dots across all 7 brands** — She sees the whole picture and can help you think through how things fit together.

## How to Use Her

Just go to `/ops/chat` and type your question. No special format needed — talk to her like you'd talk to a coworker.
