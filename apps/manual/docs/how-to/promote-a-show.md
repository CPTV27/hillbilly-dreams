# How Do I Promote a Show?

## Steps

1. **Go to the Content Studio** — Open `/admin/studio` in your browser.
2. **Enter the show details** — Type in the artist name, date, time, venue, ticket price, and anything else that matters.
3. **Let it generate everything** — The studio will create social media posts, a radio spot script, and a magazine feature all at once.
4. **Review and approve** — Look through each piece. Edit anything that needs tweaking, then approve them.
5. **Publish and share** — Post the social content (via Postiz or manually), hand the radio script to the DJ, and publish the magazine feature.
