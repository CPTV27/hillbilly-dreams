# How Do I Create a Social Post?

## Steps

1. **Go to the Content Studio** — Open `/admin/studio` in your browser.
2. **Pick "Social Posts"** — Select Social Posts from the content type list.
3. **Type in your topic** — Describe what you want to post about. Include any details like dates, names, or hashtags you want.
4. **Review and approve** — Look over the posts it generates. Edit anything that doesn't sound right.
5. **Copy to Postiz or post manually** — Copy the text and paste it into Postiz to schedule it, or post it directly to your social accounts.
