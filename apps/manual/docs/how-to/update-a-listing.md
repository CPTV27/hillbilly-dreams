# How Do I Update a Listing?

## Steps

1. **Go to the client list** — Open `/admin/clients` in your browser.
2. **Find the business** — Search or scroll to find the business you need to update.
3. **Click to edit** — Open their profile and make your changes — update the description, swap photos, fix the phone number, whatever's needed.
4. **Save** — Hit Save. The changes will show up in the directory right away.
