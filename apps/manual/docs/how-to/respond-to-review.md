# How Do I Respond to a Review?

## Steps

1. **Check your reviews** — Go to `/ops/reviews` in your browser to see recent guest reviews.
2. **Pick the review you want to respond to** — Click on it to open it up.
3. **Write your response (or let Delta Dawn help)** — You can write your own reply, or go to `/ops/chat` and ask Delta Dawn: "Help me respond to this review" and paste the review text. She'll draft a warm, professional response for you.
4. **Review the response** — Make sure it sounds like you. Adjust the tone if needed.
5. **Post it** — Copy the response and post it on the review platform (Google, TripAdvisor, etc.).
