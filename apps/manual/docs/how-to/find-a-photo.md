# How Do I Find a Photo?

## Steps

1. **Open the Media Vault** — Go to `/admin/media` in your browser. This is where all our photos live.
2. **Search for what you need** — Use the search bar to look by filename, tag, or keyword. For example, try "porch" or "show night" or "logo."
3. **Browse the results** — Scroll through the results and click on any photo to see it full-size.
4. **Copy or download** — Copy the photo URL to use it in an article, or download it to your computer.
