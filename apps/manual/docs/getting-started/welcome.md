# Welcome

This is your cheat sheet for running the Big Muddy. Everything you need to know, step by step.

## Who Does What

- **Amy** — Inn & bar operations. Inventory, pricing, guest experience, show nights.
- **Tracy** — Business & finance. Bookkeeping, compliance, liquor license, financial dashboards.
- **JP** — Shows & programming. Booking acts, sound/stage, scheduling, promotion.
- **Chase** — Tech, sales, and media partnerships. If something's broken or needs building, text Chase.

## Your Tools

These are the shared tools everyone uses:

| Tool | What It Does | Where |
|---|---|---|
| [Photo Workflow](/getting-started/photo-workflow) | Get photos onto the website | Drop files in a folder |
| [Content Studio](/getting-started/content-studio) | Generate social posts, articles, radio spots | `/admin/studio` |
| [Edit Mode](/getting-started/edit-mode) | Edit any page on the website visually | `/api/draft?enable=1` |
| [Asana](/getting-started/asana) | Your task list | Asana app on your phone |
