# Content Studio

The Content Studio generates social media posts, magazine articles, radio spots, and review responses — all from one screen.

## Where to Find It

Go to: `bigmuddytouring.com/admin/studio`

## How It Works

1. **Pick what you're writing about:**
   - A business name (e.g., "Fat Mama's Tamales")
   - A show event (e.g., "JP Houston, March 29, Big Muddy Inn")
   - A topic (e.g., "Spring on the Natchez Trace")
   - A photo description (e.g., "Sunset over the Mississippi from the bluff")

2. **Click Generate**

3. **The system creates content for 4 channels:**
   - Social media posts (Instagram/Facebook/Twitter)
   - Magazine feature article
   - Radio spot script (60 seconds)
   - Review responses or hotel package descriptions

4. **Review each one.** Click "Approve" on the ones you like.

5. **Copy and post.** Copy the text and paste it where it needs to go.

## Tips

- The more specific your input, the better the output. "Fat Mama's Tamales" is good. "Fat Mama's Tamales — the tamale plate with green sauce" is better.
- You can edit the generated text before posting — it's a starting point, not a final draft.
- Social posts work best when you pair them with a real photo from the Media Vault.

## The Brain Sidebar

On the right side of the screen, there's a context panel that shows related information from our database. This helps the AI write more accurate content. You don't need to do anything with it — it's automatic.
