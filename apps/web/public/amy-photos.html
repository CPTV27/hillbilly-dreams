How this works

  1. Pick your photos — tap the box below or drag photos onto it (JPG, PNG, HEIC, WEBP, all work)
  2. Tap Process All — every photo gets resized twice, once for CloudBeds (2048 × 1536) and once for VRBO (2400 × 1600)
  3. Download each set — tap the download buttons. Your browser saves the files to Downloads (or the Files app on iPad)
  4. Upload to CloudBeds and VRBO from the Files app on your iPad — use the CloudBeds files for the CloudBeds listing and the VRBO files for the VRBO listing
