The Playout System

One event. Six outputs. Mostly automatic.

When a show happens at the Blues Room in Natchez, here's what the system does with it — without anyone staying up late:

1
Radio — audio enters the scheduled rotation. bigmuddyradio.com →
2
Social — video clips generated, scheduled via Postiz to Instagram / TikTok / Facebook.
3
Magazine — article with photos published. bigmuddymagazine.com →
4
Directory — venue and artist listings updated. deepsouthdirectory.com →
5
Records — live recording released as a single. bigmuddyrecords.com →
6
Archive — high-res assets tagged by AI, searchable in the photo library.

Imagine what happens when the Hudson Valley's creative output hits the same engine. A session at Studio C → Utopia Radio → Bearsville Magazine → the directory → the archive. Same pipeline.

The Production Pipeline

Capture to cloud — how content moves through the system

Capture
Camera, iPhone, field recorderYou shoot and edit in Lightroom as usual. Nothing changes about your creative process.
Tag
Adobe Bridge + Google Vision AIBridge for your keywords. Vision API auto-tags everything — "performer," "landscape," "night scene." Tags travel with the file.
Store
Google Cloud StorageThree sizes, named descriptively, backed up with versioning. We're processing 18,000+ photos through this right now.
Publish
The platform pulls it in automaticallyPhotos appear on the website, in articles, in the directory, on social. Upload once, appears everywhere.
Measure
Engagement trackingPhotos that perform on social get promoted to hero images. The audience tells us what works.
