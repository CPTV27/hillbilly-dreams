Section 1 — Right now

CloudBeds + VRBO photos

You are working on the Big Muddy Inn listings on CloudBeds and VRBO. Each platform wants photos in a specific size, which means you need two separate sets of the same photos — one set for CloudBeds, one set for VRBO.

The tool does this for you

Open the Photo Resize Tool, drag your photos in (or tap to pick them), and hit Process. You will get two folders back, one for CloudBeds and one for VRBO, with every photo already resized to the right spec. Upload them directly from your iPad.

Step by step:

  1. Open the Photo Search page in Safari on your iPad. Search for "inn", then "bedroom", then "bar", then "hallway", then "porch", then "exterior", then "blues room". Each search finds different photos.
  2. Long-press any photo you want to keep and save it to your Files app (in a folder like "Inn photos April").
  3. Pick your best 30. You want at least 20 for CloudBeds and 24 for VRBO.
  4. Open the Photo Resize Tool.
  5. Tap Select Photos or drag them onto the page, and pick all 30 you just saved.
  6. Tap Process All. The tool resizes everything in your browser — nothing uploaded anywhere.
  7. Tap Download CloudBeds Set and Download VRBO Set.
  8. Log into CloudBeds → your property → Media → Upload. Select the CloudBeds photos.
  9. Log into VRBO → your listing → Photos. Select the VRBO photos.
  10. Check each listing from a real phone (not the admin view) to confirm the photos look sharp.

The sizes used by the tool (verified against industry standards — double-check the latest at the platform help sites before a mass upload):

PlatformSizeFormatMax file
CloudBeds2048 × 1536JPG, sRGB5 MB
VRBO2400 × 1600JPG, sRGB10 MB
What to do next
  • Open the Photo Resize Tool in a new tab now
  • Open the Photo Search in another tab
  • Collect 30 Inn photos to your Files app
  • Run them through the resize tool
  • Upload to CloudBeds and VRBO

Section 5

Making a poster

Rule: AI generates the art. Canva handles the typography.

You pick or generate the image, and you use Canva to add text, layout, and brand elements on top.

HDI brand colors to use in Canva:

  • Background: #0a0a08 — near-black
  • Text: #e8e0d4 — warm cream
  • Accent: #c8943e — amber gold

HDI fonts: Georgia (serif) for headings, any clean sans-serif for body. Do not mix more than 2 fonts.

What you will probably make first:

  • Show posters for the Blues Room
  • Inn social posts (rooms, breakfast, the porch, guest moments)
  • Event flyers for weddings and private bookings
  • Radio show promos

Section 7

Security and logins

The one rule: all passwords go in Bitwarden. No exceptions.

If you have a password written down anywhere else — Notes, your phone, sticky notes, your email — move it into Bitwarden tonight. Chase will help the first time.

Turn on two-factor authentication on everything important: your Apple ID, CloudBeds, VRBO, Gmail, Bitwarden, your bank. Use the iPad built-in authenticator or Google Authenticator. Do not use SMS two-factor for anything financial — phones can be SIM-swapped.
