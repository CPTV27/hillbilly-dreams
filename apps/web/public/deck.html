The Risks We Manage

Clear eyes, full hearts.

1. Model Provider Dependence

We rely heavily on Google (Gemini/Vertex). Mitigation: The core business logic and client workflows live in our DB/Node layer, making models hot-swappable if an API changes radically.

2. "Wrapper Crowding"

The B2B wrapper space is loud. Mitigation: Our hyper-regional focus (Deep South) and physical presence (Showroom) create an offline moat against pure-software competitors.

3. Execution vs. Operations

Running a product org and operational businesses simultaneously splits focus. Mitigation: AI agents handle >80% of routine dev/ops tasks, keeping the human footprint radically small.
