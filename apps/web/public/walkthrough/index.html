Chapter 06 · The product locked

MBT — SaaS + Service hybrid, billed in tokens

Chase locked it in one sitting. MBT is a chatbot-first hybrid. Software tokens for AI work. Human tokens for the team work AI can't do. Same chat meters both.

The pieces fell into place: Studio C IS the operating company that runs MBT. Open Notebook is the data layer. The 30+ specialist agents are the routing intelligence. The 6-tier pricing — Free, $20 Standard, $100 Service (the target sell), $200 Operator, $500 Business, Enterprise — covers the whole audience from tire-kicker to enterprise contract.

The $100 tier is the killer. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini are all $20/mo. The MBT $100 tier adds premium models AND access to a human when the chat hits a wall. No competitor offers human-on-call at any consumer price.

Software is cheap. Humans are the premium. We bill them differently because they are. — The MBT pricing tagline
