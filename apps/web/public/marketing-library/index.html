The Four Anchor Lines

Lock these — they propagate everywhere.

"AI tells you. We do the rest."

The campaign tagline. Goes on every ad, every landing page footer, every social bio.

"Software is cheap. Humans are the premium. We bill them differently because they are."

The pricing tagline. Goes on the pricing page, in onboarding, in any tokenomics explainer.

"Same chat. Two meters. Pay for the answer, not the path."

The visual manifesto. Goes on the hero of any page explaining the token model.

"Inverted Intelligence."

The brand thesis. Two-word headline, infinite essays. The radio show. The publication. The reframe of the entire labor market.

What we DO NOT say

These phrases are banned. If a draft contains any of them, it gets rewritten. This list grows monotonically.

nestled vibrant perfect getaway world-class best-in-class leverage (as a verb) moving forward circle back synergy hidden gem your home away from home unlock potential transform revolutionary game-changing next-generation end-to-end solution AI-powered platform* ecosystem*

* Context dependent, but generally avoid.

Voice Rules

  • Lead with the action / verb / scene
  • Real proper nouns over adjectives
  • "Could a tired person understand this in 4 seconds?"
  • "Could the customer act on this in 10 seconds?"
  • Short > clever
  • Truth over polish
  • Honest framing where the product isn't a fit ("if X for you means Y, look elsewhere")
  • The product mark whispers; the customer outcome shouts
