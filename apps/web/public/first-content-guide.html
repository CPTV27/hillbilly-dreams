For Tracy, Amy & Chase

Your First Content

Five things to do in the admin panel. Takes about 30 minutes total. By the end, you'll have created a real event, written a real article, uploaded a real photo, and sent a real newsletter.

1

Log In and Look Around

5 min

Open the admin panel. Click around. Get familiar with the layout.

→
Go to bigmuddytouring.com/admin
→
Sign in with your Google account
→
You'll see the dashboard — stats, recent content, quick links
→
Look for the gold chat bubble in the bottom right — that's Delta Dawn, your AI assistant
→
Try asking Delta Dawn: "What events are coming up?"
If you can't log in, text Chase. He may need to add your Google account to the system.
2

Create a Show

5 min

Schedule a real event at the Inn. Even if it's a test, this is how every show gets into the system.

→
Click Events → New Event
→
Fill in: Name (e.g., "Friday Night Open Mic"), Date, Time
→
Set Artist (e.g., "Arrie Aslin & Rise Up")
→
Set Price and Capacity (e.g., $10, 40 seats)
→
Check "Stream" if it'll be on the radio
→
Save — the show now appears on the Radio live sessions page
Amy: This is your primary tool. Every show at the Inn or any venue gets created here. The system handles the promotion.
3

Write a Short Article

10 min

Write something real for the Magazine. Even 3-4 paragraphs counts. This is how stories get published.

→
Click Articles → New Article
→
Enter a Title — something real. Examples:
Tracy: "What Running a Boutique Inn in Natchez Actually Looks Like"
Amy: "The Bar After the Show: Why 1am is the Best Hour at the Inn"
Chase: "Why We Started a Radio Station in a Hotel"
→
Write the body. Don't overthink it — first draft is fine
→
Set Status to "Draft" (not Published — Chase reviews first)
→
Save
Tracy: The Magazine is where you publish the story of the Inn, the business, the town. Your perspective is the one readers can't get anywhere else.
4

Upload a Photo

5 min

Get a real photo into the system. Take one with your phone right now if you want.

→
Click Media Upload
→
Drag a photo from your computer or phone into the upload area
→
It uploads to Google Cloud Storage automatically
→
The photo is now available for articles, the gallery, and social posts
The best photos of the Inn, the bar, the food, the shows — those are content. Every photo you take is potential Magazine or social material.
5

Draft a Newsletter

5 min

Start a newsletter draft. Don't send it — just see how the tool works.

→
Click Newsletter → New
→
Enter a subject line: "This Week at The Big Muddy Inn"
→
Write 2-3 paragraphs about what's happening this week — shows, food, the town
→
Save as draft — DO NOT hit send yet
Once the WiFi portal starts collecting guest emails, this newsletter becomes your direct line to every person who's stayed at the Inn.
+

Try the Creative Studio

5 min

Generate a radio promo. The AI creates a voiceover from your script.

→
Click Creative Studio
→
Click the Audio tab
→
Pick a voice — Delta Dawn, Catfish Carl, or Chase
→
Write a short script: "You're listening to Big Muddy Radio, live from Natchez, Mississippi."
→
Hit Generate — listen to the result
This is how we create all the radio promos, station IDs, and voiceovers. Seven different AI voices available.

That's it. You just created content in the system. Questions? Ask Delta Dawn or text Chase.
