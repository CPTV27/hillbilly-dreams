Approach 04 · Pure Numbers · By Finance Director

Two kinds of tokens. One bill.

Software answers cost pennies. Human answers cost dollars. Both come through the same chat.

Token rates

Token Unit Customer rate
Software LLM 1,000 tokens (~750 words in/out) $0.02
Software · premium model 1,000 tokens (Opus / GPT-5 class) $0.10
Human SERVICE 1 minute of service-team time $5.00
Human · specialist 1 minute (legal, finance, dev) $10.00

Software rates are pass-through plus margin. Human rates reflect $300/hr fractional ops — what would otherwise cost a $150K/yr hire.

Every escalation logs time. Every line item shows on the bill. The token meter is always on screen.

The ratio

~500×

Human tokens cost about 500× what software tokens cost. They're worth it when they are.

Math: 1 minute of human time at $5.00 vs. 1,000 software tokens at $0.02 — a single human-minute buys what 250,000 software tokens buy. Specialists at $10/min push the ratio to 500×. The chat decides which one you need; you decide if the answer is worth the rate.
