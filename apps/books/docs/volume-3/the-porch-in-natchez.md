# Prologue: The Porch in Natchez

A Tuesday evening on the porch of the Big Muddy Inn. Tracy's checking tomorrow's guests on her phone. Amy's warming up for a house concert in the parlor. The radio show is streaming from the second floor. None of this existed two years ago. All of it runs on one platform. And everything that's about to happen in fourteen other towns starts from this porch.
