## How to Use This List

1. **Start local.** Your CVB, Main Street program, and SBDC are the first calls.
2. **Stack support.** Get SBDC consulting + SCORE mentoring + CDFI financing. They're all free or low-cost and designed to work together.
3. **Show up in person.** These organizations respond better to a face in the office than an email in the inbox.
4. **Ask about upcoming deadlines.** Grant cycles and programs change. The people at these organizations know what's coming before it's posted online.
