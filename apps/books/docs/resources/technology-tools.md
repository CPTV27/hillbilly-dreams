# Technology Tools

![Recommended technology stack comparison](https://storage.googleapis.com/bmt-media-bigmuddy/illustrations/outsider-economics/10-saas-price-ladder.webp)

The recommended stack for Main Street operators. Prioritizes simplicity, cost, and local control.

## The $0-$100/Month Stack

Everything a single Main Street business needs to be digitally present:

| Need | Tool | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Business listing | Deep South Directory | $20/mo | Local, AI-enhanced, includes review alerts |
| Website | Square Online or Carrd | $0-$12/mo | Good enough. Don't overthink it. |
| Payments | Square | 2.6% + $0.10 | Simple. Works with most POS systems. |
| Email | Google Workspace | $6/mo | Professional email at your domain |
| Bookkeeping | Wave | $0 | Free accounting software. Seriously. |
| Social media | Content Studio (via DSD) | Included at $49+ | Or do it yourself with the phone in your pocket |
| Reviews | Google Business Profile | $0 | Claim it, update it, respond to reviews |
| Scheduling | Google Calendar | $0 | Shared calendars for teams and events |

**Total: $26-$38/month** for a fully functional digital presence.

## For Venues and Hospitality

| Need | Tool | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reservations | Resy or OpenTable | Varies | For restaurants with wait times |
| Hotel booking | Direct booking page | Custom | Avoid 15-25% OTA commissions |
| Event tickets | Eventbrite or Square | Per-ticket fee | Simple ticketing for shows |
| Sound/lighting | In-house setup | One-time | Invest in good PA. Rent the rest. |

## For Federations

| Need | Tool | Cost | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Task coordination | Asana (free tier) | $0 | Up to 15 members free |
| Shared documents | Google Drive | $0 | Shared folders for the federation |
| Communication | Group text or Slack | $0 | Keep it simple. Most people prefer text. |
| Shared marketing | Content Studio | Shared cost | One tool, multiple businesses |
| Directory | Deep South Directory | $20/member | The federation's public face |

## Tools to Avoid

| Tool | Why | Use Instead |
|---|---|---|
| Wix/Squarespace (for primary site) | Monthly cost adds up, limited SEO control | Square Online (free) or a custom landing page |
| Hootsuite/Buffer (at full price) | $100+/mo is too much for one business | Content Studio or post directly |
| Yelp for Business | Pay-to-play model, aggressive sales tactics | Google Business Profile + local directory |
| Constant Contact | Expensive for small lists | Mailchimp free tier or Google Workspace |
| QuickBooks Online (at full price) | $30+/mo for basic bookkeeping | Wave (free) until you outgrow it |

## The Non-Negotiables

Regardless of what tools you use, these three things are non-negotiable:

1. **Claim your Google Business Profile.** It's free. It's where tourists look first. If you do nothing else, do this.
2. **Separate business bank account.** Not optional. Mixing personal and business money is a legal and tax mess.
3. **Your own domain name.** $12/year at Google Domains (now Squarespace Domains). Your business name dot com. Not facebook.com/yourbusiness.
