# The $20 Revolution

The right price for a Main Street digital tool is $20/month. Not $99. Not $299. Twenty dollars.

## Why $20?

1. **It's the ChatGPT anchor.** Everyone knows $20/month for AI. Your tool has AI in it. Same price. No negotiation needed.
2. **It's below the pain threshold.** A Main Street business owner doesn't need a meeting, a demo, or a contract to say yes to $20. They say yes on the sidewalk.
3. **It's the price of lunch.** "This costs less than what you spent on lunch today" is an unbeatable pitch.
4. **It compounds.** 100 businesses at $20/month = $2,000/month = a full-time community coordinator's salary.

## The Tier Ladder

$20 is the door, not the ceiling:

| Tier | Price | What They Get |
|---|---|---|
| The Listing | $20/mo | Directory listing, review alerts, monthly report |
| The Works | $49/mo | + Social media posts, content calendar, auto-publishing |
| The Engine | $99/mo | + Review responses, competitor watch, priority placement |

Start everyone at $20. Let the tool prove itself. Upgrades happen naturally when they see the value.

## The Walk-In Pitch

This is a face-to-face sale, not a funnel:

1. Walk into the business
2. Pull up their Google listing on your phone
3. Show them what it looks like now vs. what it could look like
4. "I can do this for twenty bucks a month"
5. Close on the sidewalk with a Stripe link

No deck. No demo account. No follow-up sequence. Just a phone and a handshake.

## What $20 Replaces

Most Main Street businesses are paying $200-800/month across 4-6 different SaaS tools they barely use. The directory at $20 doesn't replace all of them on day one — but it's the first step toward consolidation.

The upgrade path ($49, then $99) progressively replaces:
- Review management ($100-300/month elsewhere)
- Social media scheduling ($30-100/month elsewhere)
- Local SEO ($200-500/month elsewhere)
- Competitor monitoring ($50-200/month elsewhere)

## The Key Insight

You're not selling software. You're selling a relationship with a local operator who answers their phone. The $20 just covers the cost of the tools you bring to the relationship.
