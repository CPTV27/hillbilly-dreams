# Outsider Economics, Volume 1: A Field Manual for Independent Economic Systems

**A Complete Implementation Guide for Community Wealth Creation**

By Chase Tuthill Pierson

---

Every community already has the wealth it needs. The problem isn't lack of resources — it's lack of coordination.

This book proves through mathematical modeling and real implementation that coordinated labor creates exponentially more value than individual effort. Twenty people contributing 20 hours monthly to collective tasks can generate $450,000 in monthly value. Not through investment, debt, or external funding — through systematic coordination of existing time and skills.

The secret isn't complex financial instruments or innovative technology. The secret is treating people as currency and coordination as infrastructure.
