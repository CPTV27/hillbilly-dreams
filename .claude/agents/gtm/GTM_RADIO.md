# GTM Plan — Big Muddy Radio

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **18 shows fully programmed** — Each show has a description, host voice, playlist, and schedule
2. **DJ drops and bumpers** — TTS-generated intros for every show using ElevenLabs + Google TTS
3. **Live stream reliability** — Icecast stream running 24/7 with auto-rotation
4. **Web player polish** — bigmuddyradio.com player works on mobile, shows now-playing
5. **Social clips** — 15-second audio clips from shows posted to social weekly

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** Web player (primary), show pages, schedule
- **Streaming:** Icecast live stream, potential TuneIn / Radio.com listing
- **Social:** Audio clips on Instagram (waveform video), show announcements
- **Magazine:** "On the Radio" column — what's playing this week
- **Directory:** Local businesses get radio mentions in $20 MBT tier

## Partnerships & Distribution
- TuneIn Radio listing (free, extends reach)
- Venue partnerships (play Big Muddy Radio in-house)
- Local business sponsorships (radio mentions for MBT subscribers)
- Plex integration (Mac mini serves radio archive)

## Revenue Model
- Radio mentions are part of MBT $20 and $99 tiers
- Sponsor spots (30-second, generated via Content Studio)
- Drives entertainment attendance ("heard about this show on the radio")
- Feeds Records (radio play for label artists)

## Success Metrics
- **30 days:** 6 shows programmed, stream running 24/7, DJ drops for all shows
- **60 days:** 12 shows live, TuneIn listed, web player on mobile
- **90 days:** All 18 shows programmed, 100 concurrent listener milestone, first sponsor
