# GTM Plan — BuyCurious Art

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **Artist onboarding** — Kate Skwire, corridor photographers, local painters
2. **Gallery pages** — Artist profiles, collections, individual piece pages
3. **E-commerce basics** — Buy button → Stripe checkout → shipping confirmation
4. **Print-on-demand setup** — Photography prints without inventory risk
5. **First exhibition** — Physical show at the Big Muddy Inn, documented online

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** buycurious.art — gallery, shop, artist profiles
- **Magazine:** Artist features cross-published
- **Social:** Instagram (visual-forward), Pinterest (collections)
- **Entertainment:** Physical exhibitions at the Inn / Blues Room
- **Newsletter:** Quarterly "From the Gallery" — new pieces, artist news

## Revenue Model
- Commission on sales (BuyCurious keeps 30%, artist gets 70%)
- Print-on-demand (higher margin, no inventory)
- Physical exhibition sales
- Feeds Magazine (artist features), Entertainment (exhibition events)

## Success Metrics
- **30 days:** 3 artists onboarded, 30 pieces listed, Instagram active
- **60 days:** First online sale, first physical exhibition
- **90 days:** 10 sales, 5 artists, print-on-demand operational
