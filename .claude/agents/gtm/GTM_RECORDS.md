# GTM Plan — Big Muddy Records

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **Mechanical Bull album launch** — "Artificially Inseminated" as the proof campaign
2. **Artist onboarding flow** — Application → review → terms → listing
3. **Royalty dashboard** — Artists can see their splits and earnings (MelodyVault)
4. **First sync placement** — Get one track placed in a film/TV/ad project
5. **Records page polish** — Album pages, artist profiles, streaming links

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** Album pages, artist profiles, philosophy page ("Artists Own Their Masters")
- **Radio:** Priority airplay for label artists on Big Muddy Radio
- **Magazine:** Artist features and album reviews cross-published
- **Social:** Behind-the-scenes studio content, release day posts
- **Entertainment:** Release shows at the Blues Room

## Revenue Model
- 20% of streaming/sync revenue (artists keep 80%)
- Sync licensing placements (film, TV, ads, games)
- Vinyl/merch sales through BuyCurious
- Release show door revenue (feeds Entertainment)

## Success Metrics
- **30 days:** Mechanical Bull album live, 3 tracks on Big Muddy Radio rotation
- **60 days:** 2 additional artists onboarded, royalty dashboard functional
- **90 days:** First sync placement, 1,000 streams milestone
