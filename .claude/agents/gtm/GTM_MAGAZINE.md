# GTM Plan — Big Muddy Magazine

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **Publish 12 articles** — 3 per city (Memphis, Clarksdale, Vicksburg, Natchez). City guides, features, interviews.
2. **Photo essay series** — "The Corridor" — editorial photography from each stop
3. **Artist interview series** — 4 long-form interviews with corridor musicians
4. **Social excerpts** — Pull quotes and photos from articles for Instagram/Facebook
5. **Magazine identity polish** — Hero images, section headers, reading experience on mobile

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** Primary home for all editorial content
- **Social:** Instagram (photo-forward), Facebook (article links), X (quotes)
- **Radio:** Articles inspire radio show segments ("Read about this artist, now hear them")
- **Newsletter:** Weekly "Editor's Pick" — one article highlight with exclusive context
