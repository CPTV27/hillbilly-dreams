# GTM Plan — Big Muddy Touring

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **Lock the route page** — 18 cities, each with lodging, food, music. Currently live but thin.
2. **City guide content** — 5 deep city guides (Memphis, Clarksdale, Vicksburg, Natchez, New Orleans) as Magazine/Touring crossover
3. **Lodging pipeline** — 10 additional properties onboarded with photos and descriptions
4. **Snowbird Circuit launch** — Announce the touring route with dates and venues
5. **Social presence** — Weekly "road dispatch" posts across Instagram and Facebook

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** Primary. Route pages, city guides, lodging listings
- **Magazine:** City guides cross-publish to bigmuddymagazine.com
- **Radio:** "Road Music" playlist tied to each corridor city
- **Social:** Instagram (photography-heavy), Facebook (event announcements)
- **Newsletter:** Monthly "Corridor Dispatch" — what's happening in each city

## Partnerships & Distribution
- CVBs in each corridor city (Natchez, Clarksdale, Vicksburg, Memphis, New Orleans)
- Airbnb / VRBO cross-listing for Big Muddy Inn
- Music venue partnerships (Ground Zero, Cat Head, etc.)

## Revenue Model
- Inn bookings (direct + OTA)
- Touring packages (lodging + shows + meals)
- CVB sponsorships for corridor promotion
- Feeds MBT ($20 tier businesses get Touring visibility)

## Success Metrics
- **30 days:** 5 city guides live, Inn booking page operational
- **60 days:** Snowbird Circuit dates announced, 10 lodging partners onboarded
- **90 days:** First corridor package sold, newsletter list at 500
