# GTM Plan — Big Muddy Entertainment

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **Rise Up band schedule** — Monthly shows at the Blues Room, quarterly corridor tours
2. **Talent search launch** — Open call in 5 corridor cities
3. **Show calendar** — Every upcoming show listed with tickets/RSVP
4. **Artist-in-Residence program** — Arri Aslin's residency documented and promoted
5. **Entertainment page overhaul** — Warm, inviting, talent-first redesign

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** Show calendar, artist profiles, talent search applications
- **Radio:** Show previews, artist interviews, post-show recaps
- **Magazine:** Artist features, show reviews, talent search coverage
- **Social:** Live show clips, artist spotlights, talent search announcements
- **Newsletter:** "This Week at the Blues Room" — weekly what's-on email

## Revenue Model
- Door sales ($15-$35 per show)
- Private event bookings (Blues Room rental)
- Sponsorships (show sponsors, series sponsors)
- Feeds Radio (live recordings), Records (talent pipeline), Touring (event draw)
