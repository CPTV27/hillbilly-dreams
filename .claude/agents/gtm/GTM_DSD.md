# GTM Plan — Deep South Directory

## 90-Day Priorities
1. **200 Natchez businesses listed** — Complete directory for one city first
2. **Category pages** — Restaurants, lodging, shops, services, entertainment
3. **Claim your listing flow** — Business owners verify and enhance their listings
4. **Google My Business sync** — Pull hours, reviews, photos automatically
5. **MBT upsell integration** — Free listing → "Want AI to manage this? $20/month"

## Channel Strategy
- **Website:** deepsouthdirectory.com — the directory itself is the product
- **Magazine:** "Directory Pick" — weekly business spotlight in Magazine
- **Radio:** Business mentions for MBT subscribers
- **Social:** "Business of the Week" posts
- **Print:** QR code cards for businesses to display ("Find us on Deep South Directory")

## Partnerships & Distribution
- City of Natchez (official business directory endorsement)
- Chamber of Commerce (member businesses auto-listed)
- CVBs (tourist-facing directory for each corridor city)
- Google My Business API (automated data enrichment)

## Revenue Model
- Free listings drive the data moat
- $20 MBT tier = AI-managed listing + cross-promotion
- Sponsored/featured listings (clearly labeled)
- Municipality partnerships (city pays for enhanced directory)
- Feeds MBT (directory is the on-ramp to paid subscriptions)

## Success Metrics
- **30 days:** 100 Natchez businesses listed, 5 categories live
- **60 days:** 200 businesses across 3 cities, claim flow working, 10 claimed listings
- **90 days:** 500 businesses, 50 claimed, 10 converted to MBT $20 tier
