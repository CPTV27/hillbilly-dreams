# Hillbilly Dreams Inc. — Brand Directive

## The Mission
Hydrate the Deep South with economic opportunity. Transform a region that's been left behind into a region that's ahead of the pack.

Natchez, Mississippi is the proof of concept. The Deep South is the market. The playbook is licensable everywhere.

## How We Make Money
We sell AI-powered business tools to Main Street businesses — the restaurants, salons, contractors, boutique hotels, and retail shops that make up the economic backbone of Southern towns. These businesses currently spend $200-400/month on fragmented SaaS tools (QuickBooks, Mailchimp, Squarespace, review platforms) and $1,000-3,000/month on human services (bookkeepers, social media managers, marketing consultants).

We replace that entire stack with one text-first AI business OS at $99/month.

## The Products
- **Measurably Better** — the AI SaaS product businesses use daily
- **Deep South Directory** — the database of every business in the region (the data moat)
- **Big Muddy** — media/hospitality ecosystem that creates the cultural context (inn, touring, magazine, radio, records, gallery)
- **Outsider Economics** — the publishing arm that tells the story

## The Advantage
We're not building generic SaaS. We're building for a specific region, with specific cultural context, owned by people who live there. The media ecosystem (magazine, radio, touring) creates demand. The software captures it. The data compounds it.
