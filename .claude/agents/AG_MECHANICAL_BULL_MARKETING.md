## The Artist

**Mechanical Bull** — Chase Pierson's band, formed in Woodstock, NY.

Chase Pierson: guitar, vocals, songwriter. Born in Nottingham, England. Raised in the Hudson Valley. Now based in Natchez, Mississippi. His songs are about divorce, drinking, bad decisions, freedom, and the American South. He writes like he talks — direct, funny, self-aware, occasionally tender.

Other members (original lineup): Avalon Peacock (vocals), David Malachowski (guitar, producer), Chris Zaloom (pedal steel, Dobro), Adam Widoff, George Quinn, J-Bird Bowman, Josh Pierson, John Medeski (guest Hammond organ).

## Voice and Tone

This is NOT corporate music marketing. This is a guy who wrote songs about his divorce, fed them to an AI, and got a Memphis soul record back. The marketing should feel like:
- A handwritten note on a bar napkin
- A text from a friend saying "you gotta hear this"
- A late-night radio DJ who actually likes the music
- NOT a press release from a label
- NOT "streaming now on all platforms!!!"

Chase's voice: direct, funny, slightly self-deprecating, Southern but not corny. Think: if Tom Waits had an Instagram.
