# Promo Video Campaign — Agent Prompt

## Mission

Write a full library of video promo scripts for the Big Muddy / Hillbilly Dreams ecosystem. Each script is designed to be produced through Google Veo (video generation) and Cloud TTS (voiceover). The output is a complete advertising campaign — different lengths, different angles, different audiences.

## Before You Start

Read these files in order:
1. `.claude/agents/MASTER_HANDOFF.md` — system overview
2. `.claude/agents/ORIGIN_STORY.md` — the narrative
3. `.claude/agents/BRAND_NARRATIVE_OVERHAUL.md` — value props, voice
4. `memory/feedback_chase_voice.md` — Chase's writing voice
5. `docs/sovereign-box-spec.md` — The Sovereign Box product

## The Brand Voice

Direct. No jargon. No startup buzzwords. Speak like a human who built something real. Mississippi warmth, New York sharpness. The tone is: confident, earned, slightly amused that nobody else thought of this.

Never say: "leverage," "synergy," "disrupt," "game-changing," "revolutionary."
Always say it like you're explaining it to a smart person at a bar.

## The Products Being Marketed

1. **Big Muddy Platform** — 15 domains, one codebase. Hotel, radio, magazine, directory, entertainment, gallery, photography — all from Natchez, MS.
2. **Measurably Better Things (MBT)** — The SaaS product for Main Street businesses. $20/$99 tiers.
3. **Deep South Directory** — The local business directory that powers the ecosystem.
4. **The Sovereign Box** — $250 Raspberry Pi appliance. Off-grid. Solar. AI in your pocket. No internet required.
5. **Outsider Economics** — The philosophy. Small-town infrastructure at Fortune 500 scale.
6. **Big Muddy Radio** — 18 AI-hosted shows, community broadcasting.
7. **Bearsville Media Group** — The Northeast expansion (Woodstock, NY).
8. **Studio C / Tuthill Design** — Production arm for real estate and media clients.

## What You're Producing

For EACH concept below, write:
- **Visual direction** — What the viewer SEES (describe shots for Veo to generate)
- **Voiceover script** — What the viewer HEARS (for Cloud TTS or human VO)
- **Text overlays** — Key phrases that appear on screen (Canva handles typography, not AI)
- **Music direction** — Mood/genre for background (for Lyria generation)
- **CTA** — What the viewer does next

## Format Requirements

### :15 Spots (Social Ads — make 10 of these)
- Instagram/TikTok/YouTube pre-roll
- One idea per spot. Hit hard, get out.
- Must work without sound (text overlays carry the message)

### :30 Spots (TV/Streaming Ads — make 6 of these)
- Traditional ad format
- Setup → Tension → Resolution → CTA
- Voiceover driven with supporting visuals

### :60 Spots (YouTube/Conference — make 4 of these)
- Room to breathe. Tell a small story.
- Character-driven (a barber, a chef, an innkeeper, a musician)
- End with the product, not the pitch

### 3:00 Mini-Docs (Website/Presentation — make 3 of these)
- Deep dive into one angle
- Interview-style pacing (even if generated)
- Facts, numbers, proof points woven into narrative

### 10:00 Full Feature (Keynote/Conference — make 1 of this)
- The whole story. DeFacto Codec Market → Big Muddy → Sovereign Box
- Chase's journey as the spine
- Product demo woven throughout
- Ends with the vision: "Now deploying to your town"

## Output Format

For each script, deliver:
1. **Title** and **length**
2. **Veo prompt** — the visual generation prompt (detailed shot descriptions)
3. **TTS script** — the voiceover text (with pacing notes)
4. **Lyria prompt** — the music generation prompt
5. **Text overlays** — exact phrases for Canva
6. **CTA** — URL or action

## QC Rules

- No hardcoded fonts or colors in text overlays (Canva handles typography)
- Illustrations must be diverse — mix of races, ages, genders
- No high-tech imagery — Main Street, not Silicon Valley
- No wide AI maps — zoom tight
- AI generates art, Canva handles typography
- Every person shown should look like they live in the town, not like a stock photo model
