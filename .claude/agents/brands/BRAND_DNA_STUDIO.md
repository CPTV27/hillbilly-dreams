# Brand DNA — Studio C / Tuthill Design

## Identity
- **Entity:** Tuthill Design LLC (d/b/a Studio C Video)
- **Domains:** studiocvideo.com, studio-c.video, studio-c.com, tuthilldesign.com
- **Theme class:** `.theme-studio` / `.theme-tuthill`
- **Tagline:** "Production. Recording. Broadcasting."

## Voice Attributes
1. **Professional competence** — Studio C is the production arm. It does the work. Copy reads like a capabilities sheet, not a brochure. "4K video. Multi-track audio. Live streaming. Post-production. One crew."
2. **Understated** — The work speaks for itself. No hype needed. "We've produced 200 hours of broadcast content in the last year. Here's the reel."
3. **Client-facing** — This brand talks to people who want to hire a production company. Different audience than consumers. "Tell us what you need. We'll tell you what it costs and when it's done."
4. **Technical when appropriate** — This audience understands specs. "RED Komodo. Blackmagic ATEM. Shure SM7B. OBS + Icecast for live streaming."
5. **Dual identity** — Studio C is video/audio production. Tuthill is design/photography. Same company, slightly different registers. Tuthill is warmer, Studio C is more technical.

## Anti-Patterns
- NEVER sounds like a creative agency ("we craft stories that move hearts")
- NEVER sounds cheap ("affordable," "budget-friendly")
- NEVER oversells ("world-class," "award-winning" unless literally true)
- NEVER sounds like the consumer-facing Big Muddy brands

## Tone Spectrum
- **Formal:** Proposals, SOWs, client onboarding, rate sheets
- **Casual:** Portfolio descriptions, behind-the-scenes, social posts showing process
- **Urgent:** Project deadlines, availability announcements

## Vocabulary
- **Use:** produce, shoot, edit, record, stream, deliver, specs, timeline, crew, reel, project, client
- **Avoid:** content (use "production"), magic, storytelling (use "production" or "piece"), passion

## Reference Voices
"This brand sounds like **a good freelance DP's portfolio site** — clean, professional, shows the work, lists the gear, makes it easy to hire them."

## Differentiation
Studio C is the only B2B brand. Every other brand talks to consumers or community. Studio C talks to clients who need production work done. It's the most businesslike voice in the ecosystem.

## Example Paragraphs

**Services overview (Studio C):**
Studio C handles video production, audio recording, and live broadcasting from Natchez, Mississippi. We produce content for Big Muddy's ecosystem — radio shows, magazine video features, live event streams — and take outside clients. Four-person crew. Full post-production in-house. Turnaround is typically two weeks for a finished piece.

**Portfolio item (Studio C):**
Rise Up Gospel and Blues Band — Live at the Blues Room. Three-camera shoot. Mixed live audio from the board plus a room mic for ambience. Edited to 45 minutes from a 90-minute set. Delivered for radio broadcast and YouTube. Shot on Blackmagic Pocket 6K.

**About (Tuthill Design):**
Tuthill Design is a design and photography studio based in the Hudson Valley, New York. We work with small businesses, artists, and cultural organizations. Brand identity, print design, editorial photography, and web design. Clean work. Fair pricing. We answer emails the same day.

## Visual Identity
- **Studio C Colors:** Dark, professional. Near-black backgrounds, subtle accent colors. Technical feel.
- **Tuthill Colors:** Warmer, cleaner. White backgrounds, natural tones.
- **Fonts:** Clean sans-serif throughout. No decorative fonts.
- **Photography:** Behind-the-scenes production shots, gear, studio spaces. For Tuthill: portfolio work, design process, photography samples.
