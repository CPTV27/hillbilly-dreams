# Brand DNA — Big Muddy Entertainment

## Identity
- **Entity:** Hillbilly Dreams Inc
- **Domains:** bigmuddyentertainment.com
- **Theme class:** `.theme-entertainment`
- **Tagline:** "The Talent Has Always Been Here"

## Voice Attributes
1. **Community-first** — Entertainment exists to give artists a stage and audiences a reason to come. "We didn't bring talent to Natchez. We built a stage for the talent that was already here."
2. **Warm invitation** — The most welcoming brand in the ecosystem. Open door energy. "Come to the show. Bring someone who needs a good night."
3. **Talent-protective** — Like Records but softer. Artists are people first, performers second. "She drove three hours from El Dorado. She's playing for forty minutes. Make it count for her."
4. **Event-driven** — Copy often points to something specific happening soon. Dates, times, places. "Saturday. 8 PM. The Blues Room. $25 at the door."
5. **Rise Up energy** — The talent search, the gospel band, the corridor tours. This brand believes in people. "We're not looking for the next big thing. We're looking for the person who's been singing in church for twenty years and never had a microphone."

## Anti-Patterns
- NEVER sounds like a corporate entertainment company ("presenting," "in association with")
- NEVER sounds like a talent show ("compete for your chance," "who will be the next")
- NEVER reduces artists to content ("our talent roster," "entertainment assets")
- NEVER sounds exclusive ("VIP," "elite," "invite-only")

## Tone Spectrum
- **Formal:** Partnership proposals, venue booking info, artist applications
- **Casual:** Show announcements, social posts, artist spotlights
- **Urgent:** Tonight's show, last tickets, weather changes, schedule updates

## Vocabulary
- **Use:** show, stage, tonight, artist, band, audience, room, door, set, talent, search, rise, play, sing, community
- **Avoid:** content, brand, platform, roster, leverage, networking, industry

## Reference Voices
"This brand sounds like **the person who runs the best small venue in your town** — knows every artist by name, genuinely excited about tonight's show, wants you to be there."

## Differentiation
Entertainment is the heart. Where Records is principled and Radio is fun, Entertainment is warm. It's the brand that hugs you at the door.

## Example Paragraphs

**Show announcement:**
Rise Up Gospel and Blues Band is back at the Blues Room this Saturday. Arri Aslin is headlining with a full band — guitar, bass, keys, drums, and three voices that hit you in the chest. Doors at 7. Music at 8. $25. Bring someone who needs to hear something real.

**Talent search:**
We run talent searches in every city on the corridor. Not auditions — conversations. We're looking for musicians who could step into the Rise Up band, artists who could anchor their own touring act, voices that the mainstream never found. If you know someone, tell them to come. If you are someone, come.

**About page:**
Big Muddy Entertainment exists because the talent was always here. In every small town along the Mississippi, there are people who can sing, play, write, and perform at a level that would fill any room in Nashville or New York. What they didn't have was a stage, a sound system, and someone to open the doors. That's what we do.

## Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Warm tones, stage lighting amber, deep reds, dark backgrounds
- **Fonts:** Serif display, DM Sans body
- **Photography:** Live performance, audience faces, backstage moments, community gatherings. Warm. Human. Never stock.
