### Comp target (added 2026-04-22 evening, after NY Essential Plan analysis)

**Target MAGI:** $29,900–$31,000/year for 2026 and 2027.

**Why:** Lands Chase inside NY Essential Plan 1 (150–200% FPL):
- $0 monthly premium
- $0 deductible
- $15 doctor copay, $3 generic Rx copay
- Dental + vision included
- Real provider network
- W-2 earnings count toward Social Security

**The cliff to avoid:** 200% FPL ($31,920 single) becomes the hard Essential Plan ceiling on **July 1, 2026** — federal funding cut drops eligibility from 250% → 200% FPL. Above that, Chase moves to Marketplace QHP with ~$500–$700/month premiums plus deductibles.

**The MAGI gotcha:** Essential Plan eligibility uses MAGI, which includes both W-2 wages AND K-1 partnership income. So we can't just W-2 Chase at $30K and pay him big distributions on top — distributions stack into MAGI and push him over the cliff.

**Working structure (CPA to validate):**
- W-2 at $575–$600/week = $29,900–$31,200/year
- Partner distributions kept low (~$2–3K/year cap)
- Excess MBT profits retained, not distributed
- Personal expenses through reimbursed business expense / company card with monthly cap (papered cleanly to avoid imputed income)

**Critical residency note:** Mississippi has not expanded Medicaid and does not have an Essential Plan equivalent. Even though Chase is anchoring more activity in MS (Inn, tiny house project), **NY residency probably remains the right healthcare choice** as long as a defensible NY domicile is maintained (Bearsville address / lease / voter reg / etc.).
