# The North Star: Brand & Visual Manifesto

*Established 2026-03-31. Authored by Chase Pierson + Vesper (Gemini Creative Director).*
*This document overrides all previous visual direction.*

---

## 1. The Core Identity

- **The Holding Company:** Hillbilly Dreams Inc. (The Foundation).
- **The Product:** The Monorepo (The Intelligence).
- **The Narrative:** Measurably Better Things (MBT). A high-performance technology platform that tells the story of the stack. It is the "Glass Engine" — precise, transparent, and powerful.
- **The Manifestation:** Big Muddy. The flagship cultural ecosystem. This is where the tech meets the dirt. It must feel like a premium, legacy publication (think *Monocle* or *National Geographic*) powered by a futuristic backend.

## 2. The Visual DNA: "Phillips-66 & Polaroid"

We are moving away from "AI-shiny." We want **Tactile Grit + Surgical Precision.**

- **Photography First:** The design exists to serve the image. Use full-bleed backgrounds, massive hero sections, and high-contrast photo-journalistic layouts.
- **The Palette:**
  - **Darkness:** `#0a0a0a` (Deep Obsidian)
  - **The Soul:** `#c8943e` (Raw brass / harvest gold)
  - **The Type:** `#f5f0eb` (Paper / Bone)
- **Typography:** Monospace for technical data (MBT layers); Elegant, high-contrast Serifs for storytelling (Big Muddy).

## 3. The "Real-World" Directive

- **No "AI-Fluff":** If the design looks like a generic SaaS landing page, it's a fail.
- **Scale & Space:** Use extreme white space (or "black space") to make Chase's photography breathe.
- **The "Glass" Effect:** Technical overlays (charts, maps, UI) should feel like they are projected onto the photography, not covering it up.

## 4. The Cinematic Scroll

When implementing hero sections and photo-heavy pages:

1. **The Impact:** User hits the page — 100vh full-screen photo. No text, maybe just a tiny gold 1px coordinate in the corner.
2. **The Reveal:** As they scroll, the MBT Technical Data (the "Glass Engine") slides over the photo like a HUD in a cockpit.
3. **The Result:** It tells the viewer: "This beautiful moment is brought to you by a very powerful machine."

## 5. Agent Workflow & Handoff

- **Vesper (Gemini):** Creative Director & Visual Architect. Responsible for the "vibe," schemas, and high-level UI design.
- **Claude Code:** Engineer & Implementation Lead. Translates Vesper's designs into clean, modular code within the monorepo.

## 6. The Test

Every line of code and every design choice must answer the question:

> *"Does this make the photography look more expensive and the technology look more invisible?"*

If the answer is no, start over.
