## Mission

Close towns, brokers, banks, and institutional buyers on MBT. Move them from "interested conversation" to "signed contract with a deposit" without ever overpromising a feature that hasn't shipped. Turn each closed pilot into the case study that opens the next three doors.

**Explicitly NOT in scope:**
- Walk-in DSD $25/mo sales to small businesses (separate department)
- Fulfillment after signing (Studio C + Tuthill Design handle delivery)
- Product decisions (Product MBT owns those)
- Contract language (Legal & Entity Structure, once that department exists)

## What This Department Does Day To Day

1. **Maintains the pipeline** — one CRM, one source of truth, one weekly pipeline review. **Recommendation: Attio** — cheapest decent B2B CRM, modern UI, works on iPad, integrates with Gmail and Google Calendar natively.
2. **Runs the discovery → pilot → close sequence** for every prospect. Standard stages: Identified → Contacted → Discovery → Proof-of-concept shoot → Pilot proposal → Contract → Deposit → Kickoff.
3. **Partners with Fulfillment** (Tuthill for Hudson Valley, Studio C for Deep South) so no deal is signed that can't be delivered.
4. **Feeds proof points back into Marketing** — every closed pilot becomes a case study.
5. **Enforces the honesty gate** on every pitch artifact. If Product MBT hasn't shipped it, this department does not sell it.
6. **Reports weekly** to Chief of Staff on pipeline health, blockers, and asks.

## What This Department Does NOT Do

- Does not write contracts (waits for Legal)
- Does not bind insurance or COI requests (waits for Insurance & Risk)
- Does not fulfill customer work (Studio C + Tuthill Design)
- Does not set pricing unilaterally (Chase's call)
- Does not close DSD walk-in $25/mo deals (separate department)
