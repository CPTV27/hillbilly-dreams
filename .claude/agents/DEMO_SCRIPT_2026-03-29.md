# Demo Script — The Big Muddy Platform
## March 29, 2026

### Opening (2 min)
"This is one codebase serving 15 domains. Everything you're about to see runs from the same Next.js app."

Start at **bigmuddytouring.com** — the hub. Show the dark, warm aesthetic. Click through to the Inn, show a suite page, click through to Cloudbeds booking. "That's a live reservation system."

### The Corridor (3 min)
Navigate to **/route** — Memphis to New Orleans. "This is the corridor. Every town along the river has businesses, artists, and music. We connect them."

Open **bigmuddymagazine.com** — "This is how we tell their stories."
Open **bigmuddyradio.com** — "This is how we reach them. 18 shows programmed, DJ characters, automated scheduling."
Open **bigmuddyentertainment.com** — "Records, radio, touring, talent search — all under one umbrella."

### The Product (5 min)
Open **deepsouthdirectory.com** — "This is what we sell. $20/month gets a Main Street business an AI-powered listing, review alerts, and a monthly report. $49 adds social media. $99 adds everything."

Show the three tiers. Click "Get Started" on The Listing. Show the onboarding form.

Open **measurablybetter.life** — "This is the pitch page. $99 replaces $500-800 in tools. But the real value isn't software — it's membership in a media ecosystem. Your business gets mentioned on the radio, featured in the magazine, listed in the directory."

### The Admin (5 min)
Sign into **bigmuddytouring.com/admin/dashboard** — "This is Mission Control."

Show the greeting, the spotlight card, the quick stats, the tools grid. "Every card ends with a verb. It tells you what to do, not just what happened."

Click into **Content Studio** (/admin/studio) — "Type a business name, hit generate, get social posts, a magazine feature, a radio spot, and review responses. Four channels, one input."

Click into **Media Vault** (/admin/media) — "Drop a photo in a folder, it's indexed in 2 minutes. Every photo in our system, searchable."

Click into **Lookbook** (/admin/lookbook) — "36 illustrations across 12 styles. All generated, all on-brand. Vote and comment to pick favorites."

### The Art (3 min)
Open **bigmuddytouring.com/gallery/chase-pierson** — "This is a photography storefront. Eight works with real pricing. Click Buy Print — it goes to Stripe checkout with a 75/25 artist/platform split."

Show a proof gallery link — "When I shoot an event, subjects get a private link. They browse their photos and order prints. No login needed."

### The Philosophy (2 min)
Open **outsidereconomics.com** — "This is the framework behind everything. Outsider Economics. Six core concepts, a practical toolkit, case studies from the corridor, and Deep South-specific resources — grants, organizations, legal frameworks."

Click into the Field Manual. "Every chapter has the math. No hand-waving."

### The Team (1 min)
"Tracy, Amy, and JP each have their own Asana board with 4 tasks. There's a team manual with role-specific guides — bar inventory checklists, compliance calendars, show booking templates. Plain English, step by step."

### The Infrastructure (1 min)
"One Mac mini runs the radio station, Plex for in-room entertainment, social media publishing, and AI notebooks. One MacBook Pro runs the code. One repo, one database, 15 domains, 56 custom illustrations, and a photography storefront with Stripe payments."

### Close
"We're selling the $20 listing starting Tuesday. Five walk-ins a day. The claim ladder adds features weekly through April 21. By then, $99 replaces $500-800 in tools and nobody else in Mississippi has a media ecosystem backing their directory."
