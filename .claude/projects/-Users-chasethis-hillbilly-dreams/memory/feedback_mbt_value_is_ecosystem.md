MBT is the engine — the Glass Engine platform underneath everything. It is NOT a consumer product itself.

**Products built on MBT:**
- **Deep South Directory** (deepsouthdirectory.com) — MBT for businesses. Directory + media ecosystem. $20/$49/$99/mo.
- **Measurably Better Life** (measurablybetter.life) — MBT for individuals. Personal technology that consolidates digital clutter. Competes with ChatGPT (~$20/mo). Free tier available. NOT a directory — it's personal tech.
- **Hudson Valley Directory** (future) — MBT for businesses in the Northeast. Bearsville territory.

**Why this matters:** DSD and MBL are siblings, not the same product. Same engine, different audiences. DSD = businesses. MBL = people. Both integrate with each other and feed the same regional database.

**The flywheel:** By covering a region with entertainment (Magazine, Radio, Touring) and services (Directory, personal tech), HDI becomes a regional data utility. The data we collect by providing value and entertainment is itself very valuable.

**How to apply:**
- measurablybetter.life is NOT on ice — it's a real consumer product, just in dogfood phase
- Don't conflate DSD and MBL — they're different products for different audiences
- MBT branding goes on the platform/engine layer, not on either consumer product
- Consumer pages for MBL: personal, accessible, "replace your digital clutter" messaging
- Consumer pages for DSD: neighborly, Main Street, business-owner focused
- Both benefit from the shared database and media ecosystem
